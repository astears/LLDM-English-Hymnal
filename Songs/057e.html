057 BLESSING AND COMMUNION

Blessing and communion, is the loveliest divine song,

All of those who've been washed, through God's Lamb dwelling on Mount Zion.

CHORUS

My cups overflowing contemplating all my Brothers,

I'm satisfied seeing, all of those washed in the Lord's blood;

Oh how joyful, oh it is my contentment,

This is what I feel in the Lord's church.

2

Prayer, communion is joy for the just ones in Christ Jesus,

It's the hymn sung by saints, to all those gaining redemption from God.

3

Salvation, communion, in the church of the live God,

He's dispersed His Spirit;

With His sweet love to the humble souls.

4

In the end there's communion, eternal life, redemption,

We'll be dwelling in mansions;

We will sing praises and honor Christ.
