048 TO ALL THE PEOPLE

To all the people and races God is calling,

To be repented from all their evil;

And that they serve Him with all of their being,

So that one day they have liberty.

CHORUS

To Jesus Christ the King of kings,

Let there be honor always amen;

Because He has come to save His people,

His Holy Spirit He has given them.

2

For in these times we see people are toiling,

Only for things that are of this world;

They are despising the things of our Jesus,

They are rejecting what's spiritual.

3

Fortunately, blessed are all the people,

Those who are found doing what is good;

Their prize will never be taken for granted,

For those believing, it's their reward.

4

It is a battle we Christians are fighting,

And face to face with evil they endure;

Only through Jesus they will gain the victory,

He is the one who makes them prevail.

5

Sometimes the waves hit so strongly and harshly,

Even the large ship seems to overturn;

Although the small ship confiding in Jesus,

No wave will ever bring harm to them.
