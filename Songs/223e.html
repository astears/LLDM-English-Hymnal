223 PRAISE THE LORD

Praise the Lord in highest heavens,

That showed human kind His love;

Giving to us human creatures,

A Savior so powerful.

With the hymns of holy people,

Sung with ours to make a choir;

Of God's praise and gratitude,

For our health in this new day;

And we say in one loud voice,

In the heavens, praise the Lord!

2

Let the earth sing of His glory,

And be joyful of His love;

His peace shines on us forever,

'Cause He is our God of love.

Every tribe and every nation,

Rise up poems towards the heavens;

To the King Emmanuel,

Who has come from Israel;

And they burst in one loud voice,

In the heavens, praise the Lord!

3

Praise the Lord, the church is singing,

'Cause from sin the Lord freed them;

Through Christ Jesus who's their Master,

Fullness of their heart and souls.

Living vigilant and careful,

And aware when wars approaching;

Until there is peace and rest,

In His glory and in peace;

Where the righteous sing as one,

In the heavens, praise the Lord.
