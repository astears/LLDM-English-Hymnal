597 AS A PRISONER

As a prisoner sunk in an abyss,

And they said I would never leave that place;

When Lord Jesus heard of all my groaning,

To them He said: This lost soul is mine.

CHORUS

Jesus Christ in His love freed me,

Glory to Him//Amen//

2

In the prison the devil said to me,

That he wanted to kill all mankind;

When the Lord Jesus heard all my groaning,

And Christ told me:

I come to save your soul.

3

Oh how glorious that life is with Jesus,

Giving praises and singing to God;

And be saying by grace I have been saved,

I 'm singing with great jubilation.

4

This church is of the Lord Jesus Christ,

Jesus Christ said that He would return;

Here in this church are all of those convicts,

Christ freed them, may they proclaim joyfully.
