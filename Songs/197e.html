197 IN THE MOUNT THAT GOD

In the mount made by God, being the Creator,

A place full of curse and of shame, a man nailed to a cross;

In very great pain,

He spilled out His heart,

To save from condemnation, all humanity.

CHORUS

Who from your love will take me away?

Anguish or the bitter pain;

Danger or my nakedness,

Hunger or a scorn.

Who is the one who will condemn?

Lord Christ endured the cross;

Eternal life does wait for me,

The pain of Jesus Christ was great!

2

Eternal loneliness was what he suffered,

Despises, curses, mockeries, were all that he endured;

The Lamb of our God, His sufferings were great,

And all that he suffered, have moved me to praise.

3

Wisdom from our God, overflowed from His soul,

His health was kept by our God, the omnipotent Lord;

It was His duty to speak, all of God's truth,

And He always carried, my burdens for me.
