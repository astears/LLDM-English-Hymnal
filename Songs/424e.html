424 SINNER COME TO JESUS CHRIST

Sinner come to Jesus Christ,

Then His blessing you will enjoy;

//See how God is only love,

And He will pardon you this day//

CHORUS

The angels are singing, Hallelujahs to the Lord;

And all the Christians rejoicing,

From all the blessings of God.

2

If you want to be a soldier,

On the front lines of the war;

//Go deny thyself and follow,

Jesus Christ with your cross//

3

Don't deny the time before us,

For the war soon will begin;

//In Ephesians six, fifteen,

You can find your armor there//

4

The war is about to start,

And all the Christians will conquer;

//With the blood of all those righteous,

The Church of God will prevail//
