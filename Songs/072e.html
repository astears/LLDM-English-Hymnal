072 UP HIGH THE ANGELS

Up high the angels sing in one sweet voice,

Men on the earth sing with sonorous voice;

Jesus Christ will come someday on the clouds,

Jesus Christ soon will return.

CHORUS

//Christ will return//

Down to the earth He will come once again;

//Christ will return// soon Christ is coming to reign.

2

Look on the earth at the wind and the sea,

Great signs and wonders are being fulfilled;

All indicated will soon come to be,

And Jesus Christ will come down.

3

All of the dead in Christ Jesus will rise,

From all their sepulchers happy they'll go;

Soon they will find and meet with their dear Lord,

Christ will return some sweet day.

4

Down on a cloud my good Savior will come,

He'll come to earth oh my glorious Lord;

Come Lord we anxiously hope your return,

Christ will return some sweet day.
