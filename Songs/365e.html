365 MY LIFE WAS CRUEL

My life was cruel when I wandered in this world,

My soul was always lost and without hope;

But the great love of Jesus is eternal,

He rescued me from a dangerous place.

CHORUS

With gratitude I glorify His great Name,

I manifest His power to the world;

There is no love, so immense in this whole world,

Just as the love, of Jesus my great King.

2

Eternal life is what Jesus has promised,

If I can persevere in holiness;

If I obey His word without exceptions,

I know He will give me eternal life.

3

So fortunate that day as I remember,

When I was rescued from my wickedness;

He suffered death because of my transgressions,

And in the end taught me to do His will.
