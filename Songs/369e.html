369 OUR LORD IS VERY CLOSE

Our Lord is very close to us,

He is our only God and Lord;

He is the only one who saves,

All men from their dark load of sin.

CHORUS

//The Lord is always very near,

He will endure all of our pain//

2

The faithful people of the Lord,

They will have holy peace and light;

The enemy they will defeat,

As snow he'll melt away from us.

3

Souls living in captivity,

They will have liberty through Christ;

Dark shadows will surround those men,

Full of ungodliness and sin.

4

The evil servants of satan,

They will not have peace on this earth;

Only the saints of Christ will have,

Triumphs and victory while they live.

5

The faithful will see His good face,

And perfect holiness they'll gain;

Oh Christ give me of your great pow'r,

Only your good will I will do.
