313 LIFE IS LIKE A BOAT

Life is like a boat, navigating at nighttime,

Making its journey through the waves of the sea;

And guiding us, Jesus, through fog and the pitfalls,

We can now advance without fear to heaven.

CHORUS

With Christ at the helm, we navigate assured,

Confiding in His grace, He will guide our souls;

And even though strong winds destroy all the sailboats,

The weak little sailboat will not be shipwrecked.

2

And when in the darkness, and lonely obscure nights,

A light guides our pathway; with great brightness too;

And as we are onboard, the hours are shorter,

Feeling as the Savior, draws nearer to us.

3

Even if the winds and the waves get tempestuous,

We confide that one day, to the port we'll arrive;

The sooner we arrive, this saddening voyage,

After long sleepless nights, will come to an end.

4

Then filled with much joy, we'll dock at that port.

Singing songs of glory to Jesus Christ Lord;

And then we'll forget all the hardships that passed us,

Forever we dwell in His beautiful light.
