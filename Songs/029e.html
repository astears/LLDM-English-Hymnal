029 OH I WISH TO BE SERVING THE LORD

Oh I wish to be serving the Lord,

For I know He will save me from sin;

He has promised to lead till the end,

For I know that the Lord will save me.

CHORUS

I have fought in the battle oh Lord,

I will fight to the end of my career;

I have also maintained firm my faith,

I await you to take me away.

2

In this world I will have to survive,

With all those not possessing a faith;

Being born as a child of my God,

I wish never to abandon His love.

3

All the things I may have to endure,

They will never compare to His light;

With the glory of Christ when He comes,

To the faithful He will then impart.

4

Royal crown is awaiting for all,

Who will fight till the end of their days;

Given then by the Lord; fairest judge,

In the day when He makes His return.
