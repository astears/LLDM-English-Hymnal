360 MY SAVIOR LORD

My Savior Lord in His kindness,

Was in this world of wickedness;

A deep abyss of wickedness,

Is where He saved me from.

CHORUS

Jesus gave me security,

When He stretched out His hand to me;

I went from shadows to His light,

He was so kind, He raised me up.

2

His constant voice I did resist,

When lovingly He called to me;

And when I gave heed to His voice,

He faithfully raised me.

3

He suffered cruel torture for me,

When He was suffering on the cross;

Only this way would I be saved,

This way he raised me up.

4

I am happy, I know this well,

With this new life He gave to me,

But still I cannot comprehend,

Why Jesus raised me up.
