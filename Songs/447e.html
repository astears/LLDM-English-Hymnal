447 HOW GREAT IS TO HAVE

How great is to have in the soul a remembrance,

That we were delivered from misery and sin;

For this we sing to Him, a sweet song of joy,

A song which inspires great courage to all.

CHORUS

We sing in sweet harm'ny, united as one,

For the Lord has sent, a Servant of God;

Who has been anointed and chosen for us all,

He brought us together, and has given our souls;

The good news of peace.

2

How great is to serve, our God and Creator,

All anguish has passed, all pain and all grief;

All darkness has left us, so light has arrived,

Our darkness departed, our Savior prevailed.

3

God has compensated all your works of labor,

He's blessed you with gifts, with grace, with knowledge;

The people ignore, that Jesus sent you,

May God prosper you, oh Servant of God.
