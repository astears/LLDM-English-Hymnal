452 WHO IS LIKE YOU MY GOD?

Who is like you my God?

Among all other Gods,

Who is like you? Magnificent, so holy;

Awful in praises, worker of many miracles,

Your right hand oh my God, wonderful things it has done;

Your mighty right hand is your strength oh my Lord,

It's broke in pieces all our cruel enemies.

Jehovah is all of my strength and joyful song;

He has given me health, this is my God,

And I will exalt well His name;
