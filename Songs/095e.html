095 WITH YOUR LOVE

With your love and your blood that's so precious,

You have cleansed me forever, my Lord;

Today I walk in your path so glorious,

//With your light// oh my Christ.

CHORUS

I am nourished by your divine kindness,

I feel peace with your love sustaining me;

A pure love that is light, and does shinneth,

//Of your love// give me more.

2

Don't permit that I ever leave this path,

Even if the cross weighs more each day;

Make a flame to be blazing in my soul,

//Of your light// oh my Christ.

3

When I journey with you up to heaven,

In your dwellings I'll happily live;

And sweet hymns so serene and so loving,

///I will sing///
