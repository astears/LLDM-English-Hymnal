201 YOU ARE LIKE THE CEDARS OF LEBANON

You are like the cedars of Lebanon, just like streams of running water,

In greatness and full of power is the Apostle of God;

Like the stars up in the heavens, like the sand that lie in the sea,

Just like that are His blessed people, people now chosen by our God,

And all powerful in truth.

CHORUS

Israel is an all powerful battalion,

Everyone's ready just like soldiers in a war;

The banners wave all together in high places,

In the midst of the holy people, is the one that our God has chosen;

A great Apostle of the Lord.

2

Who is there like you among the people, you've made laws that are so holy,

On firm rock they have foundation in Jesus Christ our Lord;

No one has our God so nearby, like His people children of God,

All saved now by our Jesus, rejoicing in wondrous teachings;

Holy people of the Lord.

3

He's the most important of His people,

He was chosen by my Jesus,

Apostle all full of God's grace you are the one sent from God;

Your enemies you shall not fear, although like a lion they roar,

The God of gods is your refuge, all of your people are with you;

Holy Apostle of the Lord.
