086 WITH CHRIST MY LORD I LONG

With Christ my lord I long to be,

His marvels always I shall see;

Near my Lord's feet I'll always have,

The glory of my Lord and King.

CHORUS

Oh keep me safe, Lord Jesus Christ,

Always faithful, close to your cross;

Show me oh Lord, all your good will,

And keep me in your sanctity.

2

Make me more patient in my grief,

Fill me with your protection Lord;

I never want to feel dismay,

I'll be firm for my Lord and King.

3

Give me more faith my Savior King,

Impart me of your courage, Lord;

Grant me oh Lord of that firm faith,

I want to be faithful oh Lord.
