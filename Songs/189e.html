189 IN THE DARKNESS

In times of darkness God's light had triumphed through,

With all its brightness, it gleamed and glistened my heart;

And without fear I am walking over hardships,

And through His word I envision victory.

CHORUS

All the people agonized by darkness, saw the light,

All the people who despaired and were hopeless in sin;

His light helped them clearly see, that Christ had taken from their life, all the strife,

From anguishing darkness God rescued me.

2

Be lifted up, Jesus Christ has come to us,

He is the light who will glisten our hearts and souls;

And you will shine like the dawning of the morning,

And you will radiate further than the sun.

3

During creation great light was formed by God,

The sun and moon: luminaries,

He placed on earth; And an Apostle to save most vile sinners,

During one time, I among them had once lived.
