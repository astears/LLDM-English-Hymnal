527 I'M A SOLDIER OF JESUS

I'm a soldier of Jesus, of Christ my Savior Lord,

I will fight for my Lord, in the battle of the faith;

With my eyes on the Lord, only Jesus my Captain,

I will fight against Satan and his hosts.

CHORUS

Hear the voice of Jesus, who is calling,

//Unto the battle//

Oh hear the voice of Jesus He who tells us,

Who today is going to fight?

I am a soldier of Lord Christ Jesus,

And through faith,

I'm going to win the war;

I am a soldier of Lord Christ Jesus,

And the triumph and victory I will have.

2

The word of the Lord is the sword of a great faith,

That which helps me to fight, for my Savior Jesus Christ;

I will fight, gloriously, with the troops of Jesus Christ,

Today you will see me fighting fearlessly.

3

Don't you want to come follow,

Christ your Savior and Lord?

And to fight now for Him, in the battle of the faith?

If you're faithful to Christ, a crown He will place on you,

And with Him you will have felicity.

4

Very soon, I know well, all my struggles will then cease,

And with Jesus Christ Lord, we will reign with Him on high;

Oh what blessing divine, to live with Jesus Christ on high,

Where there is no more sadness, nor sorrow.
