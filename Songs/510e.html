510 IF MY LIFE IS FORGOTTEN

If my life is forgotten, and I'm left alone,

If I walk with depression, in darkness I go;

If I falter in my strength, and my pain conquers me,

There is one who will care for me.

CHORUS

My Friend Jesus Christ, will care for my being,

His shield will not stop guarding me;

He knows all of my sins, and my restlessness too,

Only in Jesus we'll find help.

2

If all my joys are weakened, and now I am sad,

If I see God's glory, falling from my side;

If I am overwhelmed, there are conflicts nearby,

There is one who will care for me.

3

If I am tired from battle, confused without peace,

I cannot find aliment, nor strength though they're near;

In the pathway I will go, sustained by my faith;
