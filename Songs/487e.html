487 LORD I ASK FOR COMPASSION

Lord I ask for compassion, come work within our hearts,

Remove contamination from your beloved church.

CHORUS

And Jesus spoke like this, as He called them all His friends,

For many have been called, but only few are chosen.

2

Don't let me be conceited, so I may be near you,

For that will always prevent us from drinking from your waters.

3

The gifts you gave to us because that's what you promised,

They are to make us stronger to defeat the adversary.

4

Give us love with perfection remove doubt from within us,

Because you have asked for this church without blemish or wrinkles.

5

All anger and all quarrels, all envies and dissensions,

Can never give us perfection which is what we are seeking.

6

Depart yourself from sin, and try to do what's good,

If life is what you are seeking, refrain your tongue from sinning.

7

Never speak of your brother, much less of all the pastors,

For God is never far off, He's always there beside you.

8

Miriam did speak of Moses to her tongue she gave freedom,

She had not finished speaking when in leprosy she'd fallen.
