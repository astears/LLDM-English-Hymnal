441 HOW NOBLE IS OUR MASTER

How noble is our Master,

How could we ever repay?

When He saw me navigating,

In the world of wickedness,

He extended His arm to me;

He wanted to rescue me.

CHORUS

Oh Master, Oh my Master,

Oh Master from Galilee,

Once you saw me wandering as a boat lost out in the ocean;

Oh Master, oh my Master, my Master from Nazareth,

Don't forget about your own; please don't let us perish here.

2

How noble is Jesus Christ, I will never forget that;

Whenever I've sinned against Him,

He has not seen my evils;

He showed me a faithful friendship,

Forgiving my wickedness.

3

My life had already been lost,

The enemy injured me;

But the Good Samaritan did,

Find my soul when passing by;

He extended His arm to me,

And my injuries He cured.

4

I only ask you my Master,

That you augment more my faith;

So that I may keep advancing,

Encouraging Him who doubts;

'Cause the time is drawing nearer,

There's a need to preach the faith.
