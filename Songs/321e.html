321 ALL HAIL TO THEE IMMANUEL

All hail to Thee, Immanuel, we cast our crowns before Thee,

Let ev'ry heart obey Thy will, and ev'ry voice adore Thee;

In praise to Thee our Savior King, the vibrant chords of heaven ring,

And echo back a mighty strain; ////All hail//// Immanuel.

CHORUS

// Hail to Thee oh Immanuel//

Glory and honor and majesty;

Wisdom and power be unto Thee, now and evermore.

//Hail to Thee oh Immanuel// King of kings and Lord of lords,

All hail Immanuel.

2

All hail to Thee Immanuel the ransomed hosts surround Thee,

And earthly monarchs clamor forth, their Sovereign King to crown Thee;

While those redeemed in ages gone, assembled 'round the great white throne,

Break forth into immortal song;

////All hail//// Immanuel.

3

All hail to Thee Immanuel our risen King and Savior,

Thy foes are vanished and Thou art Omnipotent forever;

Death, sin, and hell no longer reign, and Satan's pow'r is burst in twain,

Eternal glory to Thy name;

////All hail//// Immanuel.
