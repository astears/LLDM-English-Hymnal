602 OH, I WANT MORE

Oh, I want more and more of Jesus,

And of His power I want more; Oh,

I want more of all His presence,

Yes I want more and more of Him.

CHORUS

I want to live, closer to Him,

And feel His presence day by day;

Help me to live only for you my Lord,

In holiness until the end.

2

I want to always be with Jesus,

Rejoicing always in His love;

Forever in my life completely,

Be consecrated to my Lord.

3

I want to give my life to Jesus,

For when He comes I'll be prepared;

So He can take me up to heaven,

Where I can serve Him every day.
