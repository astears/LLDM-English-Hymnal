110 AS IF IT WERE SUN OF DAY

As if it were sun of day,

And even more brightly shinning;

Splendor has come into our hearts, to increase our understanding,

Oh what blessed light of Savior Jesus Christ;

Oh divine Christ Jesus Lord,

You are our redeemer Lord.

CHORUS

This light is shining in the church of our Lord,

Because Jesus Christ Lord, gave us the Counselor;

From His Spirit filled with graces,

This is the might of our God;

That is why your people do praise thee,

'Cause of your great love.

2

Now with joy and gratitude,

Your people are reunited;

Joyful because of the health, from Jesus we've received,

Guard us now oh Lord, in holy communion;

Walking onward, all united, so we may enter your home.

3

Through your spirit full of light,

That you've conveyed to your people;

Jesus Christ has now been preached, to the man who was a wandering,

Call them to you Lord, bring them closer to you;

Give them life now, oh Christ Jesus,

To those who you will redeem.
