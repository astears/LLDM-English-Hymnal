103 CHRIST IS THE DOORWAY

Christ is the doorway to heaven,

Because this is how He said it;

When the Lord forgave my soul,

He attired me with garments.

CHORUS

Lord Christ put shoes on my feet,

And He placed a ring on my hand;

Then He named me as a soldier,

Put me in a sovereign squadron;

My God is a God of love,

'Cause He has forgiven my soul.

2

If I keep what I have promised,

Not adoring wicked things;

'Cause the Kingdom of the heavens,

Only valiant ones will enter.

3

I would like to be a soldier,

With the weapons of a Christian;

With the sturdy shield of faith,

To defeat the adversary.
