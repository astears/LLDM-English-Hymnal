582 A VOICE OVER

A voice over this earth has been calling unto us,

That Christ Jesus will be coming down from the heavens;

With His pow'r He will take up all the faithful,

Which He bought, for me it will be great joy, without cease.

CHORUS

Over the heavens, My Lord Christ,

With all His power will come down;

He will illuminate with light,

So I will see Him in glory without a doubt.

All of the legions, that will come,

Over the heavens so clearly;

Giving the news: it's time to go,

And all the living things will have their end.

2

For in me, all of these glorious heavens,

And to see Jesus Christ, in all His beauty;

He will come over the heavens with glory,

In order to take His faithful church with Him up high.

3

Sinner come and ask the Lord for forgiveness,

May He also give you comfort and calmness;

So that righteously you will be living on,

If in truth you do God's will, He will save your soul.
