569 I COME TO BRING YOU

I come to bring you, oh Ambassador,

A song from heaven, new from the royal home;

I come to tell you, don't you be scared,

To make well known to the peoples God's message of peace.

CHORUS

I come to offer, Servant of the Lord,

A golden powder from a great treasure;

It's from the deepest part of all my heart.

2

A gold crown of diamonds,

God has placed on you,

And in a gold seat, will be where you sit;

When the Lord's mission you have fulfilled,

You are my faithful Servant, God will say to you.

3

Now all your children, those esteeming you,

We hope to see you, forever with a smile;

It is a blessing, to see your face,

It is our wish from your hands to receive God's news.
