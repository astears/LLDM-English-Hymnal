153 COUNS'LING SWEET MOMENTS

Couns'ling sweet moments, I've passed on my cross,

Those which I have passed, close to you Christ;

There's so much suffering, agony so painful,

Looking to Jesus, the Lamb of God.

2

Look how His arms are, upraised and open,

His arms inviting to go with Him;

Making as His own, all of my errors,

For me His lips don't mind bitter herbs.

3

Flowing's a fountain, from Jesus's wounds,

Blood I see flowing, from all His veins;

Sprinkling on my head, impure unholy,

My childish faults He, managed to erase.

4

Look at His anguish, how it has perished,

Offering is now done, of penitence;

His noble forehead lowly and inclined,

Redemption for souls, Jesus gave us.

5

Moments so charming, rich in His blessings,

Of peace and grace and, of life and light;

Only there's comfort, and many blessings,

Close to Christ Jesus, close to His cross.
