414 JESUS IS PRAYING

Jesus is praying to the Father,

Suffering in Gethsemane;

And also on His radiant face,

Sweating with blood His forehead be.

CHORUS

Gethsemane, profound remembrance,

Gethsemane, a place of beauty;

Where I can see so humbly kneeling,

The eternal Son of our God.

2

The soul of my good Jesus Christ,

Sad as when death has taken life;

Drinking the bitter chalice for us,

Only to redeem those who've sinned.

3

In the pretorium he has peace,

Although they whip Him furiously;

The crown of thorns that they have made Him,

Now girds the forehead of my Christ.

4

They have now nailed Him to the cross,

Oh sinner see how He bleeds;

He suffered because of your evils,

Cruel shame and pain He bears for you.
