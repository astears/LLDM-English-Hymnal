386 OH JESUS BELOVED

Oh Jesus beloved, my blessed savior Lord,

For you I have left all of my pathways of error;

I want to serve you, because you have loved my soul,

///Once I did not love you/// but Lord I do so now.

CHORUS

Arriving to the heavens, these words I'll surely sing,

///I was vile, unworthy/// but Jesus has saved me.

2

I love you oh Christ, because in your endless love,

You took the defilements, embarrassment, and pain;

Crown of thorns and needles, you wore for me Jesus,

///Once I did not love you/// but Lord I do so now.

3

In life and in death I will always love you Lord,

And with songs of glory my voice will sing your praise;

Because on a cross you died for me oh Jesus,

///Once I did not love you/// but Lord I do so now.

4

In holy sweet dwellings of everlasting joy,

To Christ Jesus the Lamb, forever immortal;

Forever I'll sing, what joy will be for me!

///Once I did not love you/// but Lord I do so now.
