428 BY FAITH I'M GOING TO REACH

By faith I'm going to reach eternal life,

The most treacherous storms I'll overcome;

Since my Lord Jesus Christ erases my woes,

By faith His face in glory I will see.

CHORUS

By faith, I'll surely see,

By faith I'll surely wait;

By faith, I will enjoy eternal life.

2

Oh great sweet hope that is feeding my soul,

To live in the dear arms of Jesus Christ;

Through triumph I will have a crown in glory,

Dwelling in heavenly mansions of light.

3

Oh Lord, forgive my weaknesses and sickness,

That I can be victorious through my faith;

That I never return to my perversions,

The glories of the life I left behind.

4

Oh Lord if in my soul my faith does perish,

Oh Lord, I beg to augment more my faith;

May I cross the rough sea, to reach the shoreline,

Always be my dear Lord, my faithful guide.

5

I have full confidence in Lord Christ Jesus,

That I am justified only through faith;

By faith I will observe what I cannot see,

By grace I am saved Lord, only through faith.
