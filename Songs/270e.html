270 OH HOLY CHURCH

Oh holy church, mansion of light and life,

Abundant blessings you have from Jesus Christ;

You are the glory of every and all Christians,

And only God our Father we love with all our hearts.

CHORUS

Jesus, oh Lord do bless us,

In our reunion with all your promises;

May many souls come and be repenting,

And find salvation when they come to you.

2

You are the glory oh my beloved Jesus,

Of those who walk in and love the faith and truth;

It is our pride that we serve the God of heaven,

And sing to Him with yearning, and live in holiness.

3

You have from Jesus a doctrine sent from heaven,

That is the work of our Savior Jesus Christ;

In different places you have a Christian brother,

Where the gospel is reaching with peace and holy love.

4

Oh blessed church you are filled with many blessings,

'Cause your enjoying the favor of the Lord;

May the evangel illuminate your being,

And carry to the whole earth, the news of salvation.
