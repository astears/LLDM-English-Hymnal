316 HOLY BOOK

Holy book that by God was inspired,

It's a fountain truthful and eternal;

You have come to break loose all the chains,

Of all sin, ignorance, and of fear.

You have spoken to us of the mansions,

In a far off joyful destiny; Manifested to our eyes you've placed it,

What a saint will receive from our God.

2

That divine mandate has been so perfect,

As it guides every soul so uprightly;

And the little ones you love so much,

All your teachings can help them to grow.

Perfect peace and joy you've given to them,

All the mortals who drank from your streams;

An immortal reward will be given,

If they keep your commands faithfully.

3

Continue always shining in this world,

For there's millions who do not know God's light;

May they feel your presence and rejoice,

May your name be known throughout the world.

Blessed God now come enlighten my mind,

So that I may see your perfect truth;

Make me guard it obediently always,

While I praise your fine excellency.
