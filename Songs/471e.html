471 ROCK OF THE ETNERNITY

Rock of the eternity, you have been opened just for me,

Be my faithful hiding place, only in you I can find peace;

Rock of the eternity, you have been opened just for me,

Rich and clean fountain of love in which I was cleansed and washed.

CHORUS

If the world gives me caresses, with its foolish vanity,

My Lord offers unto me now, the immortal crown of life;

I leave this world and its glories, and the mundane friendship too,

Be my faithful hiding place, rock of all eternity.

2

In the meantime that I'm here, in life or death I will love thee,

Till I'm asked to give account, unto the righteous tribunal;

In the meantime that I'm here, in life or death I will love thee,

Be my faithful hiding place, rock of all eternity.

3

I will not be justified, when I am faced with all my sins,

Only having faith through you, will I ever pay such debt;

Now your blood has come to cleanse me, And your love gave me forgiveness;

Be my aid and protector, through the cruel tribulations.
