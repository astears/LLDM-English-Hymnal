237 LONG AGO

Long ago a child was born, by the will of God,

With kindness and tenderness,

His mom cared for him;

God was guiding Him through the pathways of his love,

To be his faithful Servant.

CHORUS

Oh, Apostle of God, may God guard you always,

Faithfully, always serving the Lord;

As your father Aaron, so you'll be,

He left you an example, and all of his virtues he gave you;

He gave you to the Lord, with all of his heart and soul.

2

Aaron was a Servant very loved by the Lord,

His words were as water to the dry desert lands;

As his flesh was dying, he did carry a smile,

Unto the eternity.

3

Struggles the Lord gives you may last many long days,

But full of God's spirit, he will be guiding you;

Faithful to His orders follow without delays,

And He will give you your prize.

4

You're a Prince sent by God in the Church of the Lord,

All the church does love you as a Servant of God;

By the Holy order, your are destined to be,

Servant of Lord Jesus Christ.
