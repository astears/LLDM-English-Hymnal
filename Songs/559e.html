559 ONE MORNING

One morning He illuminated my soul,

The glorious light of my eternal God;

All of the darkness then fled from my soul,

Beautiful dawning my soul now enjoys.

CHORUS

I will give praise only unto God,

With the angels up in heaven;

In the perfume scented mornings,

To my Maker I will sing.

2

And in the mornings as I am awake,

All the redeemed give their praises to God;

They all unite in a beautiful prayer,

They sing their hymns sung with might and with zeal.

3

My soul is filled with great joy as I sing,

When I wake up in the morning with Him;

All of the flowers overflow with love,

They all exhale tender scents unto God.

4

I heard in the deepest part of my soul,

A new song that I offered unto God;

All of my faults He has forgiven me,

With living water that Christ gave to me.

5

The Lord adorned the blue skies up above,

With all the stars with the moon and the sun;

In this same way He has transformed my soul,

He took the blemishes from my stained heart.

6

Up in the heights where my mind sometimes goes,

I see the dawning of paternal love;

Now everything has become light for me,

I walk with Jesus onward till the end.
