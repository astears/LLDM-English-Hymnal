283 JESUS CHRIST TO EARTH WILL COME

Jesus Christ to earth will come again,

He has promised us,

To pick up all His faithful church, that He has redeemed.

CHORUS

Oh//what sweet peace//that Christ Jesus has put in my soul,

Oh//what sweet peace//I'm not able to grasp;

Oh what sweet peace, that Christ put in my being,

Hallelujah to Him for His kindness!

2

In the service of Jesus Christ,

I feel so comforted, In the service of the Lord,

I do find much peace.

3

In the service of Jesus Christ, one encounters the truth,

From the instructions of my Lord, one encounters peace.

4

In the doctrine of my dear Lord, my faith is affirmed,

From the instructions of my Lord, my faith does persist.

5

In the church of Lord Jesus Christ, freedom I have,

From the instructions of my Lord, with love I do sing.
