038 ANGELS KNEEL IN REVERENCE

Angels kneel in reverence to the Lord, our King,

Singing all their glories and their praises to;

My Savior Lord.

CHORUS

On this earth there's also angels of great light,

Giving Hallelujahs to my Savior Lord.

2

Young and old together come sing to our God,

United with the universe in one loud voice; Unto the Lord.

3

Announcing that on earth there is a Man of God,

With a message of salvation coming from above;

The place of love.

4

Servant of the Lord, God shall safeguard you,

With the church always united and in holiness;

With liberty.

5

The message given to me by the Man of God,

Has brought me peace and gratitude within my heart,

Giving me joy.
