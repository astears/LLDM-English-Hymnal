255 OH LORD I KNOW

Oh Lord I know that your beauty's majestic,

My redeemer, Jesus who does us well;

You have imparted me your love,

And by your love you saved my soul.

My lover is white skinned with blonde hair,

And He is noticed in a crowd;

And His head shines as a rock of gold,

And His splendor is like the sun.

CHORUS

Song of all the songs sing to your people,

You are beautiful, most Holy Jesus;

In my soul there is a mark, it is marked with your love,

Your love is sweet to my being.

2

My Redeemer He is with great beauty,

And with His love He fills up well my heart;

And in the arms of Christ my Lord, My heart takes pleasure in His love.

Just as a pastor is caring for His flock,

Jesus Christ did show His love;

And now exclaiming in a loud voice,

I live with joy in Jesus Christ.

3

I am of Jesus, and Jesus lives in me,

The testimony of God Jesus has told;

'Cause in my being he has marked,

And I have marked myself in Him.

I can now say that I am of my loved one,

And that my loved one is of me;

And if I'm living, I live for Jesus,

And if I'm dying, I die with joy.
