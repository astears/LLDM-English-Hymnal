296 ALONGSIDE OF MY GOD

Alongside of my God is where I'll abide,

Although I know that this world will not love me;

//I am not of this world//

Only with him I travel, heaven's my home.

2

My soul can only love God and no one else,

Because He fills all my soul with His sweet love;

//And truthful is my Lord//

Only His word is saying:

God is all love.

3

My God, my goodness, my all, please come to me,

Don't let me abide ever far from Thee Lord;

//You will receive me there//

I cannot be without thee, oh no I can't.
