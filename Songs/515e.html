515 YOU SOLDIERS OF JESUS CHRIST

You soldiers of Jesus Christ,

And vanguards of this blest fleet;

We will have God's blessing always,

If we walk in His justice.

For God's truth we will be battling,

Defeating temptations too;

And at the end of our journey,

Salvation we will obtain.

CHORUS

Every Christian's utmost care,

Is to seek their holiness;

Amidst suffering and battling,

We will obtain God's great love.

2

Fear not my beloved brethren,

For you will obtain a crown;

As you go in the Lord's mansion,

His welcome you will receive.

Walking always in obedience,

In the perfect path of peace;

One day we will be rewarded,

The promise God gave to us.

3

The seal of holiness brethren,

Every Christian will obtain;

For it is a mighty weapon,

That destroys all wickedness.

Those walking along with Jesus,

Never turn to look back;

They decline any advantage,

That this world may offer them.
