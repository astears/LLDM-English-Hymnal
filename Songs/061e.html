061 BLESSED BE OUR KING

Blessed be our King, the King of the kings,

Who with His strong arm has freed us from death;

Blessed be His name now and forever,

Because He guards us each and every day,

Glory to Christ Jesus, whose always' worthy of praise, Amen!

CHORUS

Glory to Jesus my King, glory to my great King,

Hallelujah let us sing to Him, for His sovereign power.

2

Great is the joy, also contentment,

There's in God's church, a people of the Lord;

To see completed until this day,

To be united, everyone in love,

Let us all be praising, the King of Zion, Lord Jesus Christ.

3

Holy bride of Christ, the Lamb so faithful,

Today is the day to praise our Lord;

For all His virtues, also His kindness,

That our Lord Jesus has given to us,

Glory to Lord Jesus, who lets His blessings descend to us.

4

To the Lord Jesus the King of kings,

Who now is seated in His majesty;

Let us forever, be praising His name,

Because He conquered the most evil one,

For His sovereign power, a strength more mighty than satan's host.
