349 MY GOOD JESUS

//My good Jesus, conquered my soul,

And never from Him will I depart//

Never will I make sad my good Lord.

CHORUS

///Never will I grieve/// my good Lord Christ.

2

Look at the sun, look at it rise,

Never will it stop, to take a rest;

Oh look at the sun arise,

Creation will never cease to work;

Never will I make my good Lord sad.

3

Speak everything you wish of me,

When I am in prayer, I'll speak of you;

Speak as much as you wish of me,

Only to the Lord, I'll speak of you;

Never will I make my good Lord sad.

4

//One day in justice we'll all go,

And gossiping lips, will cease to speak//

Never will I make my good Lord sad.

5

//Maybe at morning when it is dawn,

With wings through the air, He will return//

Never will I make my good Lord sad.
