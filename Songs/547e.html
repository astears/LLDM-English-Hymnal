547 TAKE LORD

Take Lord and keep my heart and soul,

Your will I would like to comply;

Just as I am, I give to you,

Oh come and reign in me.

CHORUS

Take all my being, oh Father,

I trust in all your goodness;

My life, transform oh my Lord,

According to your will.

2

Take away all my vanity,

All of my pride and worldly ways;

In your compassion give to me,

A heart that's pure and clean.

3

Help me to live like Jesus Christ,

Fill all of me, with love and light;

I want to serve you oh, my Lord,

Devoted and with love.
