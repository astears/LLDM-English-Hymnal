589 TIMES OF TRIALS

Times of trials are now approaching,

The antichrist will be coming real soon;

And when this life of trials is over,
Jesus Christ will give us something new.

CHORUS

//Faithful I will be to the Lord,

He will reward me with the crown of life//

2

All those purchased at the price of love,

With the blood of the Lord Jesus Christ;

With resignation they will suffer,

As Christ did being nailed to the cross.

3

As the prophets at one time announced,

This is word that is true and is real;

Just as they killed the saints in those days,

So today there will also be death.

4

If you would like to serve our Lord,

You must seek for His power above;

So that during any persecution,

You and I may successfully win.
