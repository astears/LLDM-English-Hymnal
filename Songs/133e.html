133 FROM THE DEPTH

Out of the depths, oh Lord I cry to you,

Oh Lord, hear my voice;

Let your ears be attentive,

To my cry and plea for mercy.

•••••

If you, oh Lord, kept a record of sin,

Oh who oh Lord, could stand before you?

But with you there is forgiveness,

Therefore you are feared.

•••••

I wait for the Lord, my soul awaits,

And in His word, I put my hope.
