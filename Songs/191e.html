191 IN THE INSTANCES

In all the moments when temptation comes,

In the black hours, when my soul's confused;

Oh Jesus Christ, imploring of your pow'r,

Come quick towards me, give me pardon, Lord.

CHORUS

Jesus come with all your power,

Free my soul from Satan's trickeries;

Your strong hand will be sustaining my faith,

And humbly I, will follow you Lord.

2

In your example I would like to walk,

So that in glory I will live with you;

If I'm seduced by this world full of sin,

Unto your glory, you'll conduct me, Lord.

3

In all the places, my souls tempted, Lord,

My weakened body is about to fall;

But with your courage you give me the strength,

And with your love, increase all my faith.

4

The world does want me to succumb to them,

It wants to fill me with its wickedness;

You reach towards my hand, and you lift me up,

With your love I'll, follow you my Lord.
