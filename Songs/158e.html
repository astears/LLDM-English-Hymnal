158 THE SPIRIT OF LIFE

The spirit of life came to me,

For to my soul one day it came;

Never could I comprehend,

That Jesus is the light to my life.

CHORUS

Hear well the word of our God,

It was given to God's Servant;

Don't fear to go to the battle,

'Cause you've been sent by our Jesus.

2

When I came to this church of God,

My soul felt much joy inside me;

Jesus made me see His light,

I'll be saved if I am faithful.

3

Christ is the light of the daytime,

My soul feels happy and joyous;

He is the guider of our souls,

And at His feet we should all be serving.

4

Christ is your strength and your victory,

Always be guided by Jesus;

Brother do come to His people,

'Cause all souls can be saved by Jesus.
