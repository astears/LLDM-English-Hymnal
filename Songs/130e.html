130 IT'S DESIRED THIS MOUNT OF ZION

It's desired, this Mount of Zion,

They are running towards it all of the people;

Giving glories ever to our King,

Tribute praises forever to Him.

CHORUS

Mount of all my sanctity, You will be so named,

Zion of the Lamb of love;

'Cause in the arms of our God, for you have been marked,

And in the great heart of God, you have been sealed too.

2

Oh you church, so blessed from our God,

Because all this world someday will pass away;

And to love always our God, forever,

To Christ Jesus our King oh praise Him.

3

It was Jesus who gave you your life,

Consecrated through all of the Lord's virtues;

All the children of God do desire.

To be loved by Him forever more.
