140 DAY OF FEASTING

Day of feasting, day of gladness in the house of my God,

Happy, be my soul forever; sing to your Savior with joy;

Be united with the cleansed ones, by the grace full of His love,

And united we will praise our Savior Lord.

CHORUS

Come, Lord Jesus Christ, Hear my clamor Lord,

Contemplating you in your sweet home;

Of your blessing Lord, fill up my heart,

Only you reign Lord in my heart and soul.

2

Holy Spirit do be guiding, all my songs unto the Lord,

May you tie our hearts together, with your holy love,

Oh Lord; Take away all of my sadness, flowing deep within my heart,

And the Savior will receive us united.

3

Jesus is near at the doorway, let his people have no doubt,

For our eyes are now perceiving, the fulfillment of His law;

Of his people he demands that we be quick to reconcile,

And united we will all praise our Lord King.

4

We are hoping for your coming, and the calling of your voice,

May you tie our heart together, with the fire of your love;

We will rise up to the heavens, for all of eternity,

And forever we will praise our Savior Lord.
