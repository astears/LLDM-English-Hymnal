156 THE DAY PREDICTED

The day predicted is coming, Jesus Christ will return,

What a sublime apparition, all eyes will witness this;

And multitudes of all people, will be around His throne,

Hymns which have never been heard, will inundate the earth.

CHORUS

//Glory// to Christ, King for eternity, //Glory// to Christ,

His people will proclaim; Glory, Hallelujah,

His people will proclaim,

And His faithful believers, to glory He will take.

2

For those of the chosen flock, they will not be confused,

Perpetually they'll be dwelling in the mansion of light;

There will be no cries and weeping, they will all disappear,

Forever in untold delight, with Christ they will rejoice.

3

Sorrowful hours of torment, try to weaken my soul,

A more divine sanctuary, with Jesus I will have;

The thorns that painfully pierce me, will no longer prevail,

Difficult trials won't matter, they won't opaque His love.

4

My thirsty soul is desiring, more holiness Lord,

I want to follow the steep path of immortality;

With all the saints on that day, in your sweet presence be,

In a spiritual concert, praising your pleasant name.
