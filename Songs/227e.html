227 WHAT DAY OF GLORY

What day of glory when God's voice I had heard,

You spoke to my soul with sweet and tender love;

And by God's grace, my soul was moved within me,

As I surrendered, forgiveness you gave me.

CHORUS

Now the peace that's flooding my soul,

Is the proof that he pardoned me;

Only God came to save my soul,

And to offer me sweet rest.

2

I wandered hopeless in this unfaithful world,

And I was giving my soul to wickedness;

I was so lost in transgressions and in evil,

All of the people despised me everywhere.

3

I did not know deep within my wicked heart,

That God could remove from me the wicked curse;

Today by his grace, which has finally reached me,

Purchased by bloodshed, when nailed to the wood cross.

4

It is my longing to be close to Christ,

And with the saints I would like to live one day;

I know in heaven, it is rejoicing forever,

There will be no pain, no anguish and no tears.
