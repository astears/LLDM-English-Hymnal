453 WHO IS THE ONE

Who is the one who is humble and contrite,

And comes to Jesus asking for helping hand?

Who is the one who embarrasses themselves,

And doesn't receive an answer to all their prayers?

CHORUS

//Who is the one?//

Who comes to me, yes to me with all their heart?

//Who is the one?//

Who doesn't receive what they are asking in prayer?

2

Jesus has said: ask, and I shall give to you,

All the petitions that your heart will have;

And when you knock I will also open up,

So I can crown you with my holy blessings too.

3

Don't ever tire when praying to your Lord,

If something's missing, demand of it with faith;

For Jesus Christ in His love that's never ending,

Has left a promise saying: ask, and I will give.

4

Who is the one who is asking for forgiveness,

And I reject Him because of all their sins?

You should know that I have come to give salvation,

Come to me quickly and you will have salvation.
