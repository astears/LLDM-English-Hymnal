173 IN JESUS CHRIST MY SOUL IS HAPPY

In Jesus Christ my soul is happy,

Precious is His celestial gift;

His voice returns calmness to me,

His face forgives me of my sin.

CHORUS

//I do rejoice with Him//

His joy and His peace;

Are flooding my soul,

Well, I do rejoice in him.

2

A long time ago He did find me,

He brought me to His blessed church;

In His arms of love I was taken,

Where thousands of fortunes I found.

3

His paternal love does surround me,

His grace can now comfort my soul;

I'm flooded by His Holy Spirit,

It's a strange and powerful might.

4

Someday I will be like His image,

When I leave this body behind;

Meanwhile as a faithful disciple,

I would like to be till the end.
