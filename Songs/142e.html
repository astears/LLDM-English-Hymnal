142 MAY GOD BLESS THE SOULS

May God bless the souls who come together,

Through ties of sacrosanct love united;

May He keep them from all pain and suffering,

In this world filled with thorns and with strife.

May the home that begins to be formed,

With the union of these hearts together;

May they always enjoy many blessings,

At the shield of the God of Israel.

2

As these two are approaching the altar,

They have come to swear their faith together;

May they always be searching the fountain,

The Lord's secret of immortal life.

If perhaps there is pain and there's sadness,

Which one day may be found as they walk;

Only Jesus will give them sweet guidance,

And His pathway He'll surely show them.

3

May the Lord with His sweet loving presence,

Come to give His blessing to this wedding;

And through His path of life may He guide them,

These who swear loyalty on this day.

He'll remind them that nothing in this world,

Is eternal all things have an ending;

In the meantime with His divine grace,

They will base all their joy in their God.
