171 THE SOLDIER

The soldier who prevails God will honor,

He's sent to fulfill his mission here;

He should always be constant in service,

Otherwise he will not have a reward.

CHORUS

The soldier who in Jesus is fighting,

Being faithful to their General;

In the heavens your crown is awaiting,

This is one of many celestial gifts.

2

The soldier who's unfaithful in battle,

In the battle he turns to look behind;

There is danger from the darts of evil,

And a little of evil is seen well.

3

All of they who do fight in the Lord's grace,

Conquering all the glory of peace;

From their faith and from hoping they're shielded,

In your soul may you always carry them.

4

The soldier who confides in Christ Jesus,

It's the teaching to find liberty;

In his soul he will be decorated,

With the flames of fire from above.
