184 ON THE MOUNTAIN JESUS DID DIE

On the mountain Jesus has died and victory He's gained,

He has now freed me from my sins and all my wickedness;

And here I am free from all sins, woes, faults, and judgments too,

To His eternal glory He'll take me.

CHORUS

To heaven I will go, and Christ I will see there,

A home of peace where eternal light does reign;

Its light will be the Lord; there will be no pain there,

Brother of mine I want to see you there.

2

In the steps of Christ Jesus I go towards eternal light,

My tears He quenches and He frees me from all of my pain;

Through paths of woes and burden He will guard me with His love,

How sweet it is to walk with my Jesus.

3

My home is in the heavens and my flag is made of love,

As part of my inheritance His spirit He gave me;

The holy word of our Lord is my provision here,

By grace to my celestial home I go.
