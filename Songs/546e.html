546 WE HAVE ALL NOW BEEN SUBMERGED

We have all now been submerged, just as the servant Naaman was,

Our leprosy was buried in the waters of Jordan.

CHORUS

And submerged we were all together,

In the baptism of Jesus Christ;

Only through His death we are hopeful,

Because He is our life and our light.

2

In the ocean and the clouds, we can see in Corinthians 10,

They were all baptized in Jesus, all together with Moses.

3

And Saint Peter does remind us that they were saved through their faith,

Noah's family was protected from the waters on that day.

4

Welcome oh my Brother! Welcome! May you be with all my love,

Because you have been submerged just as the Savior asked of us.
