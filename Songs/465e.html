465 I WANT TO BE FAITHFUL LORD

I want to be faithful Lord, persevering till the end,

I desire to hear your voice, and desire to hear your call;

Carry me to live in Zion, where there's life forever more,

A city without a sun, where the moon's light is no more.

CHORUS

Oh Jesus when you come down with your sovereign power,

Remember me oh Lord; please don't leave me behind;

If I'm found in my tomb and I can't speak to you,

May your angel awaken me there.

2

Carry me to heaven Lord, where all saints will go to reign,

Help me triumph over sin, and protection give to me;

Because in your house Lord, many dwellings we will find,

And he who has loved his neighbor, through the doorway he will pass.

3

I also ask of you Lord, for the gift of charity,

He who loves his neighbor too, has fulfilled all of your laws;

But those who do not have love, their souls surely will be lost,

And they will not have a pass, to enter my Gods mansion.

4

God of heaven and of this earth, I ask from you through this song,

Have compassion on my being; don't erase me from your book;

When your beloved Son comes, to redeem all of your people,

May you call me to your right side, to the place of the righteous.
