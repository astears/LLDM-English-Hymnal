066 OH MY BROTHER

Oh my brother be welcomed to the battle,

To the battle where truce is not to be;

Where the Lord sends all His valiant soldiers,

They'll prevail through faith in Jesus Christ.

CHORUS

Unto the battle with our faith, we all walk after Him in His steps,

Until the heavenly place we all arrive;

Giving the praise to Christ Jesus our King.

2

The invincible sword placed into your hands,

Carefully with your skill handle your sword;

You will conquer the crude adversary,

Through the faith in Christ Jesus my King.

3

Oh ye soldier of Christ fight with much valor,

In the battle of waving coat of arms;

Preach the gospel, don't stop, yes announce it,

Be ye strong when the dragon you fight.

4

Oh you valiant you'll see your forehead girded,

With a crown and a diadem of gold;

Singing glories, Hosannas to our King,

On the clouds He'll come down to take us.
