558 A HOPE WAS BORN

A hope was born and reigning in my soul,

Since times of darkness when I was a slave;

Without directions walking place to place,

But Jesus saved my soul and gave me light.

CHORUS

Salvation's in Jesus Christ,

Don't doubt His power, trust in Him;

Come then to Him without delay,

Come to give Him all of your heart.

2

I am a witness to the power of God,

'Cause many wonders He has done in me;

I once was blind, but now I see the light,

Celestial light, that which is Jesus Christ.

3

Christ in His death atoned for all our sins,

A special sacrifice for you and me;

Rest you will have in the sheepfold of God,

Repenting fully from all of your faults.

4

Don't wander more, you who is lost in sin,

Come to Christ Jesus, He'll forgive your soul;

Leave this whole world and all its vanity,

Salvation only comes from Jesus Christ.
