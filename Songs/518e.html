518 WE ARE THE WORKERS OF THE LORD

We are the workers of the Lord, with love we're sowing seeds of joy,

News of salvation we have preached of Jesus Christ, to him who's lost;

Now we are working well with love, with Jesus Christ our Savior Lord,

Because He gave His life for souls, and in the Calvary He has died.

CHORUS

Let us now go to reap, the Master's calling,

We all should go do some work, tonight and tomorrow;

The time's passing in a flash, Brother it will not return,

Come you worker come and go to work!

2

Let's all go out and preach the Word, to every nation, race, and tribe,

And without fear and loyalty, lifting the flag up now today;

In all our hands the Lord has placed, this great commission of His love,

This news I give you to go preach and all the victory you will have.

3

Hours and days do quickly pass, the time will never be returned,

If you don't preach they will not know, of His apostle and Christ's word;

The world is dying without faith, of God's true doctrine to be saved,

Oh don't despise you this honor that Jesus Christ gives you this day.
