242 UNTIL WHEN LORD?

How long, oh Lord ? Will you forget me forever?

How long, will you hide your face from me?

How long must I wrestle with my thoughts,

And every day have sorrow and pain in my heart?

How long will my enemy triumph and be over me?

•••••

Look on me and answer me, oh my God.

Give light to my eyes, or I will sleep in death;

My enemy will say, "I've overcome him,"

And my foes will rejoice when I fall, yes when I fall.

•••••

But I, trust in your unfailing love,

My heart rejoices in salvation, and your health;

//I will sing to the Lord, for He has been good to me//
