284 OH DIVINE JESUS

Oh divine Jesus, faithful and blessed,

I do surrender upon your feet;

I come expressing my gratitude,

There's no other love that's given me peace;

That is found in this world.

CHORUS

With all my heart and my soul,

I give you my song,

There's no way that I can Lord, ever repay you;

I want to give you my Lord, all of my soul.

2

You have suffered death to save my soul,

Your sacrifice has redeemed my life;

I want to serve you, good Jesus Christ,

I want to arrive at that mansion above;

Where you're reining my Lord.

3

Up in the heavens where you are reigning,

I'll have the blessing to see your face;

Your face of beauty, flowing with love,

With you I will be, rejoicing like this;

For all centuries, Amen.
