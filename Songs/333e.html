333 FILL UP ALL OF MY HEART

Fill up all of my heart, fill it with your presence, oh Lord,

Give me more joy, more peace, contentment, give me gladness;

So that my soul can feel Lord, your holy blessing too.

CHORUS

//Give me more joy, give me more gladness and more peace,

So that my soul can feel, that you are by my side//

2

I feel your power heals, all my afflictions, pains, and cares,

For this I ask you to come fill all my soul;

With your beautiful fire, your Holy Spirit Lord.

3

Fill up all of my heart, fill it with your presence, oh Lord,

I want to feel you reigning deep in my soul,

When my cups overflowing, I will give you all praise.
