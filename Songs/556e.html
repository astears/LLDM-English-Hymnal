556 YOUR HOLY WORD

Your holy word calls the peoples, every person who has sinned,

To fill us with all your love and to give us calmness too;

If accepting with all our souls, we will feel it in our hearts,

That you truly, Christ our Savior, love us, that is why you died.

CHORUS

Heaven, earth, moon, and the sunshine, everything will pass away,

Only your eternal words for the centuries will last.

2

Your word saving all the sinners, it is life, it's light, and truth,

That illuminates toward heaven where you're seated my dear Lord;

May I have the honor, Jesus, to always carry your cross,

And I'll reach and live in heaven, where you dwell now, my Lord Christ.

3

Your word is the bread of life 'tis the nourishment for souls,

When arriving to the wounded, 'tis relieving and gives peace;

Everyone who will receive it, they will never ever die,

Because you want them forever, to live with you in the sky.
