337 MARCH ON WITH JOY

March on with joy oh people of the Lord,

Praising and singing our glories to our God;

It is Jesus who lovingly invites us,

To come and join him in this banquet made with love.

CHORUS

God had predestined His church before creation,

And once His people had been scattered through the world;

But in His kindness He's brought us all together,

Oh come and join Him in this banquet made with love.

2

If you want to be reconciled with Jesus,

You must abandon the illusions of this world;

Come now worship the feet of Jesus Christ,

Enjoy His blessings and forgiveness from your sin.

3

All of the saints who rejoice in His communion,

Relinquish all that kept them far from Jesus Christ;

This feast is made in remembrance of His death,

And as a memory of His passion and His love.
