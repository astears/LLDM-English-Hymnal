271 TIRELESS COMPANIONS

Tireless friends on this journey,

Let's be faithful to the Lord;

Through our Jesus we are warriors,

With His weapons and His light;

We will overcome satan.

CHORUS

I journey with Christ,

While carrying my cross;

I'm leaving this world,

With so little light.

2

Let us hurry my companions,

Let us work now without cease;

Because here we are just pilgrims,

We are messengers of God;

There's no rest till we arrive.

3

If we're tired from the pathway,

And we sit to meditate;

Someone else will take our place,

There won't be a place for us;

There's no rest till we arrive.

4

When arriving at the presence,

And the glory of the Lord;

He will give a beauteous bequest,

To the ones who did prevail;

There's no rest till we arrive.

5

Let us not abandon our path,

Or cease to fight the war;

We're all pilgrims on a journey,

At the end we will prevail;

There's no rest till we arrive.

6

Once we finalize our journey,

From here to eternity;

God will give us a white vestment,

To all those who persevered;

We'll arrive to our sweet home.
