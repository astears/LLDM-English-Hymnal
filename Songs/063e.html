063 BLESSED MAY YOU BE

Blessed may you be, so full of strength,

May God bless you, and be your rampart;

You were elected to finish His work,

The Lord Christ Jesus bestowed upon you.

CHORUS

You're an Apostle of our Lord Jesus,

And in His divine grace He has sent you;

To be announcing His blessed gospel,

So he who's wandering will come into God's light.

2

You did not know of, God's plan for you,

Until you heard from, the voice of God;

That voice that told you, "I am your God,"

You were elected, Man of our God.

3

You're an Apostle, from God on high,

We are your children, given from God;

You can now say, as Paul once said,

We're the seal of your apostleship, Brother.

4

The years are passing, so are the times,

We are the fruits, of all your sufferings;

We are your children, given from God,

We're the seal of your apostleship, Brother.
