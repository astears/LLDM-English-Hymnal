159 THE HOLY SPIRIT

The Holy Spirit is being conveyed and is shedding,

Among us in this church;

Blessings coming from heaven are now contemplated,

Just like early showers.

The brothers are preaching,

They speak things from heaven they're singing and crying;

It's a feast from the heavens it's the one that we're seeing,

Like that day at Pentecost, oh glorious day.

2

If we ask for the Unction of the most high God,

We will be rejoicing; It is the Holy Spirit, who will fill our lives here,

With ineffable joy. Precious gifts will be given,

And a feast we will have as we sing Hallelujahs;

All the sick will be healed as they lay hands over them,

Because God is the same now and forever more.

3

When Christ comes back descending, with great might and power,

We will see His glory;

All the stars will be singing, the heavens, the oceans,

The rivers and the mountains. In the whole universe our eyes will be watching,

The glory of Jesus; In that glorious day when Jesus Christ will descend,

For He will come back to take all His children there.
