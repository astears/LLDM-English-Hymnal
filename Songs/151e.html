151 SWEET HOUR OF PRAYER

//Sweet hour of prayer// That calls me from a world of care;

And bids me at my Father's throne,

Makes all my wants and wishes known.

In seasons of distress and grief,

My soul has always found relief;

And oft escaped the tempter's snare,

By thy return, sweet hour of prayer.

2

// Sweet hour of prayer// Thy wings shall my petition bear;

To Him whose truth and faithfulness,

Engage the waiting soul to bless.

And since He bids me seek His face,

Believe His word and trust His grace;

I'll cast on Him my every care,

And wait for thee, sweet hour of prayer.

3

// Sweet hour of prayer// May I thy consolation share;

Till from Mount Zion's lofty height,

I view my home and take my flight.

This robe of flesh I'll drop and rise,

To seize the everlasting prize;

And shout while passing through the air,

Farewell, farewell, sweet hour of prayer.
