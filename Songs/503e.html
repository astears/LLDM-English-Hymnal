503 I FOLLOW MY SAVIOR

My Redeemer and Lord,

I follow through the path,

I'm a sheep of his great flock;

I am guided with love; by my good shepherd,

I'm the sheep under His care.

CHORUS

How great is my Lord, the most wonderful in deeds,

He forgives all our sins, not repaying evilly.

2

I follow Jesus Christ; He is guidance to me,

He's my Sheppard and my Father;

As I journey through life, he keeps me on the path,

From all evil things he guards me.

3

Jesus Christ my dear Lord,

He who's caring for me,

He is watching from distance;

His protecting my path, always guiding me through,

Not a moment He forgets me.
