391 OH HOW PRECIOUS

Oh how precious it is to worship Jesus Christ,

And meditate in all the words of our Lord;

Always waiting with great patience for His coming,

Consolation for eternity will be.

CHORUS

//It will be so precious to fly to heaven,

Arrive with Jesus and give Him praise//

2

How very precious is the meeting of the brothers,

Those who are baptized in the name of Jesus Christ;

Always waiting for the promise from their Father,

That through Jesus He will send to us with love.

3

And on that day when justice will be shinning highly,

We will all be under the order of our Lord;

Leaping up just as a calf does in a wild herd,

Giving glories, hallelujahs to the Lord.

4

Look at those masses they are clean and have been whitened,

Who might they be? They come with palms placed in their hands;

These ones have passed through many great tribulations,

And the blood of Jesus Christ has cleansed their souls.
