600 I HAVE FOUND A FAITHFUL FRIEND

I have found a faithful friend His name is Jesus,

More than Father, Mother, Brother, He;

More than silver, more than gold, and more than copper,

Of all peoples Jesus Christ is your best friend.

CHORUS

//If I've Jesus, Jesus only,

I have everything I need,

He is my Friend//

2

He's a rose from Sharon giving off sweet fragrance,

And the lily of the valley, He;

For the soul who is so sad and in affliction,

He exhales the sweetest perfume full of love.

3

He's compassionate, benign, tender, and loving,

He is slow to anger, quick in love;

With His love He raises up all broken-hearted,

Christ is the only one who knows how to love.
