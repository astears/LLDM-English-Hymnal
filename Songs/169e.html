169 THE KING OF GLORY

The King of glory is now calling,

Humanity to salvation;

All of His blessing He's now shedding,

For plenty health and holiness.

CHORUS

Oh come to the King Jesus Christ,

The one who died to give us light, salvation too.

2

All of you souls who are lost in this world,

Who are now longing liberty;

The King of glory now awaits thee,

So that you taste His goodness too.

3

God with His word is calling mankind,

They should obey in holiness;

And being baptized in His great Name,

And purified in His great word.

4

Christ is the pathway for all mankind,

He is the truth He is our life;

Eternal father and divine love,

Through everlasting times to come.
