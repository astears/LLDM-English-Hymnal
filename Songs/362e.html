362 MY LORD AND KING

My Lord and King is very rich,

He left this earth and a talent He left me;

He said that one day He would return,

And not to be lazy, but be working for Him.

CHORUS

Oh Lord bless the talent which you have given to me,

//And multiply it Lord// let your holy name be always sanctified;

And allow my faith to grow.

2

What joy I have because I received,

It was given to me the day I was baptized;

I was also told that my faults were erased,

And with His blood He came to rescue my soul.

3

And now that I'm free to fulfill my job,

And work on the talent that Jesus gave to me;

I will build upon that spiritual house,

My joy will always be to exalt His Name.

4

United to my brethren I will always be,

Because we are kin of the author of faith;

I will use the talent that Jesus gave to me,

So that when He returns He may find faith in our souls.
