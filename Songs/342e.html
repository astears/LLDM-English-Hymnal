342 GOD GIVES ME REST

God gives me rest through sending faith;

O'er there in mansion of peace;

It saves me through my Jesus Christ,

Like this, I happily go.

CHORUS

Faith reviving my soul,

I cannot deny you;

You're a gift so blessed of Lord Jesus Christ,

For your faithful flock.

2

Faith relieves, comforts, all my soul,

Up there in all eternity;

O'er there in heaven I will sing,

Honor and glory give.

3

Your love will increase more my faith,

My being will have rest in you;

I'll contemplate your face up there,

And joyful I will live.

4

Believing always by their faith,

The church of Jesus Christ does walk;

Fighting all evilness with good,

And for their leader waits.
