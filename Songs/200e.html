200 YOUR AN APOSTLE OF GOD

Your an Apostle of God, by the order of the Lord,

So that you guide the Lord's people, that the Lord has given you;

When Aaron your father slept, he had left you with an order,

//May you guard all of God's people, until Jesus comes again//

2

We remember the past moment, when we were greatly afflicted,

We thought that we'd be alone, without a Servant of the Lord;

But my God who is so loving, sent a blessing to His flock,

//We are joyful in our beings, to see Jesus shine in you//

3

We have promised our dear Lord, to be faithful till the end,

We remember all of the counsels, that his servant left us here;

With our hands raised towards the heavens, we promise to serve you Lord

//And have promised to be faithful to gain blessed salvation//

4

You're a Servant of the Lord, your place is special from God,

Because the Lord did give you, and He did see in your heart;

We are all here reunited, also listening to your counsels,

//We will guard them in our hearts, and walk in them forever//
