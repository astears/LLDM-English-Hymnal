278 OH LORD MY KING

Oh Lord my King, my soul gives thanks and praises to you,

Because you live in light so bright we've never seen;

The years pass by and you are always everlasting,

//And to mankind you show yourself always unseen//

2

The heavens tell of all the greatness of your glory,

The sun, the moon, and the entire firmament;

The years pass by and to you everything is present,

//You've given mankind understanding in their minds//

3

The mountains tremble with your great force and your power,

The sea subsides along with your ordering voice;

The years pass by and you are always still the same,

//You call the luminaries, also the Aquilon//

4

You judge mankind in this world filled with wretched misery,

Because they do not understand your perfect mandates;

You reprimand them and you punish them with death here,

// But one day near you'll liberate all of the faithful//
