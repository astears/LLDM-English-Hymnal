056 REJOICE OH SOUL

Rejoice oh soul and praise His name, with fervent voice acclaim,

Lift up and rave His wondrous name with vehement heart and praise;

Repay Him not with ungratefulness nor without mindfulness,

For He forgives your wickedness,

He saves and gives you health.

CHORUS

//Praise Him// with sweet songs of love,

//Praise Him// the King of kings above;

Oh all ye angels that He has formed and do His will with heart,

Oh all ye works that He has formed come celebrate His name.

2

With goodness filled your heart will be, with youthfulness as well,

Our souls shall always be relieved, when you rid our distress;

His anger may be delayed to come, but quickly comes His love,

He doesn't want to punish us nor does His fury last.

3

As high as God has formed the skies from this world that's so cruel,

This way He's also given love to all the faithful ones;

And so as far away as we see the east and the west,

All my transgressions have been placed so far away from me.

4

God remembers how we were formed, remembering we're dust,

The days of man are as the grass; which withers by the night;

But He from age to age will give His endless love to us,

To those who keep His covenant, remembering His laws.
