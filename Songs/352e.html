352 MY GOOD JESUS ASCENDED

My good Jesus ascended to dwell,

To the land that He went to prepare;

He is now seated at the right hand,

Of our Father for eternity.

CHORUS

Christ has ascended to His throne, reigning with the angels high,

He is interceding for us up in heaven;

He will descend to us, to take his church with Him,

Because we all now belong to Him.

2

You are a fountain flowing with love,

You have promised that while you're up there;

You will send your Spirit to us,

So that he may be with us on earth.

3

You have made all the lame ones to walk,

You have made all the deaf ones to hear;

And the mute ones were able to speak,

You gave sight to those who were blind.

4

So this day we give thanks to you Lord,

For the manifestations you give;

You're the one who can come heal our wounds,

You're the one who forgives all the souls.
