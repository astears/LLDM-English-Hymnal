301 THE WIFE OF THE GOOD LAMB

The wife of the Lamb Jesus is preparing,

Only to come to her Lord, her bridegroom, and Savior;

He's coming soon with His holy ones in the clouds,

To take at last the Church which He bought with blood.

CHORUS

This is the Church which belongs to Jesus,

It was once hidden within in the desert;

Now she appears with such splendor,

Just like a powerful squadron;

So she may find her loving Lord,

Who has bought her with His holy love.

2

Dressed in pure white and in magnificent vestments,

Which God himself gave to her, holy spiritual vestments;

He has sealed her with His mighty Holy Spirit,

He will take her to His heavenly kingdom.

3

The triumphant will be shining up in glory,

And like the sun they'll be seen throughout all eternity;

And the good Lord Jesus Christ will give to her,

Eternal life and a crown He'll place on her.

4

The return of the Lord is now approaching,

Also His church which is now, overflowing with His love;

And holiness will be her strong shield forever,

And she will dwell in the city of Zion.
