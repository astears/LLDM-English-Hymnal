304 THE LAMP THAT SHINES

The lamp that shines my pathway is,

The Bible from my God;

Fountain in which your burning thirst,

Will be satisfied, quenched.

CHORUS

Beautiful light, always will be my guide,

Until with Christ, in the heavens I'll be.

2

The food for my soul surely is,

The peace sent down to me;

A faithful guide and missive too,

Of the heavenly realm.

3

The testimony is of Christ,

Revealed to us by God;

Without it no one has the light,

Nor will salvation gain.

4

If I could only comprehend,

Your will oh my dear Lord;

In God's words let us put our faith,

Observe well all His laws.
