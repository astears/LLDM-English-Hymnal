247 THERE IS A PATH

There is path that has been unknown to this world,

But it's a pathway I was able to find;

//In Christ I know that my soul can have salvation,

I know that I can be saved only through Him//

2

In this pathway filled with so many struggles,

But it's this pathway Christ left to march behind;

//It was transformed into such a happy moment,

When I was rescued by my good Jesus Christ//

3

All of my relatives and all of my friendships,

These were the people that I was with one day;

//They mock me now because of the name of Jesus,

When they found out that I gave myself to Christ//
