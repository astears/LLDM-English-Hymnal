479 BE COURAGEOUS

Be courageous now my brothers and sisters,

Working in the celestial work of God;

All of our efforts will one day be accomplished,

In all places we'll be hoisting up our flag.

CHORUS

Ultimately we will see all our brothers,

Being crowned for our holy works below;

And scattered all throughout the whole creation,

The great spiritual Christian work of God.

2

It is useless for all of Satan's legions,

To be putting up barriers in front;

And with gladness we'll continue on this path,

With Jesus the greatest General.

3

Not one tear should be shedding from our eyes,

Nor our voice will be put out by complaints;

We will fight on filled with enthusiasm,

Always battling the armies of evil.

4

Be courageous go forth and assemble,

And prepare yourselves for the final day;

As you guide the lost souls here unto Jesus,

Till that day when in His mansion we will reign.
