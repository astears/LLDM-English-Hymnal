269 CHURCH OF JESUS CHRIST

Church of Jesus Christ may you be now encouraged,

Do not fear may you never be embarrassed;

Enlarge all the spaces in your houses,

Because the Saint of Israel is your grand helper.

CHORUS

Pitiful church, you were fatigued, without comfort,

With His justice, the Lord Jesus rescued you;

If anyone conspires against you Church,

They will always fall to the ground.

2

Time ago the church was a lonely widow,

But you were restored, redeemed from God's mercy;

The Saint of Israel made a pact and said,

I won't be mad, says the Lord, never again.

3

He calls you, all the predestined so blessed,

The queens and all the maidens give you glories;

From the concubines you were sent my dove,

God has told you: "You are my dove, Israel."

2nd. CHORUS

Pitiful church, you were fatigued, without comfort,

But I will have compassion eternally;

For a time, time, and half time I left you,

But now with great love,

I have picked you up my dove.
