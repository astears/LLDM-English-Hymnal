474 SAVE US

Save us, oh God, we your servants,

Those with a victory, coming;

//Save us// our dear Lord God is with you.

CHORUS

Blessed you when you come in,

Blessed you when you go out;

All things you place your hands upon is holy,

//Blessing of God, Father, Lord//

2

May God's peace reign in all your hearts,

Those who have come to God's church;

//This grace// a gift from God in heaven.

3

Peace be with all of my brothers,

Those who have sanctified their souls;

//You Saints// through the power of Jesus.

4

With a holy kiss we greet you,

This through the Lord's Holy Spirit;

//His Love// is a virtue from heaven.
