420 ONLY FOR YOU MY LORD

Only for you my Lord I give my soul,

Only for you Redeemer of love;

Only you have returned to me calmness,

My delight is in your word of truth.

CHORUS

I'll contemplate that precious day of glory,

In which I have to rejoice with my God;

Singing to Him songs filled with happiness,

Enjoying there His eternal love.

2

Through the path you have marked us to follow,

With your blood, with your torment, and pain;

This church that you have rescued now follows,

To rejoice endlessly in your home.

3

On this earth I am only a pilgrim,

Till I see you my loyal Savior;

On that day when all pain and all torment,

Will no longer exist where you dwell.
