180 ON THIS GREAT MEMORABLE DAY

On this great memorable day,

We have all heard your sweet voice;

As we all come to salute thee,

Anointed of our Lord.

CHORUS

Oh Anointed, elected Servant,

Here in your presence we witness;

What with your hands you have labored,

All the hardships that you have carried;

They have not been trampled over,

You have been blessed by God.

2

All the false Christians ignore,

He who has elected thee;

Your congregation does know this,

It was revealed by our God.

3

Just as the wise men had journeyed,

Bringing gifts for their dear King;

So does the Church now give offerings,

Because you are our King.

4

This humble offering we give thee,

And we all ask for your prayer;

May you plead in all our favor,

You are the friend of our God.
