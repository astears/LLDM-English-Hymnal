412 I HEAR THEM SAY

I hear them say that further more there is a good home,

This is the place where all the souls can always dwell;

Death will not have an entrance ever in that good home,

Only with the good Lord our God everything is peace.

CHORUS

//I want to be there//

I want to see Christ my Savior and dwell with Him;

//I want to be there//

And to be praising Jesus Christ is what I want.

2

I cannot cease in saying that only Christ can save,

He brings the lost ones from their darkness to His great light;

He will give peace to all the lost ones during the storm,

He will exchange their pain for songs in His great love.

3

Happily I am walking through my tribulations,

Because I know I will see Lord Christ Jesus above;

A place where ages after ages, world without end,

I will give praises zealously to my God with harp.
