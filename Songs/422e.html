422 PEACE GRACE AND LOVE

Peace, grace, and love is what we will partake in,

The Servant of God, by our Jesus Christ;

On this day of much joy, the children of light,

We give a kiss of love;

Glory to God for His grace, He who gave you liberty.

CHORUS

The Lord has given you a crown,

He gave you a valiant people;

So they may keep on announcing,

The gospel of our Jesus Christ.

2

A man of war you are and you've won the battle,

By your faith in our Jesus Christ;

He's the one who raised Thee, as a bright torch,

So that you may be shining;

As you're teaching of God's truth, to all of humanity.

3

Christ has given you the power over this earth,

That you may free souls stranded in the dark;

So you may give them life, through Jesus Christ,

The almighty Savior;

With much fervor we will sing, giving glories to the Lord.
