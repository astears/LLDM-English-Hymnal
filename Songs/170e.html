170 JESUS CHRIST IS COMING

Jesus Christ is coming on a cloud now,

On His throne we all will see His face;

He comes surrounded by His holy angels,

To lift up all those who suffered for Him.

CHORUS

Oh how joyful to hear the voice of Jesus King,

When He tells me to enter my faithful friend;

The kingdom now is ready made of pure love,

Enter my faithful friend!

2

This day is approaching very quickly,

When all peoples will see Christ the Lord;

He will appear, He's the King of kings,

He will appear with glory and majesty.

3

The church of the Lord is now attiring,

'Cause they all know how to please their Lord;

They will be dressing with the whitest vestments,

To accept Christ, their Lord and their King.

4

Just as a servant faithful to Jesus,

He who tells you what you should now do;

Look at Jesus who comes to lift all His own,

And give full payment to this unfaithful world.
