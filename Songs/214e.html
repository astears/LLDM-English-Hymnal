214 'TIS CHURCH OF THE LIVING GOD

'Tis the Church of the living God,

Pillar and ground of truth;

Day and night she's been preparing,

For her Savior and her King,

Without blemish, stain, or wrinkle;

And with Christ someday she'll rise.

CHORUS

The Lord's justice is apparent,

With the faithful of His flock;

If we walk always obeying,

Our dear Savior Jesus Christ.

2

In the kingdom of the heavens,

In the city of our God;

No one who defiles or lies,

Shall enter in the Lord's Kingdom;

For this reason, all His church,

Cleansing all, its heart and soul.

3

Oh you Church of the living God,

The pillar and ground of truth;

Like a torch your light burns on,

Through this dark and wicked world;

Walking onward in obedience,

Preaching humbleness and truth.

4

Jesus promised all His people,

To give them the crown of life;

His redeemed Church will receive it,

As it serves in holiness;

He awaits us in His glory,

Where we will rejoice with Him.
