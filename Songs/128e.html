128 OH HOW GREAT IS THE LOVE

Oh how great is the love that Jesus Christ has given me,

And to this place I come, to bless from Mount Zion the King;

There forever we'll reign, within all of our hearts,

And to this congregation, you confirm it Lord.

2

Walking through all the thistles made your feet bleed when you stepped,

He had converted myrrh, into perfume, with love from his heart;

Permit me to be living, with your love my dear Lord,

Consider me to be Lord, victorious through cruel pain.

3

Take away all my hate; don't let my love to dissipate,

All my anxieties, He calms with love, also my pains;

May my soul leave to see, the God who formed my soul,

As an offender freed, from bitter and cruel pains.

4

With my heart I watched but; without compassion I had slept,

The sprinkle of dew was, absorbed in your, hairs on your head;

Then the guards had found me, they took away my song,

I entreat with the damsels about Jerusalem.
