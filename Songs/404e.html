404 OH WHAT HAPPINESS

Oh what happiness I feel in my soul,

Because my faith's been founded in the Lord's word;

No winds, no storms will come to separate me,

Because the love of Jesus is in my soul.

CHORUS

What love, holy love, that cured my sickness,

Of my soul that wandered aimlessly in this world;

But today if I endure for Jesus Christ,

I'll have eternal life if meek and humble.

2

The messengers on this earth are like water,

They quench all of the droughts preaching the Lord's word;

They speak of Christ the one who gives salvation,

The word received will give them peace in their souls.

3

Many homes without peace are among us,

And bitterly they're weeping in this sad life;

Although they tried to leave their sins and come,

Their souls were captive by the adversary.

4

Now Christ Jesus with power has ascended,

Leaving behind the enemy defeated;

With love He is now saying "Come with me!"

For only I am the truth and the pathway.
