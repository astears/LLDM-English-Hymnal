491 LET THE SUNSHINE IN

Do you fear the foe will in the conflict win?

Is it dark without you- darker still within?

Clear the darkened windows, open wide the door,

Let a little sunshine in.

CHORUS

//Let a little sunshine penetrate//

Clear the darkened windows, open wide the door;

Let a little sunshine in.

2

Does your faith grow fainter in the cause of love?

Are your prayers unanswered by your God above?

Clear the darkened windows, open wide the door,

Let a little sunshine in.

3

Would you go rejoicing in the upward way?

Knowing naught of darkness, dwelling in the day?

Clear the darkened windows, open wide the door,

Let a little sunshine in.

4

O that my ways were steadfast in obeying You,

The blood of Christ would be enough to cleanse my soul;

Filling our hearts with lucid grace and peace,

Let a little sunshine in.

5

The Holy Spirit shines our hearts with light and truth,

It makes us strong in struggles, and guides us through;

Open up your heart and let Him dwell within,

Your eternal light He'll be.
