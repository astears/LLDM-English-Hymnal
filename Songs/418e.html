418 OUR FATHER

Our Father who art in the heavens,

On my knees I am now at your feet;

I'm imploring and asking towards heaven,

That you come let me feel you again.

CHORUS

It's so beautiful to feel your presence,

Enough to sing, to shout and to cry;

//And to sing glory, amen, Hallelujah,

And to glorify your name this way//

2

If I cry as I'm feeling your presence,

It's not sadness nor pain that I feel;

It's a joy that I feel deep in my soul,

Which the Lord sends us from up above.

3

It's so beautiful to feel your presence,

It is bliss when we pray without cease;

When we feel that the power is falling,

When in tongues I begin to implore.
