267 WE INVITE YOU NOW MY BROTHER

We invite you now my brother,

To go to Zion too; To the city of the King,

Where my God is dwelling high.

At the right hand of the Father,

By the Lord my God of love;

// There He is receiving souls,

Souls that His Father gave Him//

CHORUS

Desired City, oh what blessed glory,

Where all the saints, can rejoice in your sweet presence;

Hear all the crying, of those that implore here,

//Give us your hand oh my Lord, so that we may reach your glory//

2

Do not stay behind my brother,

Hearken to the voice of God;

Can't you see that Christ invites us,

To Zion, the great city.

Where a great banquet is waiting,

That was earnestly prepared;

//For the conquerors to enter,

To the weddings of the Lamb//

3

Oh my brother don't disdain,

The calling of the Lord;

See how Christ invites us all,

Towards the city of Zion.

To enjoy a holy banquet,

That was earnestly prepared;

// For the conquerors to enter,

To the weddings of the Lamb//
