050 ALTHOUGH IN THIS LIFETIME

Although in this lifetime, I have not a fortune,

I know up in glory, a palace is mine;

I was like a lost soul, in distress and hardship,

But Christ Jesus my Lord, empathized with me.

CHORUS

//Further than the sun// I have a grand home,

My home, lovely home, //Further than the sun//

2

And if in this world, I walk through the darkness,

Temptations surround me, many struggles too;

But I hear my Savior, calling unto me,

Only He will save me, and take me back home.

3

To all of the nations, of the human lineage,

Jesus wants to give them, word of salvation;

And a palace also, not built up by hands,

That He has prepared us, in His mansion high.
