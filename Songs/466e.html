466 I WOULD LIKE TO SEE JESUS CHRIST

I would like to see Jesus Christ my precious Savior Lord,

See the peace of my Lord Master, who for me His life He gave;

I would like to see the heavens and the throne where my God sits,

I will live in this countryside of peace eternally.

CHORUS

I would like to see that country, where no darkness ever reigns,

I want to be in the dwellings of Christ Jesus my Lord.

2

Oh I want to give to Jesus, without holding back my love,

Oh I want to give the message to the sinners of the world;

Conducting them to heaven through the cross of Jesus Christ,

Where forever for all ages with the angels I will live.

3

The redeeming work of Jesus which has freed me from my sins,

I want to live for the ages where I'll be glad in His love;

Through the blood of Christ my Savior though unworthy I receive,

With the redeemed troops of Jesus I give praise to God on high.
