431 FOR ME THE SAVIOR

For me the Savior gave His life,

I want to love Him more,

Christ Jesus pardoned all my sin,

I want to love Him more.

CHORUS

///I want to love Him more///

Yes, more and more and more;

///I want to love Him more///

2

My Pastor he walks faithfully,

I want to love Him more,

From the vile tempter,

He frees me,

I want to love Him more.

3

He will never forsake His Church,

I want to love Him more,

His blessing He will give to them,

I want to love Him more.

4

Oh Prince of the eternal peace,

I want to love Him more,

In glory I will see His face,

I want to love Him more.
