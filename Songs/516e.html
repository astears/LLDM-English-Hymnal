516 ONLY JESUS CHRIST IN GOD

Only Jesus Christ in God, and no other does exist,

Nor in heaven, nor on the earth, nor the bottom of the sea;

Author of this universe, that which a man came to save,

And it is Him who's preparing, to come down and judge the world.

CHORUS

And the church of Jesus Christ which was established through His name,

To the one who wants salvation, the door is let wide open;

You may chose, there's many doors but the others will not prevail,

And if Jesus finds them open, He will close them in His wrath.

2

Many now deny Lord Jesus, and announce the trinity,

With their arrogance they speak lies, that in sum are vanity;

These are all the fraudulent preachers, that deceive humanity,

That do not adore Christ Jesus, in their Spirits and in truth.

3

His evangel is preached, to all human conscience here,

Those who will believe in Jesus, may another day not pass;

In this day there is salvation, that's established by the Lord,

If the Lord is calling to you, do not make your heart a stone.
