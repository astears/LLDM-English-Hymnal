181 THOUGH I AM LIVING

Though I am living poor in this world,

Nothing I have to give out;

To Jesus, King of all glory,

Because redemption He gave me.

CHORUS

When you are seen in the battle,

Claim with your heart to your Lord;

Ask Him for strength, also courage,

You'll conquer your enemies.

2

Be dressed with the proper armor,

That is the power of God;

So that you can have the triumph,

Through our prayers to God above.

3

I am a soldier of Lord Jesus,

I bring to fight, my shield too;

I've sharpened all of my arrows,

Of Lord Savior, Jesus Christ.

4

All of the saints of our dear God,

Hoping for our Captain Lord;

Extending His hand straight toward us,

So to our mansions we can go.
