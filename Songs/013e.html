013 OH MY SOUL COME AND PRAISE

Oh my soul, come and praise Him, the great Lord my God,

Glorify, and sing praise, to God of Israel;

Magnificent in holiness, dreadful in all praises,

He's the maker of all wonders, that is my Lord.

CHORUS

//I will sing// all my feats to the King,

//I will say// in the presence of God;

And my voice will reveal, Israel has been saved,

This was done by the hand of my God.
