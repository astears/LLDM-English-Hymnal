009 'TIS NOW MY DEAR LORD

'Tis now my dear Lord that I dwell at your side,

The sadness has fled far away from my heart;

My cup's overflowing with all of your blessings,

That is why I praise you my God and my Lord.

CHORUS

Please guide me oh Jesus keep guiding me,

Please guide me my Lord //close to you// keep guiding me;

May evil not keep me away from your side.

2

How precious the path that you left us to follow,

There's no other road that will lead us to peace;

You are life eternal, oh fountain divine,

Oh glorious footsteps of truth and of life.

3

My soul is enjoying your heavenly grace,

With great happiness we all honor your name;

Your love has fulfilled me, your love satisfies me,

And this is the work of a Servant of God.

4

My longings' to be at the celestial palace,

Where sadness and pain will all come to pass;

There will be no crying, there will be no death,

That is where the Servant of God now resides.
