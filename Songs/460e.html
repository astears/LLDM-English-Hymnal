460 I WANT TO TAKE THE CROSS

I want to take the cross, I said to Christ,

The cross that you've prepared for me;

Joyfully I would like to praise your Name,

Leaving behind the things I chose.

CHORUS

"Take up your cross and follow me,"

Said Jesus the most loving Savior;

How could I disregard the tender voice,

Of my kind Redeemer?

2

The cross with its despises I will take,

To where the Lord has ordered me;

Joyfully with my brothers I will tell,

The precious story of His love.

3

Crucified I will live now to the world,

Loyal as a disciple is;

A golden crown He'll place upon my head,

When my rough battles come to pass.

4

I have a balance due with my Savior,

From when the day I was baptized;

To speak to men of your salvation here,

Which Jesus on the cross fulfilled.
