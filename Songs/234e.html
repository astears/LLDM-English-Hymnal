234 AT YOUR SIDE I'VE BLESSINGS

At your side I've blessings, prosperity and peace,

Through you my beloved, guide my heart to you;

Before your presence in your love I'm joyful,

My prize is your sweet rest under your wings.

CHORUS

//Oh Jesus my King, guide my heart you,

My greatest hope is to see your face someday//

2

Blessed are those who dwell, in your house forever,

Each and every moment, strength you give to them;

Before your altar I will sing your glory,

And through the good pathway, you will carry me.

3

If through all my hardships, my heart becomes saddened,

You almighty Lord can free my heart from them;

And although I walk in a pathway of burdens,

Someday to your glory, you will carry me.

4

For the Lord God is a sun and a shield,

For those who walk blameless, with integrity;

Blessed is the man who trusts in God the Father,

For someday in heaven, they will see His face.

5

Better is one day, in the courts of God our Father,

Than a thousand days elsewhere without the Lord;

It is better one day, in your faithful service,

Than in worldly pleasures, darkness, and in sin.
