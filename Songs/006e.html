006 MARCHING ONWARD GREAT SOLDIERS

Marching onward great soldiers of Jesus,

With the Emblem of God lifted high;

The triumph and victory are waiting,

//Let's proclaim// all the truth.

CHORUS

Oh you soldiers march onward with Jesus,

Never fearing the sword of the warrior;

For the grace of our Lord is before us,

And the general awaits our arrival.

2

All you valiant lift up all your banners,

And the sword of our God take in hand;

At the end of the war comes sweet victory,

Which, you'll gain through your sanctity here.

3

All these troops that are marching with Jesus,

To our mansion He went up to prepare;

It is Jesus who's guiding our footsteps,

And in heaven He'll give us a crown.
