272 ISRAEL AN AWE-INSPIRING

Israel an awe-inspiring church,

Israel is a powerful army;

Because He who is guiding her footsteps,

It is Jesus our King the most glorious.

CHORUS

Israel confide within your heart,

In your faithful, loving King;

Great victories He will deliver,

He'll conquer your enemies.

2

God's soldiers walk unfaltering,

God's soldiers also stupendous;

'Cause the One who is guiding is mighty,

This is Jesus the King, most courageous.

3

Israel continue valiantly marching,

Israel continue praising our Jesus;

Because He's triumphant o'er evil,

Jesus Christ the King of all glory.

4

//Hallelujah// to the Lamb,

He's our guardian, protecting from evil;

Hallelujah to the King of kings,

Jesus Christ who gives us our victory.
