016 TO THE BATTLE WE'RE MARCHING

To the battle we're marching oh Christians,

Be attired ye with charity and faith;

Like a sword we are holding the Bible,

Now's the time for the Lord's liberty.

CHORUS

Oh you Christians, the torches of this world,

Raise your flag for a symbol of the truth;

Triumphant ye will be through Jesus,

Ye will enter into His eternal rest.

2

All the legions in white are waiting,

And the harvest abounds more and more;

Let us rise our eyes up to heaven,

In demand of the fire of the Lord.

3

To the battle we're marching, oh Christians,

'Cause so quickly the time's passing by;

We will be reaping what our Brothers,

With their sweat and their blood they have sown.

4

Life eternal's a gift from Christ Jesus,

To those leaving their bad habits behind;

For obedience we will have in heaven,

All the dwellings He's went to prepare.

5

Let's be faithful to all His commandments,

Because Christ in a cloud will come again;

Up above in the clouds with flames of fire,

He will carry His flock up to rest.
