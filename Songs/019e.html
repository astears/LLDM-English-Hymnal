019 WHEN I LOOK UNTO THE HEAVENS

When I look unto the heavens and the earth,

When I consider all the scenery of the sea;

In the evenings when I see those bright stars shining,

And in the mornings when I hear birds sweetly sing.

When I see the shining sun over the hill tops,

I then marvel of my God's creative hand;

//And I must say to the world with all my soul;

None like my God, not one, no other God//

2

Many men worship their idols crafted from wood,

Gods made of gypsum and those also made of brass;

Yet they cannot see, nor hear you, and cannot speak,

Though they have feet, these images can't walk at all.

But my great God has His great throne high in the heavens,

My God lives and He can hear my daily prayers;

//In the presence of my God the world does tremble;

None like my God, not one, no other God//
