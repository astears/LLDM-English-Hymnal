550 LET US WORK

Let us work for all the lost ones,

Without thinking of ourselves;

It's the work of the redeemed ones,

Who God will never forget.

CHORUS

From the heavens harvest then;

You'll be reaping joyfully;

Because God does give His payment,

For the works of justice here.

All the seedlings of the truth;

In the heavens you will reap.

Sowing always in His truth;
