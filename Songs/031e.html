031 AS OUR LIFE HERE IS OVER

As our life here is over, living forevermore,

Free from all anguish of spirit, and no more sorrow here;

While we rejoice in His presence, oh what delight t'will be,

Seeing the beautiful city and Christ our Lord and King.

CHORUS

Glory! Oh how will it be, as we meet our King,

When my Jesus will say: enter my faithful friend;

And forever with Him, into eternity,

We'll be living in consolation with Christ my Lord.

2

Dwelling with Jesus our Savior, oh what delight will be,

Seeing His splendor and grace, feeling assured and safe;

No longer we will be tempted, all through eternity,

Since we'll be gathered in glory, with God and our Lord.

3

My sweetest hope is my Jesus, for He saved me from sin,

Eternal life He will give me, to glory I will go;

And when I'm with my Lord Jesus, what deepest joy will be,

Since no more darkness or shadow, will overcome me there.

4

Sweet mercy I now can cherish, since I was saved by grace,

Jesus is just like sweet honey, to glory I will go;

I will march forward to heaven with Jesus Christ my Lord,

He is my peace and my refuge, He is my Savior King.
