007 I PRAISE THE LORD GOD

I praise the Lord God with my spirit, deep within my heart,

Because He has filled all my soul with, blissfulness and peace;

I'll sing, and I'll serve, and I'll praise Him, with solemnity,

Because He is guiding, my life and my footsteps, to eternity.

CHORUS

All my soul sings to Jehovah,

Because in Him only, my soul has found peace;

I lift up, this song to Jehovah,

Because being holy, He made me holy.

For the Lord are all my sweet songs,

Which are full of dreams, with integrity;

So for Him I want to live,

Because He has promised to dwell in me.

2

Rejoicing I walk in the pathway, because I'm with God,

By faith I can see in the heavens, His beautiful home;

I hear by my side that His voice, comes to comfort me,

And blissful and joyous, I see His great glory, deep within my heart.

3

The young soul who walks insecurely, to eternity,

In God he will find self-assurance, and security;

Because He wants to save all the souls, in their youthfulness,

And yes He can save them, if they come to Jesus, with solicitude.
