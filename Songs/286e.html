286 JESUS IS MY REFUGE

Jesus is my refuge, in every tempest shore,

He is the firm rock of the eternity;

The wind shall never move me, nor will it trouble me,

//For Jesus is my refuge// I shall not be in need.

2

The turbulent waves, they rush against me,

And the obscure nights, they start to cover me;

My boat is navigating, the port it's reaching now,

//For Jesus is my refuge// I shall not be in need.

3

Jesus is the friend, of all humanity,

He is the medicine of every sickness too;

I shall not fear the battle, for He will guide me through,

//For Jesus is the friend// of all humanity.

4

Jesus is now calling, He is the captain now,

If you're a faithful soldier, prepare yourself today;

He calls you to the battle, oh come to defend him,

//For Jesus is now calling// His voice is saying come.
