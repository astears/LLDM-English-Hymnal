290 I WAS LOST WHEN JESUS CHRIST FOUND

I was lost when Jesus Christ found me,

He told me to come to the light;

And now He has also permitted,

That I take up His cross.

CHORUS

I shall look upon that radiance from above,

You can catch a glimpse right now;

A luminary that shines,

In the face of Jesus Christ.

2

Then we shall sing so very happy,

To Jesus King of glory;

And now He has also permitted,

That we take up his cross.

3

Then we continue working harder,

The harvest soon shall begin;

If we do not become discouraged,

He'll give us all our crowns.

4

For when you find yourself in heaven,

Singing to Jesus our king;

And there you shall give your praises,

For freedom He gave you.
