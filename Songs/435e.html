435 PEOPLE OF GOD

People of God make sure you're not left behind,

Because of you a nation could be condemned;

//Take care of all that our God has given you,

And in His mansion you all will have your place//

CHORUS

Let us take all of our bibles without fear,

These are the swords that will give us victory;

From all the legions who come to us fearless,

But we know with Jesus Christ we will defeat.

2

Work faithfully in the work of our Lord,

As faithful soldiers you'll get through all your toils;

//If you do this you'll be able to possess,

Eternal life that Christ has promised to us//

3

Together brethren let us fight for the work,

That Jesus Christ has entrusted with us here;

//Because the times are drawing nearer each day,

Of when we all should be awakened at His voice//
