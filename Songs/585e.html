585 I AM NOW SAVED

I am now saved, through you my Christ, with all your pow'r,

Oh what a joy I feel in me, when I recall;

That through your bloodshed from your flesh, upon the cross,

One day I'll be with you in that celestial place.

Enjoying your wonderful love in plentitude,

For this I come kneeled at your feet to shed my tears;

My tears of love and gratitude spilled from my heart,

Knowing that all my guilty sins you have erased.

CHORUS

I cry with gratitude, with running tears of love,

What can I offer you? Only my heart and soul;

What can depart me Lord, from your beloved flock?

Do guard my soul from sin, my divine Savior Lord.

2

Oh Jesus Christ, my sweetest Friend, I adore you,

Kneeled at your feet, I come to bring a sacrifice;

An offering full of my love, and full of peace,

For what you suffered for my soul, oh my good Lord.

All of my happiness I show to you my King,

Because my faults you have forgiven with your love;

Take then my life and all my being, for they're yours,

Accept this song in which I sing from all my heart.
