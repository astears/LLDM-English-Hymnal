472 THERE'S SALT TO SOW

There's salt to sow, sower of God's peace,

Follow the steps of Jesus Christ;

Many ripe fruits you will have seen grow,

Follow the pathway of light and peace.

CHORUS

///Go/// sowing seed, //Go// sowing His peace, Where ever speak of your dear

Lord, and of His holy peace.

2

The land's enormous, there's salt to sow,

Sow on the land that God gives you;

If you are sowing, trusting in God,

All of your labors the Lord will crown.

3

Oh do not despise the time and see,

Do sow God's word of light and peace;

Eternal seeds yielding plenty fruit,

Rich seeds of blessing that are not cheap.

4

It is God's order to plant the seeds,

News of salvation, life, and of peace;

Perhaps the cost will be many pains,

But in the heavens your soul will rest.

2nd. CHORUS

I, go, I go my Lord, I go sowing your peace,

Speaking always of you Lord, and of His holy peace.
