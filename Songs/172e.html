172 THE TIMES ARE QUICKLY ADVANCING

The times are quickly advancing,

The Lord's return is now closer;

This flesh cannot take advantage,

Jesus Christ is our light and life.

CHORUS

Let us never belittle, What we've received from Jesus;

For the great Lord in His kindness,

Has united all his people.

2

Let us get rid of envies,

All quarrels, dissensions, and hatred;

So that we may be with Jesus,

With our heart so clean and holy.

3

So dangerous are these times,

Even in this congregation;

Let us be ready and watchful,

Waiting for God's Holy Spirit.
