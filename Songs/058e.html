058 RAINS OF BLESSINGS

Rains of blessings of sanctity and love,

Joyfully unite together, with all the saints of the Lord;

Because on this day the Servants of God have arrived.

CHORUS

Welcome we give to the messengers of our God,

Who come with love this day, to proclaim His praise;

The church welcomes the chosen men, whom God has anointed,

To those who are elected, by Lord Jesus Christ.

2

God's church overflows with love on this day,

You Servants of the high God, are saluting everyone;

We are tasting God's communion, and His sanctity.

3

In this congregation we receive God's love,

We are lifting up our songs, for as one we sing to God;

He's the one who has received them, with honor and love.
