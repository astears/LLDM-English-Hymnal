250 THERE'S A PRECIOUS

There's a precious spring of life,

Of Emanuel's flowingblood;

Purifying everyone,

Who with Him unites their soul.

CHORUS

////If I am saved////

///By the precious blood of Jesus Christ///

By the blood of Jesus Christ.

2

Converted was wrong-doer,

Pending on the cross of death;

He saw the fountain; washed Himself,

Believing Jesus' blessed name.

3

And also me, my lost poor being,

Over there I washed myself;

The glory of your greatest pow'r,

I exalt your name with joy.

4

You're an eternal crimson fount,

A torrent of pure love;

Forever they'll be washed in you,

The people of the Lord.
