393 OH LOFTY GRACE

Oh lofty grace that comes from our God,

Grace that the messenger can convey;

When he goes planting in this dead world,

The living seed that's born from His love.

CHORUS

///Go/// planter go

/// Go/// Brother, go!

Go plant the living seed on earth, //go// planter go.

2

If your pathway seems so difficult,

Know that the promise of God is true;

With my right hand I'll be aiding thee,

I will be faithful in helping you.

3

If you're not asked to go preach the word,

You should still do what you can to help;

Your Father who's watching secretly,

He will be there to reward your deeds.

4

If you are humble as Ruth once was,

Go to fields and perform your task;

Surely you will find ones left behind,

That other workers forgot to sow.

5

Not only preaching and singing hymns,

Will save the souls from perdition here;

But he who lives in holiness,

Will compel others to come to God.

6

Plant in all places the love of Christ,

For every planter will have His prize;

Those who are planting with tears and love,

They will be reaping with love up there.

7

The time of sowing is ending soon,

Soon we'll be resting with Jesus Christ;

We will see the souls who have been saved,

Shining above just as precious jewels.
