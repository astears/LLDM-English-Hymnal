094 WITH TIME I HAVE ASKED YOU

With time I have asked you Lord, to bring into your flock,

All the souls that you wish to give redemption to;

And give them understanding, free them from suffering,

Any fierce grueling, anguish that may have to feel.

CHORUS

////Oh my Lord// may no one go astray and;

May we all be redeemed//

2

Confiding in your message I know I can prevail,

As I carry your gospel I know you'll make it known;

It's time that you be searched for, and man is saved from sin,

So man can gain salvation, and enter your mansion.

3

Extend to us your power, and let them feel you near,

I know you can lure them in, with all the strength you have;

I know that without you Lord, no one can see or hear,

They'll be sleeping so deeply, until you wake them up.

4

I beg you for me too Lord, may I be serving you,

My only God and Father; prolong my life for you;

I hope that you may hear this, my humble pleadings too,

May I always be serving with all my heart and soul.
