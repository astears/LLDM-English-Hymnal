080 AS THE PERFUME

As the perfume that gives off a pleasant aroma,

As the perfume dismisses such a precious scent;

Like the incense offered to Jesus our Savior,

Such is the prayer that is filled with great love and faith.

CHORUS

Faith is the proof, demonstration of,

All the things that we cannot see;

But we all know that when He's finally appearing,

We all will be as Christ is, Spirit just as He.

2

The pleasant incense for the offering is at hand,

It's in the hand of the angel that serves the Lord;

Because his people here have always been exhaling,

The hopeful prayers that smell as perfume to my Lord.

3

Elijah prayed so that a fire came from heaven,

It was through faith that Joshua halted the sun;

And Jesus Christ through faith performed the greatest marvels,

It was through justice that to heaven He arose.
