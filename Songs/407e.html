407 CLOSE TO THEE

Thou my everlasting portion,

More than friend or life to me;

All along my pilgrim journey,

Savior, let me walk with Thee.

CHORUS

////Close to Thee//// All along my pilgrim journey;

Savior, let me walk with Thee.

2

Not for ease or worldly pleasure,

Nor for fame my prayer shall be;

Gladly will I toil and suffer,

Only let me walk with Thee.

3

Lead me through the vale of shadows,

Bear me over life's fitful sea;

Then the gate of life eternal,

May I enter, Lord, with Thee.

4

Before me are my transgressions,

There's great misery in me;

Hide your face from my disgraces,

And blot out all of my sins.

5

Weak and anguished I'm imploring,

Lord have mercy on my soul;

All my faults and my transgressions,

All are laid out before you Lord.

6

In your everlasting mercy,

Have compassion on my soul;

And show mercy to your servant,

Giving peace and tranquility.
