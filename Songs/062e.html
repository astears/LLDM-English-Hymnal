062 BLESSED YOU ARE

Praise be to you, oh Lord our God,

God of Israel, God our Father;

From everlasting, Amen,

To everlasting, Amen.

•••••

It is yours, oh Lord God, all of the greatness and power,

And the glory majesty and splendor;

For everything and all things in heaven is yours,

Everything on earth is yours.

•••••

Yours, oh Lord God, is the kingdom,

You're exalted as head over all things and everything;

Wealth and honor come from you and are before you,

You are ruler of all things.

And in your hands are power and strength, you're a mighty fortress,

And in your hands is greatness, to exalt and give strength to all.

•••••

Now, our God, we give you thanks,

And praise your glorious name;

And we confess all things to your name,

Saying "But who am I, and who are my people?

That we should be able to give as generously as this?

Everything comes from you,

And we have given you only what we've received from your hands." Amen
