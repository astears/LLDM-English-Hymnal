252 THERE IS A TREASURE

There is a treasure stored for me up in the heavens,

That many sinners have been hoping to gain;

And walking through the many thorns, thistles, and needles,

A rich treasurer more than diamonds I will gain.

CHORUS

Although there's a dangerous storm disrupting my path,

That so cruelly does beat on my dear soul;

But I anxiously await to see the rainbow,

That's announcing forever a sweet peace.

2

It is a palace of great beauty I do seek,

And it is hidden past the bluish turquoise sky;

And it's adorned with shining stars, so very precious,

It is a treasure I am looking for my soul.

3

There is a precious Prince that's dwelling with my treasure,

And I am longing to know Him face to face;

Because with His blood my soul has been given freedom,

And my life He has changed with His mighty hand.

4

He is so loving, affectionate, and so lovely,

And in my trials I have tasted His sweet love;

And He helps me, and cheers me up when I am saddened,

Someday forever I will live with Him above.
