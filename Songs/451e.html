451 WHAT DOES IT MEAN?

What does it mean? /// This great rumor///

What does it mean? //This mad rush//

Who can one day, another too,

Move the crowds and touch their hearts?

The people shout in one loud voice,

Pass you Christ of Nazareth.

CHORUS

//Do pass//pass you Christ of Nazareth,

///Do pass/// my Lord.

2

Who is to say? ///This Jesus Christ///

That manifests with//such great pow'r//

Why when He passed through the city,

Did they anxiously crowd around?

After the peoples heard His voice said,

Pass you Christ of Nazareth.

3

Christ Jesus came ///to suffer here///

Anguish and zeal// tiredness and thirst//

And He gave comfort, peace and health,

How many would have suffered death;

For this the blind happily heard,

Pass you Christ of Nazareth.

4

And now He's coming ///The good Lord Christ///

Ready to make us//very good//

With love He calls our souls to hear,

To stay in Him and Him in us.

He's coming close, do you hear Him?

Pass you Christ of Nazareth.

5

Those who suffer///trials here///

Come close and rest and// peace you'll have//

Those walking so, so far away,

Of God His grace you'll surely gain;

If you are tempted He is near,

Pass you Christ of Nazareth.

6

But if His grace///you do reject///

His love looks on //with such disdain//

So sadly you will march behind,

In vain you will call unto Him;

But in the end will say a voice,

Pass you Christ of Nazareth.
