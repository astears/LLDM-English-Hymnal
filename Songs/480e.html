480 THE SHEEPFOLD IS FOUND PROTECTED

The sheepfold is found protected,

The horde is filled with calmness now;

But far in the cliffs without guidance,

Amidst evil one is alone.

Murkiness now covers the whole cliff,

The wandering one rests in a cave,

However the good Shepherd found her,

In His arms he returns it back home.

CHORUS

In the rough storm He is heard calling,

His blood He has shed on the path;

Praise Christ because He has now found her,

For I know that the lost lamb was I.

2

The wilderness bring her much terror,

The thistles make her poor feet bleed;

Serenely she follows her journey,

Defying death all around her.

Jesus manifests His anxiety,

When He sits to think of his lamb;

At last He prevails in His inquest,

And securely He brings her back home.

3

The prodigal boasts in her safety,

In her redeemer's arms she rests;

And with jubilation she cries out,

Oh see how fortunate I am.

The terrible night has now passed by,

The sun can be seen shining through;

He has come to rescue His lost lamb,

Once again she's returned to her home.
