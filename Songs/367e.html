367 MANY DEBTS I HAVE

Many debts I have with my Savior Lord,

Many debts I owe to Jesus Christ;

If I could pay Him for His favors here,

Instantly I would do it lovingly.

CHORUS

//What will I give?// I don't have much,

Because all of the riches are yours God;

//Also all my life// Only my being, my heart and soul,

I can give you, If you take it oh my loving Jesus;

//Then use it my Lord//

2

If sin shames infamously my dear soul,

If it's sweet deception attracts my being;

And my innocence is then taken away,

In disgrace my soul is consumed by sin.

3

A gold palace is what I'd like to give,

As a grateful gift to my Savior Lord;

But such things the Lord will never accept,

What God seeks is a great loving heart!
