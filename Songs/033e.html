033 I LIFT UP MY EYES

I lift up my eyes to the mountains ,

From where does my help come from?

My help comes from the Lord God,

The Maker of the heavens and the earth.

•••••

Jehovah will not let your foot slip,

He who watches you will never slumber;

Indeed, He'll neither slumber,

Nor will He sleep, He who watches o'er Israel.

•••••

The Lord watches over you,

The Lord is your shade at your mighty right hand;

The sun will not harm you in the daytime,

Nor will the moon at nighttime.

•••••

The Lord will keep you from any harm,

He will watch over your life;

The Lord will watch over your coming and your going,

Both now and forevermore, Amen.
