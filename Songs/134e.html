134 FROM THE EAST AND FROM THE WEST

From the east and from the west they will come,

People who did not know Jehovah God;

They will come and they'll worship the Lord,

They will be singing praises to Him.

CHORUS

Nations never heard of will come,

People that we've never encountered;

Faces we've never contemplated,

We'll see them praising Jehovah our God.

2

From all four continents they will arrive,

All those who will be chosen by the Lord;

These are the souls who once were found lost,

Now they bless their Redeemer today.

3

The Lord spoke through His prophets about,

The promise Abraham received from God;

I will make a great nation of you,

This is the blessing of our Lord.

4

All of us who have been blessed to know God,

Should always pay tribute to His name;

Because in His great kindness and love,

He made us to be children of His.
