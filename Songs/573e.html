573 ETERNAL LIFE

Eternal life //He promised all//

//That in Him// would just believe;

And he shall be there with my Lord,

For this is the announcement He gave.

CHORUS

//Come to the light that Jesus gives you//

And you shall live there with my Lord;

And you shall have ever lasting life.

2

Hallelujah //unto Him// //For His love//

He showed us all;

Because He would never lose,

What the Father gave unto His hands.

3

Time is coming //of great sorrow//

//When there'll be// no place to go;

Yet the Redeemer today,

To His people He has come to save.

4

Don't delay oh //sinner come//

//In coming// unto the Lord;

For tomorrow you shall cry,

For the shame that your errors brought you.
