389 OH HOW BEAUTIFUL

Oh how beautiful it is to dwell here,

When we are all in one;

Because our Jesus sends us life and He blesses us together,

And we are all one body here, united now by the Lord;

We are waiting for Him to come and take us.

CHORUS

When Christ comes all the people will then know,

That His children we are;

If we love one another with our hearts,

And if we can be faithful;

In the clouds He will return and He will take us with Him,

Thousands of years we'll be reigning with Jesus.

2

Jesus Christ does dwell at the right side of God,

In spirit and truth;

He is revealing and he's teaching only that which is God's truth,

Until the day we all can reach a perfection that's complete;

So that when he comes He can take us to His home.

3

Only one is our good Lord and God,

There is no name like His;

Only our Jesus who has been, is now, and who will always be,

This is why we can tribute praise, to our Savior and King;

As humans we are worshiping our God.
