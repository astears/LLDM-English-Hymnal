567 WE WILL CONQUER

We will conquer, yes we will conquer,

And arrive at our beloved home;

And we will be welcomed by Jesus,

And we will praise Him all day and night.

CHORUS

My King has soldiers in the millions up high,

We are determined to battle for Christ;

And at their side, they have a corner stone,

This is the Captain, Jesus Christ the Lord.

2

We'll be fighting, yes we'll be fighting,

With the help of Jesus Christ our Lord;

Triumphantly praising Jesus,

The Lamb who died nailed to the cross.

3

If at nighttime the stars shine brightly,

God's church will shine brighter than them all;

Because it has a luminary,

That is brighter than all light of day.

4

There are peoples trusting in fortune,

And the gods and goddesses they serve;

But the children of Jesus Christ,

Shine on brighter than the stars above.
