150 TWO PATHWAYS TO CHOOSE FROM

Two pathways to choose from, which one shall you take?

The time has arrived to choose the path you'll take;

Assuredly one pathway shall direct you to God,

However the other will distance you from Him.

CHORUS

//Which one shall you take?//

Two pathways to choose from,

Which one shall you take?

2

Two pathways to choose from of anguish and of light,

One path has the markings of Jesus Christ our Lord;

The other so ample, has no guider in front,

It guides towards death, in doubt and lots of pain.

3

Misfortune and pains has filled up the narrow path,

With burdensome crosses, and tough frugality;

But Jesus the Christ promised to help when we're down,

To those who are faithful they shall receive a crown.

4

Two pathways to choose from, choose quickly and do now,

'Cause if you do not, you may perish without choice;

If you choose the way leading to Christ Lord King,

And leave the erroneous, opposite of light.
