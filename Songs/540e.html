540 I HAVE A REFUGE

I have a refuge in the rock Jesus,

In tempest weather, it's very firm;

And Jesus Christ gives my soul His comfort,

And if there's danger, He gives me peace.

CHORUS

The rock's remaining, 'cause it's made firmly,

Although the tempest is blowing stronger;

My hiding place, He has reassured me,

It is a place I'll live some day.

2

Over this rock I've been building wisely,

For my salvation, I have great hope;

A people Jesus is now preparing,

To reach mount Zion a holy place.

3

If storm winds are heard viscously howling,

The men are dying from wickedness;

I will fear nothing, I have a refuge,

While I am walking up to my home.
