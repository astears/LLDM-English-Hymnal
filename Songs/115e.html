115 WHEN WE HAVE CEASED TO EXIST

When we have ceased to exist,

We'll go to a much better place;

We'll be blissful when we feel,

We won't return, never again;

Although I may suffer here,

I will be joyful with Him there;

I'll go to glory and not return,

Never will I return.

CHORUS

A faithful Servant of God, in heaven, Yes!

I will find, With those who will await for us, hoping to never return;

All things will be felicity, free from all anguish and all pain,

I'll go to glory and not return, never, will I return.

2

Joyful I am to meditate, That one day I'll be with Jesus;

That from this day my song will be, I won't return, never again;

That place could never be compared, Seeing the Apostle of the Lord;

He went to glory and will return, Yes, soon, he will return.

3

Not too far off the day will be, When we will sing with Jesus Christ;

A new celestial song we'll sing, We won't return, never again;

When I will walk through golden streets, With the anointed of the Lord; He lives in

glory, and will return, Yes, soon, he will return.
