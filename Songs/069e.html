069 MARCHING ONWARD

Marching onward, marching onward, to Mount Zion high above,

Where the King of kings is dwelling, Jesus Christ our Savior Lord;

Where the King of kings' residing,

Who has conquered all the world.

CHORUS

Celestial mansions, Zion of the Lamb,

The dwelling that Jesus, went up to prepare;

There He will welcome, those that He's chosen,

Those who were purchased, with redeeming blood.

2

Marching onward, and remembering

Jesus taught us with His word;

//Whosever wants to follow,

Take your cross and come to Me//

3

Marching onward, and remembering,

When the last supper He gave;

//All His bloodshed for the new pact,

Bought us all eternal life//

4

Marching onward, and remembering,

When with clarity He spoke;

//All of those who come to me,

They will live forevermore//
