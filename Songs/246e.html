246 THERE IS A LIGHT

There is a light illuminating darkness,

It is Christ's gospel that He came to impart;

Blessing the heart with peace and ridding it from evil,

All those who once were wandering have now been found.

CHORUS

It is the teaching of Christ our Savior Lord,

Brought from the heavens to fill our hearts with peace;

So that the wandering soul may find through Jesus Christ, Life,

Truth, Salvation, also liberty.

2

There is a light descending from the heavens,

Illuminating more than the sun of day;

Giving the sinner, peace, joy, comfort to their souls,

All those who once were wandering have now been found.

3

Your word to me is light guiding my pathway,

And in my journey your teaching lights my way;

It penetrates and rids the dross in minds and hearts,

All those who once were wandering have now been found.

4

I saw an angel coming down from heaven,

A great Apostle called by the God of love;

Holding a scroll with news to use in His great mission,

It was the gospel of Brother Aaron.
