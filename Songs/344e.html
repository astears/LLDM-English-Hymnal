344 MEDITATE, OH ALL YOU PEOPLES

Meditate oh all you people,

Meditate together whole earth;

From dusk on earth created,

And to dust returned one day;

God did say in the beginning.

CHORUS

He who believes in Jesus Christ,

In the memory of God he'll be;

And a new eternal life he'll have,

When we all arrive to Zion.

A new hymn we will sing in heaven,

Before the throne of the highest King;

And the Lord will guide my paths to Him,

When we all arrive to Zion.

2

Just as the herbs in the pasture,

That flourishes in the morning;

But as nighttime is approaching,

Its beauty does surely wither;

And everything disappears.

3

Our days are very fleeting,

They pass as a dream of nighttime;

Our lives are like a story,

Full of bitter trials and anguish;

Also sadness and of heartaches.
