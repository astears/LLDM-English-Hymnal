182 IN THESE TIMES

In these last times in which we are living,

Fiercely battling with anxious hearts;

Our mortal bodies are living as dead,

So we're worthy of eternity.

CHORUS

Let us fight, Brother, without a rest,

We'll be deserving eternity;

If we are wishing for joy above,

We should be walking in holiness.

2

In these last times that are filled with dangers,

We see the loss of lives everywhere;

God promised to help us be victorious,

So we inherit eternal life.

3

In these last times in which we are living,

Times of great darkness, insanity;

In these lasts days of much danger violence,

We should be fighting and not fainting.
