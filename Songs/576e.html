576 SAVIOR JESUS CHRIST CAME

Savior Jesus Christ came, only to suffer here,

So His blood would runneth over, for you and for me.

CHORUS

Perhaps tomorrow //There'll be no place//

Jesus Christ tells you //come don't delay//

The day is passing, the night is coming,

Perhaps tomorrow will be too late.

2

Jesus Christ is calling you; listen to His tender voice,

With effort He calls to you, saying "sinner come."

3

Don't wait for the new dawning, or say Jesus Christ won't come,

Perhaps in the morning, it will be too late.

4

When inside your casket lying, it will be too late to pray,

To implore to Christ Jesus, who now wants your soul.
