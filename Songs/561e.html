561 GRAIN SAND IN THE SEASHORE

Grain sand in the seashore, you are oh vile sinner,

But the Lord can make, grand temple out of you;

Grain sand on the seashore, to make God's own temple,

Where the Spirit of the Lord is dwelling more.

CHORUS

A grain on the sand shore,

I was in the desert,

A grain in the sand shore, my soul used to be;

But I have been called now, to a beautified temple,

Where the Spirit of the Lord is dwelling more.

2

Grain sand in the seashore, in the wide dry desert,

And so is the man, in the presence of the Lord;

A grain on the sea shore, has been formed a temple,

Where the Spirit of the Lord is dwelling more.

3

Now we are the temple, of God's Holy Spirit,

Faithful columns and the support of His truth;

Always in our mouths we'll, sing all songs of praises,

And with them we'll praise the name of Jesus Christ.
