494 IF YE BELIEVE IN GOD

If ye believe in God, serve then the Lord,

Come ye to the Lord, forgiveness ye ask;

Christ your Savior Lord, is calling ye to Him,

Ye who sins, stop, don't sin anymore.

CHORUS

Come don't fright, come soon to confess,

'Cause this world is full of trickery;

Jesus Christ, a new life He'll give to you,

This is if ye serve Him in truth and in light.

2

If ye believe the images can save,

Now power they have, this is sin to God;

Come ye to the Lord, ask Him for His grace,

Ye who sins, stop, don't sin anymore.

3

Hallelujah, praise, be unto God,

Let every human being, serve the Lord with fear;

Come ye to the Lord, ask Him for His grace,

Ye who sins, stop, don't sin anymore.
