604 I HAVE BEEN BAPTIZED IN THE NAME

I have been baptized in the name of Jesus Christ,

// In the name of Jesus Christ//

The Lord has made me Holy, by erasing all my sins,

Through His bloodshed down on the wooden cross.

CHORUS

Jesus Christ is the name which saves all souls,

I have confidence in all His pow'r;

So I carry on, faithful on the path,

Until I have arrived.

2

The name of Jesus is my Savior Lord's dear name,

Of His comfort He gives me, when my soul is down and blue;

I was wandering in the desert, lost in error and my sins,

He will shield me, forgive my soul from sins.

3

Christ is the beginning, alpha, omega, and the end,

He's the first and the last,

He's the one who will return;

Conforming to our actions,

He will have to judge our souls,

In that day when the

Lord will judge the earth.

4

Those who are faithful, who complete the will of God,

He will give them a reward, and a crown place on their heads;

Out of all His mighty greatness, of His grace He will give them,

In the Kingdom from times before they'll reign.

5

All those who are practicing iniquity and sin,

And a sentence He will give, in that anger of His;

Apart from me you wicked, all you workers of much sin,

To the fire prepared for you must go.
