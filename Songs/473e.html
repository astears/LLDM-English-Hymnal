473 VALIENTLY LEAVE

Valiantly leave, oh battalion of Israel,

Sing of the triumph, and the victory through faith;

Oh you the youth your seed is in Lord Jesus Christ,

Eternal rocks of all the centuries, it's so firm.

Oh burst and shout in voices loud to praise the Lord,

The evangel of God's love and justice too;

The message to the souls you will take to the world,

The souls are begging to be freed from Satan's yoke.

CHORUS

All the troops of Jesus Christ have been prepared for,

To fight strong against satan;

All the soldiers look so valiant when they're marching,

Holding up high all their swords.

Although bloodshed from the martyrs it has cost us,

This blood will be left shed o'er the fields;

In the trenches, it has been sealed,

The evangel will always be left.

2

Celestial shocks of all the angels of the Lord,

They will be help for all the soldiers of Christ;

When in the war Jehovah's justice does prevail,

Without cease going towards the front guard, they will go.

The light of dawning will be seen with clarity,

And with Jehovah in His glory I will go;

When you invoke Lord Jesus name,

He's always there,

For your response and love I'll go with you my Christ.

3

March on with valor to fight against all your foes,

Oh you the youth without vacillating, advance;

The voice of a warrior resounding in every place,

All of the demons and the devil will be vexed.

Detain the sun if you are ready to prevail,

Just as Joshua through grace and also faith;

Through his command the sun so soon obeyed his voice,

It is your duty to fight for Lord Jesus Christ.
