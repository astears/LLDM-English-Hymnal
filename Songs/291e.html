291 LORD CHRIST HAS GIVEN

Lord Christ has given my soul new life,

//How good He is// to you my friend,

He can also give; If you believe His word.

CHORUS

Flowing down was your blood,

Oh Christ, Flowing down the cross was your blood;

Always flowing from that fountain,

Guard me good Jesus Christ.

2

In my soul Christ's light has shined in me,

//How good He is//, also in your soul He can now shine;

If you kneel before Him.

3

He came to heal all the poor sick men,

//How good He is//, once I was sick but Christ has healed me;

I do give Him all praise.

4

Christ Jesus always will be my guide,

//How good He is//, also with you He can also be;

Whenever you're with Him.

5

You should always hear Christ Jesus' voice,

//How good He is//, in all things you should serve God on high;

He will light up your way.
