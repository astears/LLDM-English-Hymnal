261 HYMNS OF LOUDEST PRAISE

Hymns of loudest praise are sung in His glory,

Where the Son of God went to reign on high;

Melodies of love are exclaimed up there,

A great multitude in order to bless their faithful Lord;

That's Christ Jesus Lord.

CHORUS

He's coming, in a cloud He's coming,

And many trumpets will sound when He's coming;

Those who are sleeping in Jesus will be living,

Those who are living, will be transformed as Christ.

2

A strong voice is heard so joyful in the heavens,

Where the throne of God has been firmly placed;

Murmurs of the angels is heard on high,

In order to serve united they are to their faithful Lord;

That's Christ Jesus Lord.

3

Let's follow with love, following the pathway,

That Christ Jesus walked, with tears and shed blood;

We should all arrive to this faithful place,

In order for them to salute their God, who has rescued them;

From their wickedness.
