127 IN THAT MORNING

In that morning it will be so glorious,

When my Jesus the Savior will return;

All the nations united just as sisters,

We will welcome the Lord in His return.

CHORUS

We will never have the need for the splendor of the light,

Nor the sun will give its light, nor will it give us its heat;

There will be no crying there, no more sadness, no more pain,

For in that time Jesus the king of heaven;

He will give consolation to our souls.

2

We await for the glorious morning,

When we welcome the only God of love;

Where all things will be happiness and gladness,

In the most holy presence of the Lord.

3

All the Christians who are true and faithful,

And the missioner with courage and zeal;

And the Church, the beloved wife of Jesus,

They will be in the arms of our Lord.
