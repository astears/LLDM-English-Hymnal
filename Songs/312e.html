312 THE VICTORY IS GAINED

Victory is gained through Jesus, in the true church of the Lord,

Enemies are being conquered through our Savior Jesus Christ;

With jubilee, singing contently, On this day we bless God's name.

CHORUS

Just as the dawning grows in the morning,

So is the true Church of our Lord;

//Growing in works and grace abundantly,

Until perfect someday she will be//

2

Many are the concubines, who are waiting for His love,

Yet one dove belongs to me, said the wise King Solomon;

It's the true church of our Lord, Who awaits for Him in love.

3

It's the true church of our Lord, with two wings she flew away,

Into the desert where she was cared for, for a time, times, half a time;

But now she has reappeared, And rejoicing with her Lord.

4

You are beautiful my darling, most beloved of our Lord,

Dressed in garments of fine linen, waiting for Him to appear;

Like fine spices and a garden, Your aroma fills my heart.
