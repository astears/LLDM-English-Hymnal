144 GOD OF THE HEAVENS

//Oh my God// I seek earnestly in the morning hours,

My soul thirsts for you my Lord, my body longs for you;

In dry and weary land, where there is no water,

And beheld your power and your glory;

As I have seen you in the sanctuary.

•••••

Because your love and your mercy is better than life,

My lips will praise you;

I will praise you as long as I live,

And in your name, I will lift up my hands;

My soul will eat the richest of foods,

With singing lips my mouth will praise you.

•••••

And with my lips of jubilee,

I'll praise you with my mouth,

When I remember of you, on my bed;

When I think of you through the watches of the night,

//Because you my God are my help//;

I sing in the shadow of your wings, I sing so joyfully.

•••••

My soul clings to you my Lord God,

Your right hand upholds me highly;

And they who seek my life will be destroyed,

They'll go down to the depths, down into the earth;

They'll be given over to the sharp sword,

And become food for jackals.

•••••

//But the King will rejoice in the Lord God//

All who swear by God's name will praise His high name;

Because the mouths of liars will be silenced,

By the Lord God, Amen.
