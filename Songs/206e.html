206 I CHOSE LORD JESUS

I chose Lord Jesus when I am choosing the best,

He shows us the holy way and the most truthful path;

For that reason we are saying: That Jesus Christ is coming soon.

CHORUS

He will lift up all the faithful on the earth,

At the end of their careers, through His glory they will pass;

In the great day of the Lord.

2

In the path of Christ, let our steps march on behind,

Steps which He had marked when He had suffered for us;

And when Lord Jesus Christ comes down, all of His glory we'll see.

3

The general Jesus, to the saints who praise His name,

He will guide and carry us to His celestial place;

We will be attributing praise when arriving in glory.

4

There up in glory, when we're with Lord Jesus Christ,

Forever reigning with him, with the great Teacher and Lord;

Never will we think of this world, no never think of it again.
