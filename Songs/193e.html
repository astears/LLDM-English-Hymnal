193 IN SIN HE FOUND ME

In sin Jesus Christ had found my lost soul,

And He rescued me, give glory to His name;

He has cleansed me from all my iniquities,

And He gave me his Spirit too.

CHORUS

Joyful day when the Lord found me,

Out of darkness He took my soul;

He threw all my sins to the bottom of the sea,

And He filled my soul with a joy and a peace.

Joyful day, oh Hallelujah, Joyful day it was for me;

I will sing and give Hosannas,

In glory, when we're there with the Lord above.

2

Free me God from all temptations on earth,

Save me also from my persecutions;

Free me God from perdition of my soul,

And forever I'll serve you well.

3

When with Christ in heaven in all His glory,

We will take part in a new celestial choir;

We will enjoy a new life eternal there,

When we're there with the Lord above.

4

Do not leave me, Oh Lord, God of my health,

Help me with all of your greatest power here;

So I can fight Satan; overcome Him,

Because you are my help and shield.
