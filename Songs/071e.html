071 SING YE

//Sing ye// with great understanding,

And to the King give tribute and praise;

We give Him the glory to whom we owe all our liberty.

CHORUS

Let's march to Zion then, faithful,

City of God which is the capital;

In front of us is Christ the King, the Leader and General,

A church of Spirit, of the Lord our God.

2

We give Him praise with great understanding,

And bless the name of Lord Jesus Christ;

And we shall be singing to whom we owe all our liberty.

3

We give Him praise, the meek, humble Jesus,

It's He who gives wisdom, peace, and joy;

Those of us who were washed, glory to Jesus our Savior Lord.

4

All of those baptized in Jesus Christ's name,

They're walking gladly in their Lord's light;

The brilliant light shining and this light is Jesus Christ the Lord.
