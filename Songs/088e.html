088 WITH GREAT PLEASURE

With great pleasure jubilee, we return today to see,

Our hands shake once again and we're united;

We are happy in our hearts and let our love runneth out,

All with one voice to the Lord give Him praises.

CHORUS

//Welcome Brothers!// All the Brothers here today,

Do all joyfully proclaim: //Welcome Brothers!//

We all return to unite, Welcome Brothers!

2

Until this day God's helped you, not a moment He's left you,

And all of us have returned, Welcome Brothers!

Our Lord God has come with you, and His presence was your shield,

From all danger He's kept you, Welcome Brothers!

3

May God guard us in His love, so that deeply in our hearts,

We be sanctified to God, giving glory;

In eternal unity, where we'll never be apart,

Where they'll be no sadness, pain, Welcome Brothers!
