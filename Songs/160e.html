160 THE END OF THIS WORLD

The end of this world is drawing near,

The gospel will have its end that day;

We're contemplating all of the signals,

As they are written they will all come true.

CHORUS

Beloved friend Jesus Christ is calling,

Don't harden your heart when you hear Him;

Give yourself to Christ, He wants your soul now,

And you'll be resurrected on judgment day.

2

On that day people will be in anguish,

Desperately worried about their sin;

It will be too late for them by that time,

All those who preached will no longer exist.

3

When in the end the trumpet has sound,

Those who believed will have salvation;

The righteous judge will give them His sentence,

To those found living in their wickedness.

4

He will not let them be in His presence,

All those who had disobeyed his word;

They will be crying and grinding their teeth,

In the eternal fire they will go.
