282 JESUS CHRIST COMES

Jesus Christ comes to give gladness, where there is love,

He removes all of the sadness, where there is love;

Love helps us to come and pray, love can give us great delight,

And the darkness turns to light, where there is love.

CHORUS

//Where there is love//

All the world can sing together, where there is love;

//Where there is love//

Joy and peace always abounding, where there is love.

2

All the world just lives in gladness, where there is love,

Every heart is elevated, where there is love;

All anguish will swiftly pass, and then peace will be enjoyed,

And tranquility will abound, where there is love.

3

Oh what great joy is existing, where there is love,

Life can never be depressing, where there is love;

Everyone can have joy and peace; sadness will be left behind,

And the vices are no more, where there is love.
