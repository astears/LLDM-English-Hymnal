329 LET US FIGHT FOR THE WORK

Let us fight for the work of Christ Jesus,

Don't permit sin to dwell in you again;

Just consider the work of Christ Jesus,

Examine yourself well to follow Christ.

CHORUS

Now's the time to be purifying your soul,

All your heart and mind as well as all your being;

So you may stand in the presence of the Lord,

And be waiting someday to wear a crown.

2

The baptism that you have received,

Was in the name of Jesus Christ the King;

All your vile sins were pardoned forever,

Because of your obedience to God.

3

Jesus Christ still continues to call you,

Well then hear His voice;

He's your savior Lord;

At His side you will find rest assurance,

And be taken from all perdition too.

4

Do not sleep oh my beloved brother,

Follow faithfully;

Have faith and patience;

Don't despise now the voice that is calling,

Be persistent in journeying to His throne.
