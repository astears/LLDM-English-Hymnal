266 HOSANNA KING

Hosanna, King of the glory,

Hosanna to Jesus King;

You are the door to the heavens,

In John 10 this can be seen.

CHORUS

Let's all give praises to the name of Jesus; to His goodness we'll give praise,

Because He left His throne up in glory and to everyone gave liberty;

Let's all give praises to the name of Jesus, only give praises to Him,

Hosanna to Him of glory, to the King of Israel.

2

You are a stone that's so precious,

Placed on Zion, mount of God;

For some people you're a hindrance,

But to us you saved our souls.

3

Jesus saved the evil doer,

At the right side of His cross;

For there's no other name to save us,

Only Jesus Christ our Lord.

4

Glory, Glory, Hallelujah,

Glory, to our Savior;

Glory, glory, Hallelujah,

Glory, glory, to our God.
