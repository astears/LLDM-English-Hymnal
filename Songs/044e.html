044 JUST LIKE THE DAWN APPROACHES

Just like the dawn approaches nearer,

'Tis same oh Lord in your sweet coming;

That's why my soul longs to find you,

Because you are my savior Lord.

CHORUS

Oh dear Lord, to you we shall adore,

Oh dear Lord, to you we shall give praise;

Because you are our loving King,

And your great mercy will favor us always.

2

Long years have come and passed so quickly,

And many people have been waiting;

All with patience for your coming,

But very soon they will see you.

3

We give thanks to you our Lord Jesus,

Because you gave us this new day;

For your help, we are awaiting,

In order to prevail oh Lord.

4

There is an Apostle on this earth,

His name is Samuel Joaquin;

Name given by his own father,

Because to him it was revealed.
