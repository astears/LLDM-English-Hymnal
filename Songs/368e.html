368 MANY ARE MY FAULTS

Many are my faults oh my dear Lord,

Forgive all my sins and evilness;

Guide me in your pathway with your love,

To walk in your sanctity oh Lord.

Firm and forward I will follow you,

And take up your cross behind your steps;

Great is my debt Lord I have with you,

'Cause on Calvary you died for me.

CHORUS

Clean oh Lord my heart, and all my soul,

Free me from all evil and from sin;

Faithful Lord is what I want to be,

I'll follow you faithfully and truthful I will be.

2

It's miserable and sad living in sin,

Without love and peace from God above;

Fill up oh my Lord my heart today,

With your endless mercy and your love.

And if I endure all sin and strife,

And my soul is fatigued due to harsh trials;

I know by your love, my soul's sustained,

If I follow faithful all your ways.

3

I once swore kneeled before you oh my Lord,

To leave all my vice and vanity;

And if you help me, I will succeed,

Guard oh Lord my soul in sanctity.

Before You I kneel my Jesus Christ,

To implore of you, forgiveness Lord;

Guide me in your pathway with your light,

To my soul do give salvation, Lord.
