070 SING HAPPY TO THE LORD

Shout for joy to the Lord,

All creation, all of the whole earth;

Worship the Lord God with gladness,

Come before Him with a joyful song, and praises.

•••••

Know that the Lord is God, He is King, He has made us,

And not we ourselves, we belong to Him;

We are His people, the sheep of His pasture.

•••••

With thanksgiving and praise enter unto His gates,

And His courts with songs of praises;

//Give thanks to Him and praise His name//

For the Lord God is good,

And His love endures forever,

His faithfulness continues through all generations, Amen.
