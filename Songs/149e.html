149 DIVINE COMPANION

Divine companion leading through the pathway,

I can feel your presence as I walk along;

It has dissipated all the shadows,

Now I have light, the blessed light of Jesus Christ.

CHORUS

Stay with me my Lord it's getting late now,

I offer you my heart to dwell within;

I want you to stay in it forever,

//Accept it so// my Savior Lord.

2

The shadow of the night is now approaching,

And temptation will assuredly attack;

Do not leave me lonesome in the pathway,

//Come to my aid// until the end.

3

With you the journey does not seem as long now,

Neither thirst nor sun will make my soul fatigued;

If the ocean's waves begin to threaten,

I know upon them you will tread with majesty.
