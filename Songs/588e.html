588 THE GREAT DAY OF THE LORD

The great day of the Lord's drawing near,

Words that Jesus announced when He was preaching;

That He would come in clouds from the heavens,

To carry His church that is beloved and blessed.

CHORUS

Oh my Christ Jesus Lord, when you come down for last time,

Do not leave me here my Lord, have compassion on my dear soul;

That's been bought by your shed blood,

Take me to eternal rest so I can be close to my Lord.

2

The great day of the Lord's drawing near,

Christ announced though despised and with heartaches;

That He would come in clouds from the heavens,

With His greatness, His power, and also His fire.

3

This whole world is not ready for Christ,

For His coming nor the torment they will suffer;

To have lived without God but in their sins,

In the end God won't save all the ones of rebellion.

4

The great day of the Lord's drawing near,

That which Jesus Christ taught to all His children;

After He would take all of His people,

Many plagues God will send as He did in Egypt.
