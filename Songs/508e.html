508 IF MY FAITH DEBILITATES

If my faith debilitates He will sustain my soul,

If in me wicked increased,

He will sustain my soul.

CHORUS

//He will sustain me//

My Jesus who dearly loves me,

He will sustain me.

2

I will never be confident, and with a weak faith,

I'll let Christ be guiding me,

He will sustain my soul.

3

With His joy and satisfaction, many souls He saved,

And with His mercy He's saved me,

He will sustain me.

4

My soul was lost to be condemned;

He did not want this,

All His blood was shed for me,

He will sustain my soul.
