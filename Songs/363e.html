363 MY DARKEST HOURS

My darkest hours and anguish have now gone by,

Only sweet gladness is flooding my whole being;

I made a promise to the God of the heavens,

I will keep it though my life may be in danger;

All I ask of the church is to pray for me.

CHORUS

Merciful God, your promises are faithful,

You gave to John a divine revelation;

You said you would come to wipe away our tears,

No burden can weaken the feeling from our soul.

2

I am a pilgrim complying with this mission,

My obligation will be to speak of Christ;

I have no nest here, as a bird I am wandering,

I am a foreigner I have no home on earth;

All I have left is to serve Christ faithfully.

2nd. CHORUS

There is much joy now within this congregation,

Because the Lord has given us His great love;

And He is helping us to follow His pathway,

So we may all see Him that day at heaven's gate.
