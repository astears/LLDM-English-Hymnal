475 HOLY SPIRIT

Holy Spirit guide my footsteps,

Towards the Christ Jesus our Lord;

An may I always guard humbly,

All the mandates of His love.

CHORUS

//Tell me// how to consecrate,

All to my Father above;

//Tell me// how to do all of your will.

2

Teach me how to be submissive,

And to give you my whole being;

Vain joys and all earthly fortunes,

All these things always despise.

3

Tell me how when I'm in trials,

To always confide in you;

And if in agony bearing,

I will never be aggrieved.

4

Tell me how to be obedient,

Although sometimes my judgment;

Does not understand your purpose,

Nor find a quick reasoning.

5

Teach me how I am to follow,

Never proud with my knowledge;

Given fully to your service,

And confiding in your strength.
