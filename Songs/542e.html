542 I'LL FOLLOW YOU

I'll follow you Lord Jesus Christ, my Savior and King,

Until one day with joy I reach your dwelling up above;

So that we may obtain the gift you have prepared for us,

If I am faithful in your path until the end.

CHORUS

Never again will I remember this deceiving world of sin,

Where without peace my soul wandered rebelliously in sin;

But Jesus Christ who is the fount and the spring of purest love,

Through his grace salvation was offered to me.

2

If I go through temptations, trials and battles in this world,

They won't compare to all the joys I'll experience in your home;

If I am faithful in all things I know my soul has life,

And you'll embrace it then in your arms it will dwell.

3

There'll be no tribulation, and no bitterness at heart,

In His celestial kingdom we will all live in happiness;

Only the songs of singing angels up in heaven will be heard,

My soul will gladly sing to him and praises give.

4

Oh what great love you had for sinners' wickedness my Lord,

You left a glorious example for all to follow you;

And you had suffered many torments and much bitter pain indeed,

I am to blame for your death my dearest Lord.
