053 UNDER THE STARS

Under the stars of heaven my Jesus went to pray,

In the garden of Gethsemane;

There was no light to shine, but Christ kneeled down to pray,

When He prayed underneath the olive tree.

CHORUS

//Under the olive tree//

This is where Jesus Christ went to pray;

"Let your will be done, oh my heavenly Father,"

He exclaimed under the olive tree.

2

In His anguish and pain sweat abounded His face,

Just like blood drops that seep from a wound;

Then an angel descended and withdrew all His pain,

When He prayed under the olive tree.

3

Through His Father above, His pain was then transformed,

God infused strength and courage into Him;

He arose comforted and He gave Himself to death,

This to save all lost sinners from hell.

4

Let my song always be the great love He showed me,

When Christ went to the garden to pray;

Glory be given to Him, He did carry my grief,

When He prayed under the olive tree.
