343 MEDITATE THAT THERE IS

Meditate that there is a sweet home,

Near the margin of river of light;

Where forever they go to rejoice,

The believers of Lord Jesus Christ.

CHORUS

Farther more, farther more,

Meditate that there is a sweet home;

///Farther more/// Near the margin of river of light.

2

Meditate in the friends we'll have there,

Of the ones that we march right behind;

And to think in the end we will see them,

In the palace of God high above.

3

Where Christ Jesus now lives meditate,

Where the ones who we love now await;

And to fly to the most Holy land,

Without anguish, nor fear, nor travail.

4

Reunited to mine I will be,

Where my course will then reach to an end;

I will enter my heavenly home,

Where my soul will enjoy a sweet rest.
