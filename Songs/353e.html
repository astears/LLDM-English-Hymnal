353 JESUS CHRIST HAS SAVED MY SOUL

Jesus Christ has saved my soul, praises I will give to Him,

In His love I will rejoice and faithful I'll always be;

In my soul there's gratitude,

For He shed His blood for me one day;

To give me His salvation.

CHORUS

To Jesus Christ, the King of Kings,

Let us give honor, forevermore.

2

There is great joy in my soul, because Jesus is with me,

And He gives me His sweet peace,

I will give praise to His name;

How happy and jubilant,

I feel now with my Lord Jesus Christ;

Glory be unto our God.

3

Jesus Christ I want to see, my precious Savior Lord,

See the face of my good Lord, who for me gave up His life;

I wish to be in His home,

Where forever his love reigns with me;

And rejoice up there with Him.
