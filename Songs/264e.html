264 HOSANNAS WE GIVE

Hosannas we give the King of glory,

Because in Calvary He had a victory;

For His precious blood that He had shed,

All heaven and earth shall give Him praise.

CHORUS

Oh my dear Savior I do love you,

For you gave yourself up for my sins;

For death you suffered, upon the cross,

This was to buy us our salvation.

2

I can't repay you this debt that's so great,

Because you loved and then forgave me;

To serve and love you is my ambition,

Let's rejoice always in your blessings.

3

The pain you suffered brings tears to my eyes,

Cruel and deep anguish and great affliction;

The bloody trials you overcame,

Your name oh Jesus, immortal be.

4

It's our desire oh King of heaven,

You keep us faithful in all your pathway;

So that in one day up in your home,

Of all your blessings we shall rejoice.
