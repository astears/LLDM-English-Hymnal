096 LAMB OF GOD

Lamb of God, who descended from heaven,

When you died on the cross, to give us of your light;

And save our souls from death,

You have shed your immaculate blood;

In which my wickedness was erased on the cross,

Where you died my Lord Christ.

CHORUS

Now I do praise you my Lord, with all of my loving heart,

Because you are my God and my good Savior Lord;

The one who died for me.

2

You suffered, nailed to the cross of burden,

In affliction you showed your great love for me Lord;

You have given me peace,

You've given all my soul protection;

My soul was without God, and in wickedness too,

No faith nor salvation.

3

You gave Lord, to my life a great treasure,

Oh this peace so unique, that I could never find;

In this world of fallacy,

And now Lord, my soul joyfully praises;

I'm enjoying your love that on the painful cross,

You showed to me one day.
