76 STRONG HOUSE

Strong house and beautiful tower,

This is the church of the Lord;

Living always with the longing,

Searching for all that is love.

CHORUS

The morning is approaching,

The sun is brightly shinning;

I will sing with all of my soul,

To the one giving us life.

2

The God of heaven is reigning,

With firmness and clarity;

Teaching all of us believers,

Teaching us humility.

3

Holiness is what we ask for,
In the church of our Lord;

It's a virtue that's sustaining,

For those seeking salvation.

4

Take a sword now into your hands,

And be strong inside your heart;

Take your consecrated spirit,

Be confronted with Goliath.

5

The Lord God of every vengeance,

He will fight in your good will;

He will give you might and courage,

As He gave to King David.
