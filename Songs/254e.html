254 I HAVE WALKED WITH MY JESUS

I have walked with my Jesus, my good Savior Lord,

In a day of much fog or of light;

And confiding in Jesus I'm able to sing,

He is faithful forever, amen!

CHORUS

Deeply peace strikes my soul, yes a beautiful peace,

That I have in my good Lord Savior Lord;

And if many things are changed,

I'm able to sing, He is faithful forever, amen!

2

I have walked with my Jesus, my good Savior Lord,

In the pathway deserted and vast;

But a voice I did hear that did tenderly say:

I am faithful forever amen!

3

I have walked with my Jesus, my good Savior Lord,

In the valley of shadow and death;

All the words of my Jesus they give me great strength,

He is faithful forever amen!

4

Oh I want to walk closer with Jesus my Lord,

Yes for all of my life till the end;

And so joyful in heaven I'll shout in loud voice,

He is faithful forever amen!
