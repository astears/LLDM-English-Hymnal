273 OH LORD JEHOVAH

Oh Lord Jehovah,

I called to you and you saved me,

You also made my soul lift up from the graveyard;

From a great danger, with your virtue you have saved me,

//You gave me life// and communion with the just one.

CHORUS

Oh sing to the Lord, you servants of Jesus,

That in His great will, an Apostle was sent;

Because in one moment,

His anger will be,

But in His good will, much life may abound.

2

During the sunset, your tears will never be lasting,

For in the morning, the happiness will continue;

I'll always say, in this great prosperity,

//Unmovable// my sentiments always will be.

3

And by your kindness and the great love you have shown us,

To this Church you have, always given consolation;

Just like a relic, an Apostle has flourished,

He's from a lineage that you have promised;

Your Church would never be missing.

4

Oh Lord my God, with my cries I will proclaim,

To you my Lord, my supplications I'll bring;

And when in danger, of death my soul may be found,

//If you will help me// I will always be victorious.
