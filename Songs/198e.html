198 AT ONE TIME

At one time I was merely a captive,

Incarcerated by the tempter was I;

Without freedom in the ties of transgression,

I was alone in my darkness and cruel pain.

CHORUS

If you can hear the Savior's voice today,

Though you may be imprisoned and with fear;

Only my Lord can offer liberty,

You will receive forgiveness from Christ.

2

If you're captive accept His forgiveness,

The good Lord can erase all your sins;

Christ has come to give salvation to the lost ones,

And to give all of the captives liberty.

3

I've been liberated now from my past life,

And the Lord has broken all my chains;

Christ has come to give salvation to the lost ones,

And to give all of the captives liberty.
