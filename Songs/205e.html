205 MY FRIEND IS JESUS

My friend is Jesus the best friend I have,

Tender and loving, none other like Him;

All of my life I will rest in his love,

Only in Him I will be secure.

CHORUS

Oh my friend Jesus! Closer to you,

Your pathway firmly I want to walk;

Only I want you to reign in me,

I want to live in your holy grace.

2

When I'm in trials He's closer to me,

He gives me strength and the will to move on;

Never forgotten I'll be with my Lord,

Never will I be without His love.

3

He gives me light, when I start to doubt,

He guides the pathway in which I will walk;

I owe all my life to Lord Jesus Christ,

Only through Him, I have life to live.

4

He is my guide like a Shepherd to sheep,

He leads my life through the ways of His peace;

He rescues me from whatever I fear,

Giving me joy to see His face.
