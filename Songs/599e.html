599 I AM HOPING FOR THE MORNING

I am hoping for the morning, of that day without compare,

Where all happiness emanates, a day of eternal joy.

CHORUS

//Always hoping// of a life free from all pain,

Where a welcome is awaiting from Lord Jesus Christ my Friend.

2

I am hoping for the victory, against death I will prevail,

I'll receive eternal glory and a crown around my head.

3

I am hoping for the heavens, where love eternally reigns,

Pilgrim I am and I'm yearning, for the dwellings of the Lord.

4

To the celestial triumphant, I will soon unite my song,

All my cries will be translated, in an angelical song.
