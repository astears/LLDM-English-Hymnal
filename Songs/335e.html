335 MASTER GIVE HEED TO MY PRAYER

Master give heed to my prayer,

And lis't my constricted heart;

Ineffable tears of my soul,

Are shed as I see the lies.

CHORUS

The false teachers consent to all sin and strife,

//without remorse// But when King Jesus returns to earth,

//They'll be repaid// And then they will repent from sin,

Because the Lord swiftly will strike them then;

Excuses He will not hear again //Soon He will come//

He'll pay all men for their sin and strife, He will come soon.

2

Sleeveless and unfitted clothing,

Are unpleasant to the Lord; Fashion rings, necklaces, pearls,

Are all against piety. 3 With perms and ostentatious hair-dos,

The young and the old adorn;

Oh teachers consent to these actions,

In hell the will see their end.

4

All women who journey with Jesus,

Are filled with God's piety;

Humbling, modestly living,

Giving fruits of sanctity.

5

False teachers proclaim they are living,

In truth and in holiness;

Their teaching of error and blunder,

Confirms they are blind as well.

6

False teachers who preach of God's doctrine,

And speak of the word of God;

Allowing the people to falter,

By tricking humanity.
