370 NOTHING IN THIS WORLD

Nothing in this world can satisfy me,

As Christ Jesus, my good Savior Lord;

Nothing gives my heart more peace and comfort,

Only His compassion and great love.

And if I owned this whole world entirely,

It would not be valuable for me;

Because the joy of this world does not fill,

It does not give peace like Christ my Lord.

2

If my life was filled with many riches,

Much fame, glory, and much honor too;

But if I did not have hope within me,

Of a new life that's better than this.

If I had all things but had not Jesus,

And His love that nailed Him to the cross;

Oh where on this world would I find,

Only the Lord gives me peace and light.

3

Oh how false are all the things in this world,

Without Jesus nothing has value;

Without Christ there's no joy, life eternal,

But eternal darkness and great pain.

If I could live without Jesus Christ here,

What disgrace to die in wickedness;

Without Christ in darkness and in shadows,

Without Christ for all eternity.

4

Oh what joy and peace my Jesus gives me,

He does heal me from all of my pains;

Only He forgives all my transgressions,

He does crown me with His splendor too.

If I only have dear Lord Christ Jesus,

And from the world nothing I have more;

Only He can fill all my desires,

And in glory I will see His face.
