195 IN A CROSS

On the cross of much bitter pain,

I pondered Jesus on the cross;

Lost I was when He looked at me,

//Oh divine love//

CHORUS

//Oh divine love//your grand power has conquered me,

My heart will be for you my Lord,

//Oh divine love//

2

You did see me, my good friend, Christ,

Burning holy compassion, Lord;

Your goal was of saving me,

//Oh divine love//

3

Prodigally I walked in the dark,

From a sweet home, wandering I was;

Your eternal love was after me,

//Oh divine love//

4

I won't rebel, any more Lord,

I shall not be a vile slave;

Take me in your great power Lord,

//Oh divine love//
