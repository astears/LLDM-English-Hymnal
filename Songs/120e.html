120 AS I'M READING IN THE BIBLE

As I'm reading in the Bible how the Lord talks to us,

And he blesses the children with his love;

I would also like to be in the resting place with them,

In the arms of our tender Savior Lord.

2

I would like to see Him placing His hands over me,

And to feel His love embracing me;

To enjoy his pleasant glance, and to hear His blessed word;

Let the children come to me he now says.

3

When I'm kneeling at His platform in a prayer raised to Him,

All His love I will then experience;

If I know how to seek Him, I will listen to His words;

Of the kingdom that He went to prepare.

4

There are still so many people who don't know of His home,

And they will not receive our Jesus Christ;

Oh how I would like to show them that there's a place for them,

Up in heaven where they could go with me!
