034 TO LOVE YOU MY DEAR LORD

To love you my dear Lord is reassuring,

Your calling will forever be remembered;

You have promised to engrave unto my feelings,

And sculpt your dearest name unto my soul.

CHORUS

That's why in every step I follow you Lord,

That's why I always long to walk beside you;

And if my future should remove me from beside you,

How bitter life would be in my digression.

2

You have promised my dear God a palm on my hand,

Sacred palms from your kind hands so good in nature;

Our names forever you would have inscribed,

In the hands of your eternal loving name.

3

My existence is for you my dear Lord Jesus,

It's your promise to give me beautiful life;

In the meantime while your Holy Spirit guides me,

My life will be rejoicing my dear Lord.
