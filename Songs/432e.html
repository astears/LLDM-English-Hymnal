432 WE WILL PREACH ONLY THE TRUTH

We will preach only the truth, against evil we protest,

Any doctrine of deceit we will reject;

All inventions we reject, what are mere traditions here,

Which invalidate the word of our Lord.

CHORUS

Only Jesus Christ can save thee, sinners can be saved through Christ,

No other Savior you'll find, no one else to mediate;

Only Christ can save and guard all sinners here.

2

Nowhere have we ever found, that we all have to confess,

All our sins to a man who's a sinner too;

Those who seek only their God contrite with sincerity,

They will find that only God forgives with love.

3

We inquire the reason, they have removed from the law,

The second commandment that our God once gave;

Thou will not form an image, to it you will not incline,

You will only serve and worship your true God.

4

For Latin we do not care, on the doctrine we depend,

This is where we find the promises of God;

Christ once asked to be searching only for the word of truth,

Life eternal and salvation we will find.

5

It serves only to exploit an impoverished humankind,

When they speak of a purgatory of dread;

But we know that Jesus came to shed His blood on the cross,

To erase all of our blame and all our shame.

6

When and where has God declared, that we should give worship to,

An image that's made of metal or of wood?

Of bullas when did He speak? No indulgences are named,

In the Bible nothing of this can be found.
