348 GOOD JESUS CHRIST

Good Jesus Christ, my Lord, I wish to serve thee,

And in your vineyard I would like to work;

I will be happy just being at your service,

Throughout this whole world is where I'd like to work.

CHORUS

And when my life is over on this earth Lord,

Grant me one wish to one day reach your dwelling;

My clean and pure soul I am wishing to give you,

Oh Lord I want you to crown me on that day.

2

Good Jesus Christ, my Lord, I wish to serve thee,

With all the virtues of spirit and of pow'r;

Working with miracles through your holy gospel,

Giving believers eternal life and joy.

3

Good Jesus Christ, my Lord, I wish to serve thee,

And in your vineyard I would like to work;

Before my soul makes its way to paradise,

Lord I want to take advantage of my time.
