226 GLORY WE GIVE YOU

Glory we give you oh Lord,

In this day pleasantly passing;

May your people give your glory,

In the banquets of the pure Lamb.

CHORUS

Praise the Lord in all the heavens,

May your people sing your praise;

To the celestial Lamb: Jesus,

For all of eternity.

2

This divine banquet of love,

That which can't be bought with money;

Redeeming us with His shed blood,

Running down the wooden cross.

3

We are the people you've chosen,

By the grace of our Jesus;

For this we're singing Hosannas,

To the divine Redeemer.
