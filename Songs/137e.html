137 LET'S GIVE OUR THANKS

Let's give our thanks to God our Lord,

Our enemy has been defeat;

Our victory was for the Lord,

We should all give Him honor and praise.

CHORUS

In the world we're living, let us tell the story,

That Jesus the Christ came, to die on the cross;

Let not one be fainting, may we all arrive there,

To glory eternal, He went to prepare.

2

He's has fought against wickedness,

And we have life given by him;

With all His might, He won the fight,

We should all give Him honor and praise.

3

Great is the God of my vigor,

The earth and sea do sing to Him;

The glory of all of His might,

We should all give Him honor and praise.

4

The faith had already been lost,

Also the hope of our health;

But my dear God has shown His love,

We should all give Him honor and praise.
