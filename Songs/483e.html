483 ALL THROUGHOUT EGYPT

All throughout Egypt I wandered along,

Through many fennels, I aguishly walked;

I was fatigued from this path of mistakes,

My good Pastor had looked for me.

CHORUS

Great land that caused embezzlement,

A mystical path that my soul found one day;

Egypt the great, attractive with lust,

Full of enchantments which ruin my soul.

Just as a lion devouring me,

'Twas in the sepulcher my heart was found;

In the perdition, my soul was so lost,

Egypt the great leads to our death.

2

My Savior has gave salvation to me,

In all your Servants my soul finds rest;

I was fatigued from this path of mistakes,

My good Pastor had looked for me.

3

One day oh Egypt, you'll pay for your sins,

All your attraction deserves punishment;

All fascinations and vanities too,

The Lord Christ will abhor your pleas.
