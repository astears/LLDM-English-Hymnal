224 GLORY TO YOU MY DEAR JESUS

Glory to you my dear Jesus,

Glory for all of your kindness;

Glory eternal to your mercy,

Oh loving Jesus Christ.

CHORUS

///Glory, Glory Hallelujah/// To you Lord Jesus Christ.

2

You have loved me with compassion,

And for me the cross you've taken;

With your tenderness you have loved me,

My loving Jesus Christ.

3

All my faith is in your death Christ,

With it you brought my salvation;

Eternal life you have brought me,

My loving Savior Christ.

4

We shall see you up in heaven,

With you we shall live forever;

We'll rejoice with your great presence,

My loving Jesus Christ.

5

Have courage, yes Christian courage,

Jesus is your greatest helper;

He will take you up to heaven,

To live eternally.
