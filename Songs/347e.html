347 MY SOUL IS IN AGONY

"My soul is in agony", you said my Jesus,

My gratitude is so immense;

Because for me was your death.

CHORUS

You were the sacrificed Lamb,

He from heaven who descended;

He came to avert my soul from sinning,

And He changed my being.

And now I do want to be serving,

My days and my nights always be praising;

There is not a treasure in this whole world,

That I can pay you.

2

For this now I'm praising, you my Lord Jesus,

So when my King's calling me;

I am well ready, so ready.

3

You drank vinegar, so bitter tasting,

Three times you knelt, to pray for us;

For us you endured and bared pain.
