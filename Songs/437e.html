437 WHERE HE LEADS ME

///I can hear my Savior calling///

"Take thy cross and follow, follow me."

CHORUS

///Where He leads me I will follow///

I'll go with Him, with Him, all the way.

2

3

I'll go with Him, with Him all the way.

4

And go with me, with me all the way.
