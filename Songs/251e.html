251 THERE IS A FOUNTAIN FILLED

There is a fountain filled with blood drawn from Emmanuel's veins,

And sinners plunged beneath that flood to lose their guilty stains;

And sinners plunged beneath that flood to lose all their guilty stains,

And sinners plunged beneath that flood to lose their guilty stains.

CHORUS

Oh celiac fountain, flowing so divine,

I came to you one day, all my sins were washed.

2

Dear dying lamb, thy precious blood shall never lose any power,

Till all the ransomed church of God be saved to sin no more;

Be saved, to sin no more, be saved // to sin no more//

Till all the ransomed church of God be saved to sin no more.

3

Ever since by faith I saw the stream thy flowing wounds supply,

Redeeming love has been my theme and shall be till I die;

Ever since by faith I saw the stream thy flowing wounds supply,

Redeeming love has been my theme and shall be till I die.

4

Then in a noble sweeter song I'll sing Thy power to save,

When this poor lisping stammering tongue lies silent in the grave;

Then in a noble sweeter song I'll sing Thy power to save,

When this poor lisping stammering tongue lies silent in the grave.
