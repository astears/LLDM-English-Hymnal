102 CHRIST IS A FOUNTAIN

Christ is a fountain of the living water,

For all the souls who want to be saved today;

Coming to His feet repented and ready,

He wishes only to forgive all your sins.

CHORUS

Soul do not wander in error, Listen to the voice that's calling;

Come to the fountain of life, Fountain of pardon and love.

2

Christ is the door who will lead us to heaven,

He is the pathway by which salvation's gained;

He by His truth is illuminating our souls,

All who repent for salvation of their souls.

3

Souls who are lost and are wandering in this world,

They are fatigued lost in paths of wickedness;

Christ calls unto you with His love so tender,

From all your vile sins, He gives you liberty.

4

Souls who are lost in their evil transgressions,

Lost they have been in the shadows of their sins;

The voice of Jesus is so humbly calling,
Don't you hold back,

Peace the Lord has for your soul.
