113 WHEN MY CONFLICTS CEASE

When my conflicts cease to hurt me in this earthly life below,

And I leave this world full of afflictions too;

We will enter paradise through the celestial doors above,

In the New Jerusalem.

CHORUS

With the saints we will be singing of redemption of our souls,

//In Jerusalem//

With clear accents full of jubilee, we'll praise our Savior Lord,

In the New Jerusalem.

2

Though the waves are rising higher and a hurricane is near,

Our ship they will always want to disrupt;

With our faith in Christ we will arrive onto the beach above,

In the New Jerusalem.

3

Let us consecrate our lives to the full service of the Lord,

Let us always speak salvation of our God;

If we're working in His vineyard we'll await for His grand prize,

In the New Jerusalem.

4

In that city filled with beauty we will never say goodbye,

Without effort we'll be joyful without cease;

Face to face we will see Jesus Christ the Savior of our souls,

In the New Jerusalem.
