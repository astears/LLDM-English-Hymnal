324 THE MILITANTS OF OUR JESUS

The militants of our Jesus are now walking,

Always hoping that to the mansions they'll arrive;

It is the place where the promised rest is waiting,

Also the crown that Jesus offered us one day.

CHORUS

He says come to me my soldier,

I will give you your reward;

Because you were very faithful,

Come I will give you your crown.

2

Imperishable, filled with glory is the crown,

That's for the soldier who prevails in the battle;

And when it's over, and they sing their song of triumph,

A crown will shine on top of them with clarity.

3

All of the squadrons of our Jesus have a mark,

It has been placed within each individual heart;.

This is the password so that they may be accepted,

And be participants of life in Christ's mansion.
