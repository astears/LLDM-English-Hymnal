459 ON YOUR PATHWAY OF LOVE

On your pathway of love,

I want to walk my Lord,

Taking me always close to you;

In everyplace imparting much joy and much solace,

But always filled with your mercy.

CHORUS

My shining path of love fill today,

May your spirit be guiding me;

And every time I sing, may it be filled with love,

In the pathway where I am found.

2

Keep me closer to you oh my kind redeemer,

Do not ever, ever allow;

That I walk without light, without aim, and without peace,

My shining path of love fill today.

3

The final journey will quickly come to an end,

No more suffering I will have here;

Oh permit me to sing to you majestic Lord,

My shining path of love fill today.
