309 SACRED SCRIPTURES FROM THE BIBLE

Sacred scriptures from the Bible, they are from our good Father,

Those who guard well all the wheat, in the storehouse of the Lord;

Also the church of Christ Jesus, is the storehouse of the Lord,

That which guards the bread of life, also water of our lives.

CHORUS

Let's go to the storehouse of God, all singing Hallelujahs,

The wind is blowing more strongly, and the wheat is separating;

Let's go to the storehouse of God, now that the wind is blowing,

There is a banquet awaiting, for all the ones who are entering.

Wind is blowing, blowing more, and the wheat's separating,

Wind is blowing, blowing more, and the straw will fly away.

2

If one hears the voice of God, then come to the Lord's storehouse,

Jesus will give food for hunger, just as He is giving me;

If you hunger to be righteous, clearly hear the word of God,

In all your ways you'll be blessed, and much comfort you will find.

3

The Holy Spirit is calling, and the bridegroom invites you,

Telling you to freely drink of the water of the living;

If you come full of repentance, you will enter through the door,

In the name of Jesus Christ you will be baptized and have life.
