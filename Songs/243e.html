243 THERE'S MANY THAT NEVER

There's many who don't know, or understand why,

That I am so joyful each day;

Crossing over Jordan, to Canaan arrive,

Oh yes it is heaven on earth.

CHORUS

//Oh yes it is heaven on earth//

Crossing over Jordan, to Canaan arrive;

Oh yes it is heaven on earth.

2

When happily singing the saints sing a song,

The peoples question as to why;

'Cause it is the Holy Spirit of God,

That Jesus has sent to our souls.

3

Celestial like tones we are hearing them sing,

Through all the earth, sky, and the sea;

The message of Christ resounding to all,

That soon He will come to the earth.

4

We will all see Jesus, in glory He'll come,

The man who had died on the cross;

The angels in heaven will then come to take,

The church that Christ bought with His blood.

2nd. CHORUS

//And then a new heaven will be//

Christ with all His angels will take us there;

And then a new heaven will be.
