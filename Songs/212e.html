212 THIS IS A DEMONSTRATION

This is a demonstration,

From a soul grateful in heart;

Whose life is always surrendering,

At the feet of my dear Lord.

CHORUS

Praise my God, for all He's done,

Giving me security; For the Lord is good,

His steadfast love endures for all,

And His faithfulness forever, ///Amen///

2

Oh what joy my soul encounters,

When I praise you oh my Lord;

My life is always surrendering,

With most humble heart my Lord.

3

Oh my Lord, my dearest Savior,

Shield me always with your love;

Abiding with you forever,

In your mansions someday reign.
