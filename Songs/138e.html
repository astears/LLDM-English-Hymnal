138 ARE YOU SURROUNDED BY SORROW?

Are you surrounded by sorrow?

Are all your trials so strong?

Are you tired of your sufferings?

And is the tempest coming near?

CHORUS

//Keep on praying// Although tempest is roaring near;

All your ways my Lord is watching,

He'll be there to comfort you.

2

In your moments of great sorrow,

When your soul is suffering;

In my Jesus you'll find comfort,

And your sorrows He'll relieve.

3

To my Lord always keep calling,

Look at God with greatest zeal;

Of your grief He will relieve you,

Mitigating all your pain.

4

If my God delays in answers,

Keep on praying do not cease;

Soon His help you will see coming,

As He answers all your needs.
