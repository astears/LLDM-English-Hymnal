564 I AM SO WRETCHED

I am so wretched here without my Lord and God,

Living in this world where I do not have a home;

My Savior offers me today a joyful place,

Where I will always live with Him.

CHORUS

//With my Savior Jesus Christ I'll go,

Walking onward to that blessed place//

2

I have new hope now that I'm living here with God,

Because salvation I have found from my dear Lord;

I have no sadness all the burdens disappeared,

Because now I have joy in Christ.

3

I can do nothing but be serving Jesus Christ,

I want to serve Him truly with all of my heart;

So that one day I may be with Him filled with joy,

To be in the palace with Christ.

4

My Savior says to me like this: Oh sin no more,

Be holy for I am holy and you'll see me;

For otherwise in the palace you'll have no place,

Where the new Jerusalem is.
