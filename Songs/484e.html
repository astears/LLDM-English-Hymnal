484 OH LORD HERE AT YOUR FEET

Oh Lord here at your feet, I cry with gratitude,

Because all my transgressions, you have forgiven me;

I have the strangest feeling, very deep in my heart,

As a flame it is burning, it is your divine love.

CHORUS

How can I demonstrate, my gratitude my Lord?

All I can do is cry, with my tear drops of love;

There's nothing I can give, only my heart and soul,

I want to be closer, closer to you dear Lord.

2

How sweet it is to cry, as I feel your pardon,

Knowing that in all truth, your grace I have received;

Who can move me from your side? If I walk in your ways,

You will guard me from sin, because now I am Thine.

3

Oh what felicity, I feel in all my being,

Coming to cry oh Lord, before your feet I pray;

Eternal life and glory, you faithfully promised,

Where you are now my Lord, do permit me to be.
