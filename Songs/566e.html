566 LET'S GO WALKING BROTHERS

Let's go walking Brothers, towards that land in heaven,

That Christ our Master went up to prepare;

Where we will arrive to eternal rest, forever, amen.

CHORUS

There is a place, I know it well, where all the streets are of gold and jewels,

It is a place, where I will live, for all of the eternity;

In this palace, where I will live, for all of the eternity.

2

Let's go walking Brothers, we are only pilgrims,

While traveling through this world, take no time to rest;

Until we're arriving to that palace with our good King.

3

Let's go walking Brothers, the time's coming closer,

In that day so joyful, day of triumph, strength;

All of those who sparkle, with the blood of Redeemer, Jesus.
