097 I BELIEVE THE BIBLE

I believe the Bible has taught me how to pray,

Jesus heard and answered,

He took my sins away;

Gave me peace and pardon, wrote my name above,

Glory hallelujah! For His wondrous love.

CHORUS

I believe the Bible,

Oh it's so divine! Heaven's golden sunlight in its pages shine;

Lights my way to glory,

I'm surely going thro',

I believe the Bible, for it is so true.

2

I believe the Bible, it teaches me to sing,

Moses' song of vic'try o'er the tyrant king;

I have songs of victory flowing from my heart,

Glory Hallelujah! For His perfect peace.

3

I believe the Bible, of holiness it speaks,

Gracious gift from Jesus to the one who seeks;

Tales of keeping power, though the tides are rough,

Glory hallelujah! For His precious blood.

4

I believe the Bible, it teaches me to run,

In this royal highway till the prize is won;

Shows the crown awaiting, if I win the race,

Glory hallelujah! For His saving grace.
