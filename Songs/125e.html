125 WHEN I SEE THE HEAVENS

When I see the heavens, the work of your hands,

The moon and the sun, how beauteous and precious!

CHORUS

Oh my blessed Lord, how precious to love thee,

When I feel your love and praise you forever.

2

The finches are singing, nightingales do also,

They rise up their chanting till they reach your mansion.

3

I can see the oceans; I can see the stars,

I can see the mountains, such precious creations.

4

I long just to serve you, with all of my heart,

Till I reach your mansion, up above in heaven.
