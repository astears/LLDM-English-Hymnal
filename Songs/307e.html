307 THE PEACE OF GOD

The peace of God may be with you now,

On this happy day, that the Lord has given thee;

With happiness, much fortune and peace,

With all of our heart, it's what we desire.

May the hand of God always bless thee, and be your strong shield,

The God that you're serving;

May He be your refuge,

Anointed of God.

CHORUS

Much stronger than sun's bright light, your Election shone,

Much more precious than the high stars,

His unction was; And just as with Aaron, the good Lord elected thee,

With fine royal garments,

He has also placed on thee.

//And now we would all like to be,

Little chimes decorating the borders of your royal vestment//

2

God of Abraham, Isaac and Jacob,

Is your God as well and the God of our souls;

The good Lord will always be your shield,

And He will grant us, to have you among us.

His people on this day so joyful, we give Him all praise,

To the only wise God and also almighty for this blessing here.
