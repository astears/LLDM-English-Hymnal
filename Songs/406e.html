406 OH HOLY CHURCH OF THE LORD

Oh holy church of the Lord, wife of our Savior Jesus,

He gave to you liberation, and now you sing with great love;

We have all heard your sweet voice, eastern and western skies,

Your pomengates are fruits of desire to sinners and those who will be free.

CHORUS

The grace of God given to all His children,

By sending us His son with love; It is with joy that we accept this blessing,

With great delight such as the King who danced for Him.

2

You are a fount of the Lord, sealed and protected by God;

With all his virtues he's crowned within the new Israel;

Flow'r of the valley of Sharon, planted in honor and love,

I'm my beloved, and he belongs to me; His love is forever my own.

3

Let us all sing in delight, let us sing with gratitude,

Within the sacred courtyard, I'm overcome with His love;

Awake oh north wind and south wind, come down from Mount Gilead,

Pleasant your orchards, a paradise glooming; A refuge for those in distress.

4

Rejoice beloved of God, you are the bride of the Lord,

For you have given Him heirs; many more than long ago;

For many centuries have passed, the prophecy was fulfilled,

This is the mystery our God has revealed us; By sending his Servants of love.
