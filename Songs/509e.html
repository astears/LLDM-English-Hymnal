509 IF ONCE MY LIFE WAS DARK

If once my life was dark and my path full of transgressions,

Just like a gloomy night making me quiver within;

Then Jesus has imparted,

His divine love and grace,

And I am dwelling blissful under a cloudless sky.

CHORUS

Oh Church of the Living God, a beacon shining brightly,

The Pillar and Ground of the utmost truth;

Your weapon is God's word that defeats the adversary,

And the man who fears your name, shall find his liberty.

2

Today I walk with courage and triumph in my battles,

Dreaming of my sweet victory with Jesus Christ my Lord;

And in this world I witness the wakening of nations,

I see the many people's surrendering to Christ.

3

No matter Satan's power and cunning plans of evil,

The weapons he may use to obstruct my path to God;

I shall not fear my battle, if you Christ walk by my side,

For I know with your power triumphant I shall be.
