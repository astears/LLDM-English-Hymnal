444 HOW DULCET IS YOUR NAME

How dulcet is your name my Jesus Christ,

Bonanza you have placed within my heart;

You died on the wood cross, and brought me salvation,

By faith I'm walking in your sweet light.

You threw my woes away in the depths of the sea,

You'll never remember them again;

Your blood has cleansed my soul from all iniquity,

And your truth was established in me.

2

How dulcet is your name my Jesus Christ,

You were compassionate towards my soul;

You filled me with great health, in all my heart and soul,

By your virtue and by your mercy.

A song of grace is what I feel within my heart,

Your pathway's been a pleasure to me;

My eyes are fixed on heaven and now I can see,

The glory of your great power, Lord.

3

How dulcet is your name my Jesus Christ,

The joy will be eternal in your home;

Dwellings of jubilee, and plentiful of light,

Up there all trials and pains will end.

Through your mercifulness give me wisdom to know,

Your will so that I may flourish here;

According to your word, I know that you'll return,

Meanwhile do not allow me to fall.
