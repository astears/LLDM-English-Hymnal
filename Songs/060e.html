060 BLESSED THE MAN

Blessed the man who in the Lord confides,

God's holy hand will give him blessings, peace;

Because our God is love,

And everyone who seeks Him, will encounter Him.

CHORUS

The Lord Christ Jesus spoke like this,

"Come unto me, and I will give you rest."

2

While in my struggles I will be sustained,

Trusting in God I will be saved from harm;

Because truthful is God,

I've felt this feeling always in my heart and soul.

3

Blessed that day in which my soul was freed,

He freed me from my slavery to sin;

A voice I listened to,

Saying, "This is my Son who came to save the souls."

4

When I was found lost in iniquity,

I felt your hand turning my soul from sin;

Because the Lord says this,

I've not come to condemn, but to save all the world.
