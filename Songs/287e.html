287 OH JESUS MY KING AND MY SOVEREIGN

Oh Jesus my King and my sovereign,

My joy is to sing him his praise;

He's King yet He treats me like family,

He's King yet He shares all His love.

Having from the Father all glory,

He came to lift me from the scoria;

//And now I rejoice// with Him.

2

Jesus is the friend that I long for,

In darkness and light always near;

Patient and humble always near me,

His help gives me comfort and cheer.

That's why I will faithfully follow,

Because He's my King and companion;

//And now I rejoice// with Him.

3

Oh Lord tell me what I could give you,

For all of your goodness to me?

Could this be enough, serve and love you?

And commit my whole life to thee?

If so then accept my devotion,

To you only now I surrender;

//And now I rejoice// with Him.
