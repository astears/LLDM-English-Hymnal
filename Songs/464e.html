464 I WISH TO BE LIKE MY CHRIST

I wish to be like my Christ,

To obey only His will;

Humbly I wish to serve Him,

To have holiness.

CHORUS

//Let us be singing, let us give honor,

All praise and worship to the Lamb of God//

2

In you I will be confiding,

My blessed Savior and Lord;

Whether I'm living or dying,

To always prevail.

3

Nothing will ever depart me,

From my Jesus and His love;

For only He gives me strength,

Consolation He gives.

4

Tenderly God guards my soul,

From all danger and from pain;

And my soul so grateful to Him,

Today sings with love.
