281 OUR LORD JESUS HAS MADE A PROMISE

Our Lord Jesus has made a promise,

To all the faithful who guard His words;

A crown embellished with gold and pearls,

That's only given to those of Christ. CHORUS

Many rich dwellings, He is preparing,

Fine linen garments someday to wear;

For all the saints who have overcome,

Great tribulation and triumphed all.

2

The light will glisten more than the sun,

Around the white crystal throne above;

And all the chosen, will gather round it,

While giving glories to Jesus Christ.

3

The Lord gives welcome to all the faithful,

To those who prospered till the end;

They'll see God's mansion, through gates of heaven,

And they'll see Christ at God's right hand.

4

Walls made of jasper, gold streets of glory,

With every kind of precious stone;

Praising our Savior with jubilation,

For they are reigning with Jesus Christ.
