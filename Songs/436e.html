436 CLAP YOUR HANDS

Clap your hands, all you nations,

Shout to the Lord God with cries of jubilee;

'Cause how awesome is the Lord King Most High,

The great King over all of the earth!

He subdued nations under us, peoples under our feet;

He subdued nations, peoples under us,

He chose for us, our inheritance,

The pride of Jacob, whom He loved.

•••••

God ascended with shouts of joy,

The Lord with the sounding of trumpets;

To our King, sing praise.

For God is the King of all of the earth,

///Sing praise/// sing a psalm of praise to Him;

Lord God reigns over the nations,

God is seated above on His holy throne;

The nobles of the nations all assemble,

As people of God of Abraham;

For the kings of the entire earth belong to God,

He is greatly exalted.
