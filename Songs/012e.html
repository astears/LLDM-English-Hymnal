012 PRAISE THE LORD FROM THE HEAVENS

///Praise the Lord/// To the Lord ///Praise the Lord///

Praise the Lord from the heavens;

Praise him in the heights above, ////Praise the Lord////

Praise him, all His angels; Praise the Lord, ///Praise Him the Lord/// Praise the Lord,

Praise Him the Lord all his heavenly hosts.

•••••

Praise Him, sun and moon, ///Praise Him the Lord///

Praise Him, all you shining stars;

//Praise Him the Lord// ////Praise Him the Lord////

Praise him, you highest heavens.

And you waters above the skies,

Let them praise the name of the Lord.

•••••

/////Praise Him the Lord///// the name of the Lord,

For He commanded and they were created.

He set them in place forever and ever;

//He gave a decree that will never pass away//

///Praise the Lord/// to the Lord,

Praise the Lord from the earth;

You great sea creatures and all ocean depths,

Lightning and hail, snow and clouds.

•••••

Stormy winds that do His bidding,

You mountains and all hills;

Fruit trees and all cedars,

Wild animals and all cattle;

Small creatures and flying birds.

•••••

Kings of the earth and all nations,

Praise the Lord God;

You princes and all rulers on earth,

//Praise the Lord God//

•••••

Young men and maidens,

Old men and children;

Let them praise the name of the Lord,

Also all the maidens.

Praise the Lord God old men and children,

Praise the name of the Lord God;

Old men and children //Praise the Lord//

Praise the name of the Lord God.

•••••

For His name alone is exalted,

His splendor is above the earth and the heavens;

He has raised up for His people a horn,

The praise of all His saints.

Of Israel, the people close to His heart,

Praise the Lord, Amen.
