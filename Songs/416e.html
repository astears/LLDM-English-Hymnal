416 FATHER I LAY AT YOUR FEET

Father I lay at your feet now,

Break my hardest chains of prison;

Oh respond while I am calling,

Put your Spirit within me.

CHORUS

Put your Spirit within my soul,

Make me what I am meant to be;

Make me pure in all things; make me free from all sin,

Put your Spirit within me.

2

Meanwhile Christ is interceding,

Meanwhile I am humbly praying;

Come and give me what I need Lord,

Put you Spirit within me.

3

I do not want to offend you,

I will live only to please you;

And I will keep you in my heart,

Put your Spirit within me.

4

Free me from being an orphan,

Oh please do not let me perish;

Keep me in your holy bosom,

Put your Spirit within me.
