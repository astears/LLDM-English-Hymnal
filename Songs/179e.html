179 IN THIS LIFE WE ARE HOPING

In this life we are hoping to gain victory,

The one someday our Lord will give to us;

After we will be with Him in His great glory,

Where we'll rejoice in eternal liberty.

CHORUS

////Hallelujah//// to the Lord,

///Hallelujah// from temptations He has freed our souls.

2

Jesus has promised our souls many rich dwellings,

The ones He said He was going to prepare;

Leaving behind all our sins back on the Calvary,

And never God will remember them again.

3

The peace will last many years more than one thousand,

To he whose gained salvation for his soul;

Their song will always speak of their sweet victory,

That from eternal condemnation they are freed.

4

The whitest vestment, crown, and palm we will carry,

One by one in the end we'll all receive;

A life eternal that God will give our souls,

And God will never take back what He's proclaimed.
