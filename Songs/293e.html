293 YOUNG PEOPLE, GO TO THE BATTLE

Young people, go to the battle out to fight,

The gospel we will all defend;

We need to conquer all of the lost souls,

Using our powerful weapon.

CHORUS

Jesus Christ will guide me, He's our support,

To all the young who will, confide in Him;

In every battle 'gainst the tempter,

Securely walking now, what's there to fear?

The cause is not ours, it's for the Lord,

And we will all go win the war.

2

In all your battles, liberty He'll give,

Against the legions of Satan;

We shouldn't lose our sight in Jesus Christ,

And glory you will all obtain.

3

All of the youth someday will come and join,

The flag of Jesus Christ their Lord;

Triumph in triumph, surely they will gain,

Celestial honors in our Lord.

4

Men without God groan inwardly with pain,

Let us fulfill our duty here;

Let's go to them and shelter offer them,

In Jesus we will always win.
