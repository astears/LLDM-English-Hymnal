607 MY SHEPHERD SUBLIME

My Shepherd sublime is Jesus,

He is at the right hand of our God;

His most subtle hand redeems me,

From all sins of the most vile man.

CHORUS

My good Shepherd, your staff is guiding me,

My good Shepherd, with your love sustaining me;

My good Shepherd, nothing will my soul fear now,

Though through darkness I journey, the shadow of death;

I know with you, securely I'll walk.

2

One day a sheep went a wandering,

And the Shepherd went looking for it;

Finding it well lost and captive,

For to save it He gave it His life.

3

I have a Shepherd that's living,

In heaven, at right hand of God;

His pathway my sight does follow,

And His voice does direct all my ways.

4

Perhaps you are lost and wandering,

Without Pastor you're living, vile man;

If His loving voice, you hear now,

Come and give all your heart and your soul.
