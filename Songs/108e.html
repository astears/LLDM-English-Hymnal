108 JESUS' COMING

Jesus' coming, this is certain,

'Cause it's written in the Bible;

Always faithful to His promise,

He will come back for His chosen;

Oh look, look at your dear Lord,

Also bring the ones who sleepeth;

Don't let even one be wandering,

He will come! Yes, He will come!

2

He who died on the cross with pain,

Gave His life up, never cursing;

He comes now, illuminating,

So impressive He comes on clouds;

Oh look, at your dearest Lord,

Oh look at Him come in glory;

He is crowned with splendor, victory,

He will come! Yes, He will come!

3

Crown of thrones placed around His head,

Have become red rays around Him;

And the staff they made Him carry,

Royal scepter of a kingdom;

Oh look, at your dear Lord,

Saints continue shining brightly;

Oh majestic holy angels,

He will come! Yes, He will come!

4

Oh, the one who's absent-minded,

He has not believed in Jesus;

He has dressed himself with rags,

To come to the festive weddings;

Oh look, at your dearest Lord,

For He's coming, look oh Christ's bride;

It's the precious hour ever,

He will come! Yes, He will come.
