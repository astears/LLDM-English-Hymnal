248 THERE IS A BOOK WITHOUT COMPARE

There's a book without compare, of my God on His altar,

It's my guide looking through pages all inside;

All my Brothers all around, contemplate this book with love,

Always listening to the Words of our God.

CHORUS

With great love I will guard, all the counsels of a chosen Man of God,

Through the Bible he taught me: to have faith in Jesus Christ;

Having heard it from the Spirit of the Lord.

2

It is pleasing to think my soul has been saved though Christ,

From this world I was freed from my chains of sin;

And his light descending down, choose by grace to fall on me,

Testimony of the love and grace of God.

3

What a blessing my Lord God did consider to my soul,

With all love and gratitude I will repay;

And with tender dedication , I elevate my song,

Because a chance at salvation Christ gave me.

4

I remember your sweet mercy, engrave it in mind,

Also the messages of Jesus Christ my King;

In his law I'll meditate, and forever I will guard,

All the teachings heard from the Servant of God.
