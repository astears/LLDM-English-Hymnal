132 IN A LIFE OF MANY TRIALS

In a life of many trials, be a hero,

In your anguish and your pain, be a hero;

Raise intrepidly the banner with noble majesty,

Fight in pro towards all the truth, be a hero.

CHORUS

Be a hero, put your confidence in God,

Be a hero, He will help all who are good;

Go you soldiers to the war with indomitable valor,

Till the triumph we have won, be a hero.

2

Many controversies exist, be a hero, But with Christ,

What will you fear? be a hero;

Always battling with faith to defeat obscurity,

Fight you faithful for the truth, be a hero.

3

If you see your Brother fall, be a hero,

Live you ready to go win; be a hero;

Of Lord Jesus it's your job to proclaim all of His truth,

I proclaim your kindnesses, be a hero.
