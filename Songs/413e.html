413 ALL MY PRAYERS TO THE LORD

All my prayers to the Lord sustain my soul,

Your great power sustains till the end;

And it brings to my soul a sweet calmness,

As I'm walking and following Christ.

CHORUS

Come teach us how to pray,

Thus said all the disciples to their Master;

Come teach us how to pray, We ask you here today;

We'd like to have joy and to sing, In the new land Jerusalem.

2

It's a prayer that conveys consolation,

When I fight all temptations away;

You're a guide who will lead us to heaven,

With our Savior we'll go to rejoice.

3

'Tis prayer that was ordered by Jesus,

And your holy power, we know well;

We will have you continuously with us,

Till the Pastor of Israel we see.
