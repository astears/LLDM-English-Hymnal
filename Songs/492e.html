492 IF SOMEONE HAS THE UNDERSTANDING

If someone has the understanding, they will find the basic knowledge,

Of the Father, of Jesus, and the Spirit sent from God;

Combine all three into one, and you'll see it was revealed,

All these three are actually one.

While on quicksand some build over, though unstable and unsteady,

Others on the rock made firm and eternal;

Some believe in the false doctrines, others in the word of truth,

In Christ Jesus truth is found.

CHORUS

It is Jesus the truth, the true pathway and the light,

God in Him, the plenitude of the great divinity.

2

Some accept the Lord's baptism which is found in the Lord's gospel,

Baptism, of immersion, to pardon all vile sins;

Others making rules of man, by tradition without God,

Baptize in their own ways.

Christ in Matthew 28, gives the order of baptism,

This baptism which all persons all will obey;

For this Acts 2:38, Peter speaks of Christ's baptism,

And He lets us know the truth.

3

All three Apostles in the Bible, confirm, witnessing the truth,

Baptism, in the name of our Savior Jesus Christ;

Three Apostles of the Lord, Peter, John and also Paul,

They confirm to us this light.

There are witnesses above, from the heavens shedding light,

Our Father, His words and, the Spirit of the Lord;

And this glorious mystery, John declares it to the world,

All these three are actually one.

4

God's son Jesus Christ is with us, sent from God down to be with us,

Our Father, in heaven, the Prince of all the peace;

In beginning there was Word, and the word was with our God,

And the Word was our dear Lord.

If someone has the understanding, they will find the basic knowledge,

Of the Father, of Jesus, and of the Spirit sent from God;

Combine all three into one, and you'll see it was revealed,

All these three are actually one.
