280 JERUSALEM CITY OF GOD

Jerusalem, city of God,

Beautiful place, flowing with love;

Glorious Eden, flowing with light,

Full of all peace, and glories too;

It will descend from heights of our Go,

Just like a wife dressed with beauty she'll come.

2

Construction that's unshakeable,

Is the city of our King;

The precious stones of jasper are,

Resplendent as the sun above;

Twelve doors are open for all they who search,

And at each door an angel stands guard.

3

The clarity and light of God,

There is in you great holiness;

In order to forever shine,

In day and night without an end;

Nor sun or moon light will illuminate,

Jesus the Savior, our light he will be.

4

To live in you will be my joy,

Having our God as our king;

Were death can never penetrate,

And then in peace we'll always live;

There'll be no suffering, nor crying nor pain,

Our Jesus Christ will be everything.

5

Jerusalem, a new city,

The sanctity does reign in you;

The one who's vile, speaks many lies,

Has not a chance, to enter there;

Only the ones who are just and are true,

Their names are written in the book of God.
