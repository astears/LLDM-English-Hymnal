581 NOW THE NIGHT

Now the night with all its darkness disappeared,

And the morning is now here;

And I find myself living in plenty light,

And the morning is now here.

CHORUS

What great joy is reigning in my heart today, through Jesus,

All the shadows dissipated with your love, my dear Lord;

So now joyfully I sing, because Jesus is with me,

What great joy is reigning now, deep within my heart.

2

Jesus Christ has made a new world just for me,

And the morning is now here;

And because of Him my life is great here,

And the morning is now here.

3

I will live a life of splendor someday near,

And the morning is now here;

Because I will have support only in Christ,

And the morning is now here.
