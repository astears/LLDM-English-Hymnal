513 IF OTHERS WONDER

If others wonder where you will go,

Answer delightful , to the city of God;

They'll say I never knew of this place,

//We're God's chosen people// the new Israel.

CHORUS

Oh blessed City, dwelling of my great King,

My soul greatly desires, to dwell in you;

The time has passed by quickly, the time has come again,

Oh joy divine, to see His blessed face;

The Prince of our God.

2

Beautiful temple, was built with love,

A great desire of Brother Aaron;

He did not see it, He's gone with God,

//Yet it has been built// by the Apostle of God.

3

If your disheartened, confide in God,

Have faith and courage; don't doubt the Lord;

Forgiveness He will give to all souls,

//Who come to his path// to serve our God.

4

Beautiful city, shining with light,

Who is there like you, not one is found;

Flowing from you are words of life,

//For in you we find// an Apostle of God.
