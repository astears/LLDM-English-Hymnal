501 IF I WAS REASON

If I was reason for a pain oh Jesus,

If the weak stumbled all because of me;

If I refused to walk within your pathway,

I beg forgiveness oh my Lord and King.

CHORUS

Listen oh God to my humble confession,

And free me from temptation when it's near;

Preserve my soul forever in your dear flock,

I beg forgiveness oh my Lord and God.

2

If all my words have been in vain and foolish,

If I ignored those suffering in pain;

Do not condemn me all because of my sin,

I beg forgiveness oh my Lord and God.

3

If you will help me oh my God, in your path,

To better comprehend your holy will;

Come to me Lord, and grant me more of your grace,

And come give me oh God more of your love.
