308 THE PEACE THAT MY FATHER

The peace that my Father has placed,

Peace that has enlightened my heart;

I looked in the world for this peace,

And never could I find it here.

CHORUS

Joy and gratitude are in my being,

'Cause my Lord on high gave me His spirit;

And with His grand love,

He's rescued my soul,

Oh blessed peace!

That God has placed in me.

2

Christ had suffered much on the cross,

Those moments full of anguish and pain;

He died as a vile sinner would,

With His blood He has cleansed our sins.

3

The peace that I give says the Lord,

It's not as the world gives their own;

It's not like a joy from the world,

This peace is a spiritual peace.

4

My God has forgiven my sins,

With what can I repay my God?

Today I'll be faithful to Him,

Wherever I go I'll proclaim.
