135 FROM THE HEAVENS

From the heavens an angel will come,

In the air all His voice will be heard;

That's announcing that Jesus will come,

To His people He's coming to raise.

Come descending, my celestial Lord,

Because you're a reality;

We should adore you my Lord, in our spirits and in the truth.
