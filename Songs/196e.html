196 IN A WOODEN CROSS

In a wooden cross,

Jesus Christ had died there,

He died for you and me, to give plentiful light.

CHORUS

//Come to the light, more close to Christ,

Jesus awaits you to come to light//

2

The blood of Jesus Christ, will save your soul from hell,

A brilliant light will come, to fill up all your heart.

3

Come closer to God's throne, Christ does await for you,

He wants to pardon you, and give you of His light.
