438 WHAT A PLEASURE

What a pleasure when you fight without regressing,

With valor fighting till you win the war;

Just like how you work to obtain knowledge,

You'll have to work to go up to God's throne.

CHORUS

Continue our mission it's not an illusion,

Fight on with the revealed evangel;

May all your names be written in the history,

//Oh you youth// May you be blessed!

2

//Oh you youth// continue forward,

With valor fighting as a hero would;

Continue fighting oh affectionate youth,

And the pride of the Savior you will be.

3

Oh you youth hold on firmly, lift up your sword,

The blessed doctrine of Christ Jesus Lord;

That God revealed to His Holy Servant,

To the anointed, the Apostle of God
