074 LET US SING NOW

Let us sing now with jubilee and joy,

To Christ our Lord;

May He be praised with glories from all nations,

From the whole wide world;

His hand so strong has redeemed all our spirits,

From all evilness;

He will complete His promise to all sinners too.

CHORUS

//Give praises//Let's praise Him from the earth,

//Give praises//, with joy and gratitude;

//Give praises//, His word commands us now,

The Messiah came just like it's announced.

2

The one desired from all the men on earth,

Who has many names;

The admirable and wise counselor,

He's the Prince of peace,

Our strong God and Father for all the ages,

The most Divine Judge;

All the Lord's sovereign power will reign forever, amen.

3

A son was born from our eternal father,

Our Lord Jesus Christ;

Who came to rescue all those blind in darkness,

From their slavery;

He opened for us a new glorious pathway.

Salvation we obtained;
And the church keeps on singing songs of greatest praise.
