417 OH BLESSED FATHER

Oh blessed father we come to you now,

Thanking you now for considering us;

Of your sweet presence we'd like to enjoy,

Never let us doubt a bit.

CHORUS

///Never/// let us doubt a bit,

///Always/// let us pray to God.

2

With gratitude for the gift of your love,

Hymn songs we're singing with great praise of joy;

In all our lives you are able to shine,

Never let us doubt a bit.

3

Gifts without end of the message of peace,

You with great love in Zion will give us;

Your royal priesthood we're able to win,

Never let us doubt a bit.

4

Strength grant us always when we're in the fight,

Firmly my brothers resist wickedness;

Before your presence we're able to reign,

Never let us doubt a bit.
