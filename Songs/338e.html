338 MARCH ON OH SOLDIER

March on oh Soldier, march towards the forefront,

That was prepared by Christ Jesus your Lord;

//You come with armor, and with a steel shield,

You bring a sword that's brightly shining in your hand//

CHORUS

Christ Jesus already came,

He's with His people, we hear His voice;

For this our adversary is roaring,

He is tormented, inward he burns.

//The Light of the World, will be triumphal,

And in His prison, Satan will stay//

2

This is the church which Christ Jesus purchased,

All with His death, pain, also His shed blood;

//So that from Satan, His wife would be freed,

Into His mansion He'll take her into heaven//

3

I am a soldier, I have come armored,

I'm also sealed by Jesus my Captain;

//I've come with armor, I've come with weapons,

I've come prepared to and go fight in the battle//

4

The adversary, has caused great damage,

Thousands of years he'll still be in prison;

//But those of God's church, are looking upward,

They'll go with Jesus after they've won the battle//
