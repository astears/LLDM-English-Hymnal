039 I LONG JUST TO DWELL

I long just to dwell in the mansions of light,

The place where my Savior reigns;

To hear the echoes of a sweet melody,

Triumph of greatest praise.

CHORUS

I will joyfully sing, to my excellent King,

My eyes expect to see, a beach so heavenly;

I am happy and free and I can walk along,

With jubilation to my home.

2

I might have to walk through paths lined with thorns,

The world might forget me here;

I know near the bank of the enormous sea,

The saints will be waiting there.

3

What glorious hope what ineffable peace,

I feel in my heart today;

How sweet when you are in communion and peace,

Worshiping God above.

4

Oh lift up your sight, contemplate Jesus Christ,

Be faithful to your Lord King;

The splendorous rays are surrounding the cross,

They cover you with their light.
