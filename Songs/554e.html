554 YOU'RE THE ONE WHO SAW

You're the one who saw the Apostle John,

Flying about in the heavens;

With salvation for all the peoples.

CHORUS

To every language, nation, and tribe,

Messenger, you angel, fly and announce, carrying the unction;

In this appointed time you are God's election,

From Zion the Lamb of God will come.

2

For our salvation, honor we give you,

You have brought souls to the Lord's church;

When the work of God, was placed in our souls.

3

You're the one who saw the Apostle John,

You brought to all peoples the blessing;

A treasure so rich from the Lord's word.
