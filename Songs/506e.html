506 IF THE LIGHT OF DAY

If the light of day is hidden,

And my faith has gone away;

I'll claim, elevating my plea,

Christ I know will be my guide.

CHORUS

My Pilot Christ will go with me,

And of nothing I will fear;

I won't be scared by the stormy sea,

Only in Him, I will confide.

2

Always when my boat is rowing,

In the tempest dangerous sea;

I'm able to trust in my guide,

And I'll rest on Christ's sweet bosom.

3

Through the route in which I'm rowing,

My Pilot will guide the boat;

In my conflicts and my struggles,

Triumph, glory He will give me.

4

When I reach the seashore's bank,

The celestial beach above;

He will give me a grand welcome,

My Guide unlike all the rest.
