358 LOOK HOW GOOD

Look how good and pleasant it is,

When Brothers live together in perfect unity!

•••••

It's like precious oil poured over the head,

Running down over the beard, of Aaron's beard,

Running over, down the collars of his garment.

•••••

It's as if the dew of Hermon were falling over the mountains of Zion,

For there the Lord bestows his //blessing// even life, forevermore.
