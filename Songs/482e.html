482 I WILL PLANT THE MOST PRECIOUS

I will plant the most precious of seedlings,

Of the glorious evangel of love;

//I will plant// while I'm living,

And the rest I will leave for my lord.

CHORUS

//I will plant// while I'm living, the seedlings of love,

//I will reap// when I'm found in the house of my God.

2

In the sensible hearts I'll be planting,

Of God's doctrine of pardon and peace;

//I will plant// while I'm living,

And the rest I will leave for my lord.

3

I will plant in the hearts made of marble,

All the blessings from God's word of love;

//I will plant// while I'm living,

And the rest I will leave for my lord.
