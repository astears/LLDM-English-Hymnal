314 THIS LIFE THAT I HAVE

This life that I have here,

I know I owe it to Jesus my Lord,

I have been guarded in His holy love;

And He has saved me through His immense pain.

CHORUS

Lord please, do not take your hand from me,

Remember me as I keep fighting on;

Sustain me through Christ your beloved son,

So that I may keep walking onward in your word.

2

Outrageous mistreatment, because of me the Lord suffered and bore,

All this to give us a much better life;

This way He showed us His most blessed love.

3

They taunted, cursed at Him, this is what my blessed Jesus went through,

This way they nailed Him to the horrid cross;

He suffered to bring us His truth and light.
