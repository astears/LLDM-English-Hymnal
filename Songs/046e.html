46 TO YOU MY PLEADING VOICE RISES

To you, my pleading voice rises to heaven,

My Savior Lord give me strength;

Only you can save all those a wandering,

From all their misery and sin.

CHORUS

//My Lord// all of my hope is in you,

Only you are my asylum Jesus, my sole desire is you.

2

You know my bitterness and all I suffer, Gaze upon me, when oppressed;

All of my hope I have lost in my battles,

No one can feel all my pain.

3

Humbly Christ Jesus I claim to the heavens,

Listen to me, Oh my Lord!

With my depressed soul I come calling Jesus,

In my terrible pain.

4

I'm very weak and I know sin is stronger,

Give me my God of your strength;

Once I was captive, but you've freed my being,

Do not allow me to fall.

5

All of the chosen with me will be joyful,

Your blessings can't be compared;

All of the hearts overflowing with God's grace,

'Cause You have pardoned our souls.
