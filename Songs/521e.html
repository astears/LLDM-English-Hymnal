521 WE ARE ONE BODY

We are one body in Jesus, with different gifts of love,

Missioners, deacons, pastors and congregation too;

And if as one we follow, kindness will then grow,

We won't be left alone more, Jehovah will come help us.

CHORUS

All as one loving people, all in communion,

Flowing with joy together, salvation we will find.

2

This ensemble in Christ Jesus has plenty aliment,

You are the Bishop, Jesus, of the parishioners here;

He is the architect and gives life to all men,

And He has called them children, for all eternity.

3

Jesus the head of the church, and good Shepherd He is,

From Him the promises roll, Jesus Christ, Redeemer;

This church was found by Jesus, that serves Him with all truth,

Who does love all of your words, for all eternity.

4

United, we are one body, in Jesus Christ the Lord,

Having been dead for this world, but living for the Lord;

As Christ has resurrected, we will rise as Him too,

We will enter triumphant, singing to Christ up there.
