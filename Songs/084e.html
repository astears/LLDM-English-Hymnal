084 JUST AS TIRZAH YOU'RE DESIRED

Just as Tirzah you're desired,

Just as Jerusalem, so majestic;

As troops marching with their banners, is the lovely church of God.

CHORUS

No matter if waves rise up high they will never put out,

His love nor will the rivers;

Not one man can give of all his treasury,

To buy the love that Jesus gives.

Certainly he cannot comprehend,

Because the love of Jesus Christ our Lord;

Will take us to mount Zion: place of God.

2

Your words flow like milk and honey,

You holy wife of Christ, who is faithful;

From your vestments pleasant odors, are of saffron and of myrrh.

3

Oh how beautiful the feet,

Which have been covered with the Evangel;

Work of our Teacher, the Master, Jesus Christ the most fair judge.

4

You are she that's always growing,

Just as the dawning that is arising;

With a beauty as the moonlight, shining as the sun above.
