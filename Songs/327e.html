327 THE CENTURIES HAVE PASSED

The centuries have passed and prophecies fulfilled,

And the tree of life flourished and gave fruit;

The same God who took the tree of life from Eden,

He took just a branch, only just a vine shoot;

And the Lord gave virtue, to Aaron's staff.

CHORUS

The virtue was given a mark,

Of life eternal, once man has died;

Branch of Aaron, from God of love,

Within this country has come to bloom.

2

Take note to when God said,

The God of goodness,

Every soul that I love no one takes away;

As much as the tempests move the evil winds,

I have conquered this world; this world is not able;

To take from any souls from my hands they are mine.

3

Come soul who is thirsty, leave all of your pain,

What beautiful virtues and marvels you see;

Cause up in the heavens, where the Lord reigns,

He disperses virtues and gifts to his children;

And all of his power to Christ he has gave.

4

The branch of that great tree came to Emmanuel,

And to the anointed of the new Israel;

The date has been branded it's to be remembered,

The ages have gone by, that from our fathers;

The great branch of Aaron has been delivered.
