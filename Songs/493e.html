493 IF SOMEONE IS IN JESUS

If someone is in Jesus they are a new person,

Having buried the dross once binded to their soul;

While they were in their own life serving how they thought best,

They now know work for Jesus will never be in vain.

CHORUS

Let us all follow the Lord Jesus Christ,

Only obeying Him in His command;

And He will guide us in His path of light,

And may He grant us, be a faithful church.

2

Jesus the Savior calls all the tired unto Him,

He takes away the burden the tempter placed on them;

Then freely they go out knowing nothing's in their way,

Letting the name of Jesus be known in every place.

3

The bible is our shield, instruction to guide us,

We use it as a light and it enlightens all our heart;

And the promise that Jesus has sent us in His love,

Can give us strength to follow peace, joy, and holy love.

4

Let us be singing hymns to our God with gratitude,

Through the virtue of Jesus He gave us holiness;

Let us fixate our sight on Christ who's our Captain now,

If He gives us His strength satan we will all defeat.
