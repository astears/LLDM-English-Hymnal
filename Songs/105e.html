105 JESUS CHRIST IS LOOKING

Jesus Christ is looking for laborers,

Who wants to go with Him?

Who will say, oh Lord,

I will go with you?

I want to serve you true!

CHORUS

Oh my Lord, the harvest is so great,

But laborers are few;

Give us light, a burning faith, and strength,

And laborers there'll be.

2

Jesus Christ would like some missioners too,

Announcing all His truth;

Who will say, oh Lord I'm ready to go?

I'll only do your will.

3

There's a room if you would like to work,

In part of Jesus' work;

Can you to the world speak of the Lord's love,

Of His mercy and love.

4

Daily are you living saved in Christ?

You've already felt His love;

Speak you then announce that in Jesus' light,

You have been living well.
