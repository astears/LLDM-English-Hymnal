166 THE PEOPLE VERY LOVED

The people very loved by their God,

That from past times ago, there have been men God's chosen;

Men with hearts made of fine gold, who obeyed God in full,

Who have been elected.

CHORUS

In these last days God has sent to us,

From the heavens to the earth, a great man;

With such a grand name, Aaron, God has named him so;

Full of blessing and strength, for the souls who believe Him.

2

The work that is most perfect is believe,

To accept oh dear Lord, the man you sent to our souls;

For the world, it is hard to accept,

To believe oh dear Lord, the man you have chosen.

3

And my God who with his love did lift a man,

And rose up on the earth His Holy Apostle;

In His people He lifted up a man,

Who was named Samuel Joaquin: A man with great power.

2nd. CHORUS

The world continues living foolishly,

Ignoring the man God sent to us;

With this holy election, He who comes and is close to this man;

He will have eternal life, and a place of resting.
