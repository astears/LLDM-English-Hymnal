425 PILGRIM GOING ON YOUR WAY

Pilgrim going on your way to heaven,

You will suffer no more sadness here;

You can hear the choir of the angels,

And soon you will also sing up there.

CHORUS

Oh surely you'll be there!

Where songs resound like,

Many running waters;

And for heavenly dwellings up there,

Oh but will you be there?

Where all the saved ones go;

Before the high throne,

They will be singing praises to the Lord.

2

Oh my soul listen to the voice calling,

Calling you to your sweet resting place;

Oh but will they be your final moments,

In which they invite you to go rest.

3

Jesus Christ is sun shining for justice,

He guides me to the celestial door;

Where all troubles will be terminated,

Where I will have my eternal rest.

4

Jesus Christ has opened up the heavens,

Through the blood He has shed on the cross;

Come oh sinner come Jesus is calling,

Come to Him and He will save your soul.
