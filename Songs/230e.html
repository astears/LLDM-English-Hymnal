230 JOY AND PEACE

//Joy and peace// in the house of the believer,

Joy and peace, there's life and holiness;

There's joy and liberty through Christ our General.

CHORUS

Let us all sing, let us give praise,

Blessings give to our Lord, and do say in one voice;

That Jesus Christ lives for all eternity.

2

//Joy and peace// in the church of Christ the pure Lamb,

Joy and peace from our celestial God;

The God of loving kindness gave us liberty.

3

//Joy and peace// in this blessed sanctuary,

Joy and peace from our kind God above;

Sing to Him with fervor, He has freed us from sin.

4

//Joy and peace//is the heritage of Christians,

Joy and peace enjoying well His grace;

United in His love, singing to Christ the King.
