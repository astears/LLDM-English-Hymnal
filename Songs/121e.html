121 WHEN MY GOD

When my God took all His people,

To journey in barren land;

With His strong arm He was leading,

Until they reached Canaan.

There He gave them the possession,

A place where they could reside;

But they became rebellious,

They followed false gods who once were not known.

CHORUS

Great in glory's the Lord,

He has such merciful love;

He has come to save us,

From the slavery of the Pharaoh king.

Up upon Mount Sinai, That's where His glory was seen;

That's where He gave unto Moses,

The plates where His holy laws were wrote down.

2

God's people were sentenced with curses,

Anyone who broke the law;

But clemency was born,

Born following God's law.

This was Jesus our great Lord,

From a woman He was born;

He came to give souls salvation,

From the slavery of Pharaoh the king.

3

But these rebellious people,

They did not want to obey;

In mankind they were confiding,

'Cause they thought man held the power.

Enraged were all of the people,

Against Moses they became;

They were all searching for water,

They could not find one place to quench their thirst.

4

But Moses was very patient,

And God was pleased when He saw;

Moses fall unto his knees,

And he showed a rock to God.

God asked him to touch the found rock,

With the rod he had received;

And suddenly water sprung up,

The water of life that Jesus gave him.

5

However God was their leader,

He took care of them with love;

Till they finally reached Canaan,

The land He promised to them.

Then they were all very happy,

Because the Lord cared for them;

For forty years they did wander,

But none of their garments had ever grown old.
