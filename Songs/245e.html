245 OH THERE IS A PLACE

Oh there is a place, of beauty untold,

A grand city, where sun does not shine;

Only those will go, who've been bought by Jesus,

With the blood so precious, of our Savior Christ.

CHORUS

Oh I want to go, to this city Lord,

Where the sons of God, will forever reign;

I do want to live close to all the saints,

If I have one goal, it is to live there;

With my Savior Lord.

2

The life with my Lord, will be so beautiful,

All trials will end, also sufferings too;

Only will I hear, the celestial choirs,

That will sing the glories, to our God on high.

3

Without sun or moon, it will be majestic,

With the lamb of the Lord, we'll shine like the stars;

Without cries or pains, everything will be joy,

Singing songs of glory, to Christ Jesus Lord.

4

In this place of joy, there is an Apostle,

Whose name is Samuel, by grace of our Lord;

He will take our souls, to Christ in the heavens,

Where we'll be forever, with Him and Jesus.
