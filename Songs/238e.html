238 TOWARDS SEPULCHER

Towards sepulcher they are walking, zealously,

Some pious women came looking to find Him;

The body of their dear Master, that had died,

They went with love, embalming Him who left the earth.

CHORUS

Why do you look for Him who raised up high,

He is not here, only His tomb He left;

Go tell the ones who loved Christ Jesus much,

That the Lord Jesus did defeat His death.

2

Magdalena with tears came close, with much pain,

To gardener in the orchard, she did ask;

Where did my Lord go astray to? So I go too,

I want to go enjoy His love, from all His being.

3

Thousands of Angels in heaven sing today,

The Savior of men who conquered with much strength;

No sepulchers can be withheld anymore,

The ones believing in the Lord, will be redeemed.
