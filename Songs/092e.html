092 WITH WHAT WILL WE PAY

With what will we pay Him, for His immense love,

Christ Jesus gave His life, for those who have sinned;

Oh my dear Lord Jesus, please comfort my being,

So that I am able to conquer my struggles;

And serve you my lord.

CHORUS

I could not give riches, for Christ's love He offered,

Receive this song Jesus, so full of my weeping;

From within my heart.

2

And during the nighttime, while laying in my bed,

My eyes filled with tear drops, I draw upon you;

I look to the heavens, and see constellations,

I know that past all them, there's my loving Father;

Who cares for my soul.

3

I cannot repay you, with gold, bronze, nor silver,

A sacrifice so great, that you did for me;

In exchange receive Lord, what I offer to you,

The most humble offering, a faithful heart and soul;

Oh Lord Jesus Christ.
