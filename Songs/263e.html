263 HOSANNA THE KING

Hosanna the King of glory,

For His greatness is always in glory;

In the heavens, and in the oceans,

On this earth and then in His glory.

CHORUS

Hosanna king of all glory, you're the rain that's very early,

And you are our happiness, ///Hosanna///

2

Hosanna the King of glory,

All the angels give to you their praises;

At your throne they are always kneeling,

They proclaim // Hosanna//

3

Hosanna the King of glory,

You are the star of the early morning;

You are the light; you are the dew drop,

You are the rain that is very early.

4

Hosanna the King of glory,

All the flowers just give off their perfume;

And all the birds are singing praises,

And they say //Hosanna//
