157 HOLY SPIRIT, COUSELOR FROM GOD

Holy Spirit, Counselor from God,

Brought to me a peace, comfort, and love;

Now my only pleasure will be to,

Bless God's dearest name and Jesus too.

CHORUS

I will fight then just as a firm soldier,

Holding firm on to the sword in my hands;

'Cause He came to give, me celestial life,

Holy Spirit counsel of the Lord.

2

Now the Lord has considered to me,

That I carry His cross made of wood;

Now He's giving to me a great strength,

So that I will fight for the Lord's name.

3

Faithfully I want to serve you,

Only waiting for your sovereign pow'r;

For you want to find your faithful flock,

Only fulfilling your holy will.
