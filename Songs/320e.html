320 YOUR PRAISES WE'LL GIVE YOU

Your praises we'll give you in the clouds,

A song of jubilee will sound;

The multitudes of angels reigning,

So joyfully they all will sing.

CHORUS

Hallelujah, to the Lamb, tell the universe entirely,

Because a loud resound is now heard; It is a trumpet celestial,

And the dead in Jesus Christ, will all awake;

So that they receive immortal life.

2

Oh where has your sting gone to oh death,

Where oh sepulcher is your victory;

For this will be a brand new report,

In the great mansions of glory.

3

Do come, the blessed of my Father,

Are words of the Prince of all peace;

Come to your inherited great riches,

To be joyful forever amen!
