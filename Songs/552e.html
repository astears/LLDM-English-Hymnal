552 YOU KNOW LORD

You know Lord how I am, how useless are my ways,

You know Lord, how I am, how imperfect my heart.

CHORUS

Augment more of my faith, and give me understanding,

That where your hand may take me,

I may serve as example.

2

You know Lord how I am, so lagging in my prayers,

You know all my imperfections and all my faults and doubts.

3

If you help my Lord, to fight against my flesh,

If you help me my Lord, with your great sovereign power.

4

If you help me my Lord, to persevere in love,

To make me meek and humble, and have a heart so pure.

5

Oh Lord, give me your grace, to triumphantly fight,

Not repaying evil, for evil done to me.

6

If you help me my Lord, I will announce your coming,

And say to all the peoples, the news of your salvation.
