004 TO JESUS I WILL ALWAYS SING

To Jesus I will always sing with all my heart and soul,

With all the congregation of His glory I will sing;

The miracles that Christ performed the people should come seek,

By everyone who wants to see the glory of His light.

CHORUS

// Honor and love, honor and love, honor and love,

To Jesus Christ I'll sing with all my heart //

2

The deeds of Jesus Christ my Lord are far beyond compare,

They mention His compassion to all those who are in need;

His promise to humanity He will faithfully keep,

To those accepting help from Christ, inheritance He'll give.

3

Our King of Zion's deeds are true, they're just and full of love,

His faithful laws have well been kept and passed from age to age;

Redemption Christ has given to the people of his faith,

His promise firm has always been and never will be changed.

4

In admiration with great fear every being will see,

Mankind will walk towards the knowledge of the Lord;

And the devoted with great fear, His name they will adore,

And through the centuries they'll sing, with glories to the Lord.
