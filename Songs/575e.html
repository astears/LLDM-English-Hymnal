575 I CAME TO CHRIST

I came to Christ and by grace He did saved,

I took the cross of the blessed Savior Lord;

And now all I need is your help to persevere,

May I always also restrain my tongue.

CHORUS

All this world can offer mankind, is perdition for His soul,

Salvation our Lord Jesus Christ will offer to us;

Sustain my soul oh Lord, do not ever let it perish,

And help me so that I may see your face one day.

2

There is a God and a Mediator too,

Who takes away our evil and our sins;

He came to save me from the depths of sin,

And so I follow the only path of truth.

3

There's a commandment that our Savior left for us,

And with the help of the Lord we must comply;

He asked us to pray for one another always,

So that the Lord may clean His people from all sin.

4

Take away from me all quarrels and all rage,

Every dissension, and every imperfection;

And may you clean my heart every day Lord,

Allow me to love all my brothers equally.
