001 OPEN UP YOUR HEARING

Open up your hearing; waken up your pious sense,

Keep His faithful teachings deep within your heart;

He who soothes the soul ought thou perceive,

And from Jesus, a treasure you'll receive.

CHORUS

God sent His Apostle, we shall not forget Him,

For He steeped this world, with spiritual health;

His memory will always, live within our minds,

His wise humble teachings, teachings of our God.

2

I am the truest of grape vines; my Father's the laborer,

Every branch that does not bear fruit in my vineyard, shall be removed;

We shall guard your words of wisdom,

We shall be obedient to your voice.

3

He brought a doctrine from heaven, preached with great authority,

What He's bound on earth, is firmly bound in heaven;

He has laid a firm foundation,

In the Church of our Lord.
