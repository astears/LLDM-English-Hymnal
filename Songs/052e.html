052 COME TO REVIVE OUR FRAGILE BEING

Come to revive our fragile being,

And of your kindness we will sing;

As we go running through your path;

Christ in His kindness will bless us.

CHORUS

Come ,my Lord, to give light, with your might encourage us,

Come, my Lord, come to teach, your commandments;

To your people.

2

Just like a lost sheep I once was,

Wandering lost without the Lord;

Oh come restore my soul for you,

And towards your mansion lead my steps.

3

Now I extend my hands to you,

Asking for your paternal grace;

Just like the land that thirsts in drought,

Thirsting for fresh heavenly rain.

4

Come, I implore my Savior come,

Come to revive my heart I pray;

And in your loving name descend,

Into my soul to be redeemed.

5

You are my God and so your will,

You will teach me your holy law;

And you will lead me through your path,

Filled with serenity and peace.

6

Your light of blessing will now shine,

Mercy and grace will glow so bright;

Within my soul and in my heart,

Which from your hand I have received.
