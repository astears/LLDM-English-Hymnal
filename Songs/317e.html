317 I SHOULD SEE IT

I should see it, When? I don't know,

A world with an immense horizon;

Where I can find His throne that's hidden,

I will have comfort, I'll have peace.

CHORUS

There I will see, His face so lovely,

There I will see, His marvelous face;

Following Christ who went before me,

I will have comfort, I'll have peace.

2

A few more years on earth don't matter,

The time so quickly's passing by;

His loving face I'll see in the end,

I will see God's face on His throne.

3

I know all life will pass away,

The flowers past the rivers flourish;

May happiness defeat our deep pain,

And everything has its own end.
