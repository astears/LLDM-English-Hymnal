118 WITH JUBILEE WHEN I LEAVE

With jubilee when I leave all this world behind me,

My King in glory will accept my soul;

A gold crown of life eternal He'll place on me,

I will reign there for all eternity.

CHORUS

//My King// with you my King I'll go Lord,

For you I will live,

//My soul will proclaim its joy//

2

I will wait patiently for all the things He's promised,

Though I am suffering hard down below;

And I will be compensated in God's glory,

For all my sufferings I bared for Him.

3

I will be using all the talents the Lord gave me,

Because I know He soon will come back down;

In this cruel world, I am living as a pilgrim,

I look for love, the love of my God.
