396 OH JEHOVAH HOW GREAT

Oh Jehovah, how great is your name,

My soul is longing ardently with desire;

The courts of Lord Jesus Christ,

Then to dwell in wickedness.

CHORUS

Oh how lovely are your dwellings, oh God,

They give me force and strength;

And they come to comfort my soul,

Through your Holy Spirit Lord.

2

Blessed are all those who are abiding,

In the house of the King of all glory;

Contemplating the glory of God,

As they worship the kind God of love.

3

Just one day amongst your courts is better,

Than a thousand years far from your grace Lord;

I would rather choose to be with God,

Than to be in wickedness.

4

See oh God our strength and force on this earth,

My soul is enjoying your sweet grace Lord;

We are dwelling in your house oh God,

We are hearing the voice of love.
