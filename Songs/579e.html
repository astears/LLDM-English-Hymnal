579 I'M A PILGRIM

I'm a pilgrim, going to heaven,

To forever live with Jesus Christ my Lord;

He's opened for me, the truthful pathway,

When He expired for us upon the cross.

CHORUS

I'm a pilgrim, going to heaven,

To forever live with Jesus Christ my Lord.

2

Mourning death, and the herbs so bitter,

//Never// will we ever suffer there;

A happy life full of glory, blessings,

All of my soul will enjoy the peace of God.

3

Holy country, pure with beauty,

I will enter through the gates saved by Lord Christ;

I will be joyful, in all my fortune,

With Him we will live forever in His light.
