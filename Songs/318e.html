318 GIVE PRAISE

Give praise to the immortal invisible King,

Give praise to the most sovereign God and the King;

Bring crowns of royalty to Emmanuel,

Holy homage bring now to our Savior Lord.

CHORUS

//Worthy// is the celestial Lamb of God,

Earth and heaven praise they will tribute to Him;

Crown Him, crown Him with universal lordship,

Glory to the undefeated; Christ the redeeming Lamb.

2

The one reigning forever, where the sun shines,

Who's right hand is giving this world all its strength;

Joyous eternal fountain blessed and rich,

Let all men united faithfully give Him praise.

3

Come and crown the one who is the faithful Prince,

All those Christians who worship so fervently;

Holy angels now render their praise to Him ,

Hymns and hosannas to Christ the Lord we will sing.
