229 SO JOYFULLY I GO

So joyfully I go to my home in heaven, //walking forward//

Never do I want to be left down here;

Walking forward to that place my home.

CHORUS

//Walking forward// to the place where Christ is dwelling with God,

//Walking forward// holding on to Jesus Christ's hand.

2

Leaving all the world behind I march on with joy,

//walking forward// Jesus promised always to be near my side;

Walking forward to that place, my home.

3

I would also like to take sinners to the Lord, //walking forward//

Because Jesus would like to save all the souls;

Walking forward to that place my home.

4

Very joyful contemplating Jesus Christ my Lord// in His glory//

Forever in the glory with the saints I'll praise;

In that place full of joy and peace.
