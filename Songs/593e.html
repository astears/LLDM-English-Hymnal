593 A NEW LIGHT HAS JUST ARISEN

A new light has just arisen, a brand new day has just appeared,

The church was lost in the wilderness;

It has appeared all full of rejoicing.

CHORUS

Let's all leave iniquity, let's all stop worshipping idols,

A new light has come upon us, the light of the day has come up.

2

All of you who are so thirsty, all of you come to this great fountain,

You can have all this life eternal;

And rejoice in your heavenly home.

3

With great power they will see you, for with the truth you will overtake them,

Get more strength so your wings grow more;

Jesus Christ is now your bridegroom.

4

If angels are our guardians, then rains of gifts we will all have,

All of your children will then acclaim thee;

For you are a mother and queen of peace.
