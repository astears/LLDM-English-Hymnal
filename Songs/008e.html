008 THIS WILL BE MY FINAL FAREWELL

This will be my final farewell my friends,

I've been separated from this world;

Soldier of my Christ I've been converted,

Christ is coming down, with Him I'll go.

CHORUS

//Glory// to God we'll be singing,

//Hallelujah// amen;

My parents and my brothers all are staying,

This because they never did obey.

2

I'll say farewell forever on that day,

When my Jesus comes to take us up;

All His redeemed church who He has chosen,

Now forever more they'll dwell with Him.

3

Farewell to this world and all its treasures,

When the land and sea will melt away;

Farewell wealthy people and you brokers,

Never more you'll contemplate my face.

4

Farewell mocking people and you teasers,

Jehovah our God will also laugh;

Joyfully and happy we will all be,

All races and nations He will judge.

5

Those who won this day would like to enter,

All God's flock: the sanctified and washed;

The most holy water they'll be drinking,

So that He can save them on this day.
