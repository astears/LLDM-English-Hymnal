319 PRAISES TO YOU LORD

Praises to you my Lord I give on this night,

For all the blessings you have given this day;

Oh save me and that your wings always serve as,

The mighty shelter that I always turn to.

CHORUS

How many faults oh Lord have I committed?

Forgive me my Lord through your Son beloved;

With you and with my neighbor and with myself,

Before I sleep I can be reconciled.

2

Oh teach me how to live and have no fear of,

A tomb more than the bed in which I rest now;

Teach me how I should die so that I'm able,

To wake with glory on the Day of Judgment.

3

May my soul be allowed to rest in you Lord,

May my eyes be shut and dream sweetest dreams;

And dream to give me vigor and much courage,

So that I serve you my only creator.
