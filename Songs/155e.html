155 THE COUNSEL OF GOD

The counsel of God, is life eternal,

The counsels of the Lord, become commandments;

Confide in Jesus Christ in every moment,

He'll comfort every care and you'll gain knowledge.

CHORUS

Put your delight in Jesus Christ and you will have,

All the petitions and the wishes you desire;

Come to Christ Lord, with all your heart,

Always proclaim to the Lord.

2

When with great pain your soul, has been afflicted,

Praise God no matter what, all of your lifetime;

And when your pain is gone, and you walk happy,

Wait for the Lord with all your heart, in every moment.

3

Always praise your Lord God, content and happy,

You will never forget, one of his mandates;

Mercy and peace you'll find, full in your soul,

Wait for the Lord with all your heart, in every moment.

4

When poverty surrounds and strains your being,

Take up your cross and go, that is God's order;

The hope of a poor man, is Lord Christ Jesus,

Wait for the Lord with all your heart, in every moment.
