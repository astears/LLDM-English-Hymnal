207 IT IS JESUS WHO MAKES ME SO HAPPY

It is Jesus who makes me so happy,

It is Jesus who gives peace to my soul;

When in sadness or pain He gives me joy,

And with Him everything is happiness.

CHORUS

In Jesus pains and woes are all forgotten,

It's only Christ my Lord, who gives us peace;

Oh sinner don't forget, He gives you gladness,

Only in Jesus Christ you will have joy.

2

Your life was very sad as a sinner,

Living so far away from the Lord;

See Him lovingly calling with His voice,

He would like to bring you to His flock.

3

If you're living today far from Jesus,

There is still a relief for your pain;

It's His voice who is constantly calling,

Sinner don't harden your heart anymore.

4

I was also in the path of evil,

I was sad I was weak with great pain;

In all ways I offended my Jesus,

But now I have his love and his peace.
