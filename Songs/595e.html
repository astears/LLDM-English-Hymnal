595 I COME LORD JESUS

I come Lord Jesus, to be with you Lord,

'Cause in my heart I do feel your sweet love;

'Cause with the price of your blood, my Jesus Christ,

I feel that you rescued me from my enemies.

CHORUS

If you're departing, and are far from God,

In the end don't say that God did not forewarn you;

If you're departing, I will still go,

'Cause in the heavens a palace I own.

2

Come with me Brother If you want to go,

To all the mansions prepared since times afar;

'Cause many people's would like to follow Christ,

Indeed if they follow they will live with Christ.

3

Glory to Jesus, the son of our God,

'Cause He has saved me through all of His love;

I won't deny Him, never He is my Lord,

'Cause I know it's Jesus, the Savior of this world.

4

I feel the crown being placed on my head,

And my white vestment which He will give me;

But I am sad that some Brothers stayed behind,

Whose souls will all perish 'cause they have disobeyed.

5

Only a memory I want to have here,

Only through Jesus my soul will be saved;

And if I follow confiding till the end,

So then with Lord Jesus, I will go to live.

6

There's one more thing that I would like to tell,

That in the heavens, Christ begs God for you;

But if you follow and never turn around,

'Cause He is the pathway, and I will live up there.
