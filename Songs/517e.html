517 ONLY BY FAITH

Only by faith in Christ there's salvation,

And consolation the soul can feel;

Christ is the dawning, He is the morning,

That brings all mankind their salvation.

Search in the Bible and read His word,

Because it tells us the truth;

And you'll find that Jesus Christ came,

To give us our liberty.

2

Do not be despising the great message,

Open the doors that lead to your heart;

With a new robe you should be attired,

Leaving behind the corrupted robe.

Jesus Christ can even change your language,

If you serve Him truthfully;

And eternal life He gives you,

In the dwellings of the Lord.

3

Now is the time of the greatest promise,

That which my Savior has offered us;

He came to give us strength and endurance,

And then to guide His people in love.

Purity Christ gives to all His children,

And security as well;

He removes all of their sadness,

Glory to the name of God.

4

In this world we are all very poor,

And persecuted with greatest fear;

All of the names are found up in heaven,

Of those who suffer for our Lord.

That's why Jesus wants the courageous,

If an effort they will make;

So that they may take His message,

To a place it may be heard.
