570 WITH JOY TODAY

With joy today, enjoy communion too,

Oh Church of Jesus Christ, we have a prize up high;

And praying without cease, singing unto the Lord,

In this new house of blessing, the house of prayer and praise.

CHORUS

Come marching all you hosts, you troops of Israel,

We give His praise and to Emmanuel sing;

The angels of the Lord, will also come with Him,

So joyfully, content, to Christ we give the praise.

2

Come to the faithful flock, that Jesus Christ has bought,

Through mocking He prevailed, He bared it on the cross;

What can I give my Lord? Only my heart and mind,

In this new house of blessing, the house of prayer and praise.

3

Come with one voice to God, and our song will be sung,

Song to our Savior Lord, giving peace to our souls;

One body we are now, through favor of the Lord,

In this new house of blessing, the house of prayer and praise.
