090 WITH GRATITUDE OH HOLY GOD

With gratitude oh holy God,

I bless your sympathy my Lord;

There is in my soul, the most sweetest song,

That only you know how to love.

CHORUS

Your kindness is forever God,

Forever to us is your love;

Nothing compares to your sweet grace,

Because you only are of love.

2

With gratitude I come to you,

I at you feet surrender praise;

I bring my being cleansed by your bloodshed,

That's full of joy and liberty.

4

We should be thinking in our minds,

The sacrifice of Jesus Christ;

Because He loved the most vile sinner: me,

He carried my faults on the cross.

5

A pleasing offering of praise,

That in your presence I will give;

With the perfume of your immense love,

Forever joyfully I'll live.
