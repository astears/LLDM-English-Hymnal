067 WITH OR WITHOUT THE SUN

With or without the sun, whether its winter,

Go through the mountains, through thickets and plain lands;

Just as Christ did in Galilee with his word,

It is your mission to plant if you're Christian.

2

Plant the Lord's divine truth in all the places,

Plant it with pain or even when in toils;

For the Lord will in its time give life to it,

Keep planting it will give fruits up to heaven.

3

Perhaps at some point your heal will be injured,

This because of the thorns lining the pathway;

Do you not see the crown of thorns on Jesus,

That fatal day He wore it on his forehead.

4

Keep planting though you're going through such hardships,

Let the seed fall on the rock that is sterile;

It tends to be that grace will find a way through,

The seed will bloom over the pride of humans.

5

Plant in the sand of deserts that are barren,

Where the soft dew of mornings' not descending;

You will encourage many hearts close to death,

With that life that your love will be igniting.

6

And in the region of doubt and of suffering,

Where the sun's rays have never given their light;

Give out the seed in handfuls to the people,

Give virtue faith and charity to all souls.

7

Keep planting do not let your ego stop you,

Plant where ungodliness is thrown with evil;

Throughout the path in this abyss of evil,

The flower of hope will bloom because of your work.

8

It will not matter if the worker tires,

Even before the seed breaks through the soil;

When having slept in the tomb it awakens,

It shall then find the seed kept up in heaven.
