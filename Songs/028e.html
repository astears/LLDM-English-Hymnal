028 TO THE KING OF KINGS

To the King of kings Jehovah the glorious,

We sing in communion with peace and happiness;

With great reverence let us humbly approach Him,

Exalting his name with true hearts and souls.

CHORUS

Joy and peace are deep within my heart,

His grace has saved my soul, salvation now awaits for me;

It's my pleasure to serve my Lord God,

Complete His will and always live for Him in holiness,

Oh what sweet hope He encourages all His church to have in

their hearts,

The pathway that the Master;

Marked for us, with His model here,

And there in the far distance, when labor has its end,

We will give praise to Jesus, our Savior and our King.

2

To the King of kings; Christ Jesus the beloved,

Sublimely, triumphant He will come back again;

With all of His saints with their jubilant hymn songs,

Someday all his church will be united to Him.

3

To the Holy Spirit; so faithful and blessed,

Let us give a welcome to the celestial guest;

And then with His presence; His virtue and power,

All pain, sin and evil will then cease to exist.

4

To the only wise God, the only almighty,

Let everyone give honor, reverence, and majesty;

And all His great blessings received in our favor,

Accept our humble service, blessed Savior Lord.
