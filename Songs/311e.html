311 THE TENDER VOICE

The tender voice of my dear Lord,

When He speaks, His words move us;

Come to the doctor who has love,

That gives life to the dead ones.

CHOURS

Christ has all power from up high,

He can heal all your sicknesses;

Full of grace, loving kindness too,

It's Jesus Christ our Savior.

2

Oh you must trust in Jesus Christ,

He who has always helped us;

He'll heal your faith, and give you peace,

Free you from any evil.

3

The deaf will hear, the blind will see,

It's Jesus Christ who's speaking;

The lame and crippled will all walk,

Through power in Christ Jesus.

4

All of these signs will then appear,

To all of the believers;

Evil demons will be cast out,

His people will be healed.
