040 BEFORE THE BREAK OF A DAWNING

Before the break of a dawning I do come to you oh Lord,

So my soul can find its comfort, and I may carry your cross;

You're the gateway towards heaven, where your palace we will find,

Serving you is my desire from the bottom of my heart.

CHORUS

With your hand you will sustain me, till the day you will return,

Oh my Lord, my sovereign King, only you can give us life;

My soul leaps with jubilation, when I feel that I am blessed,

In the nighttime or the daytime, give your holy peace to me.

2

I have nothing I can offer, come today and take my heart,

For my joy will be to see you, as we reach your holy place;

Where you go I'll always follow, with a pure and holy heart,

You're my only consolation throughout all eternity.
