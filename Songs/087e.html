087 WITH STRONG FAITH AND GREAT HOPE

With strong faith and a great hope, I walk along,

Until I reach Mount Zion, Lord;

While suffering and rejoicing always advancing,

Until I see my Lord Jesus.

CHORUS

In the meantime as a pilgrim,

I shall not weaken or faint;

I shall always follow Thee Lord,

To the grand celestial place.

2

If thorns and a great ambush are in my pathway,

Scaring my life with a great pain;

It doesn't really matter, I keep on walking,

Until I see my Lord Jesus.

3

I shall have many setbacks, no doubt about it,

Because they're put by Satan's hosts;

But Jesus Christ my Savior, will be by my side,

To triumph over them with strength.
