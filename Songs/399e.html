399 OH CHRISTIAN YOUTH

Oh Christian youth of Jesus proceed forward,

Fighting to save all sinners of this world;

Rewards up in the heavens you'll be earning,

And you'll reign with the Lord of all Lords in heaven.

CHORUS

Do not allow another to take your crown,

Which Jesus has prepared only for you;

This will be for all the righteous in glory,

When the trumpet blows its final sound.

2

If tribulation seems to be fatiguing,

All your footsteps the enemy is watching closely;

Give one look up to Jesus and instantly,

The Lord of Lords will give you consolation.

3

Together we come to give you an honor,

You've been guarded by the grace of Jesus Christ;

Who went right from the garden to the Calvary,

To wash our sins away up on the cross.

4

Oh Church of Jesus Christ keep marching forward,

Guarded by the grace of Jesus your Lord;

And at the end of our lives here on this earth,

A gold crown of glory we'll receive from Jesus.
