204 I HAD HEARD OF YOUR WORD

I had heard of your word so divinely,

I accepted your message of love;

I had walked faithfully in your pathway,

But much later the tempter reached me.

CHORUS

Oh my God do have mercy on my soul,

As with Peter you did out at sea;

Save me God before I perish,

God of kindness, my Father above.

2

Oh how sad is the life of a brother,

Who departs from the pathway of God;

Who abandons their way to the heavens,

Due to walking in dark evil ways.

3

There's no peace in this world marked with hatred,

Never peace or a joy can be found;

Only Jesus has offered them to us,

He has offered them for eternity.
