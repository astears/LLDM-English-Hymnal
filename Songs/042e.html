042 TO MAKE READY THE DWELLINGS

To make ready the dwellings, He went up to the sky,

His redeemed people all give Him glory and give praise.

CHORUS

///When the horn blows above/// the world will tremble too.

2

A royal chosen priesthood, our God will make His flock,

Conforming to His promise, to all of us He made.

3

The angels of the heavens come with the Sovereign Lord,

All singing Hallelujahs to Savior Jesus Christ.

4

The golden streets and sidewalks, a sea of crystal too,

Every doors' a pearl, how sweet to live up there.

5

In those streets of gold, my Savior Jesus Christ,

His face lights up the city, with His divine light.

6

And after a thousand years of reigning with my Lord,

With Him we'll live forever in His divine light.
