025 YOUR LIGHT WAS SHINING BRIGHT

Your light was shining bright, with your death on the cross,

After a gasp you went to heaven, you saved my soul from death.

CHORUS

All your sores of pain have freed me,

With your bloodshed you have cleansed me;

You have sealed me with your Spirit, with your glory I sing happily,

You have saved my soul from dying.

2

You suffered for my soul, you drank the bitter herbs,

I suffer now for you Christ, I wish to die like you.

3

Your yoke is easy Lord, your load is very light,

Make me so meek and humble, so I won't perish here.

4

I ask for charity, hear my plea and my prayer,

I want to truly love you, with a heart as pure as gold.
