470 HE RESURRECTED GIVE THE NEWS

He resurrected! Give the news,

To this world who witness His death;

He did take our place on the cross,

However He rose from the tomb.

CHORUS

Why do you search for Jesus there?

Among the dead He does not lay;

Mourn Him no more, rejoice and sing,

Proclaim that Christ lives and in heaven He reigns now.

2

Sadly they saw Him buried there,

All those who held their faith in Him;

They thought that all their hope had died,

Believing it was buried too.

3

The tomb was not capable of,

Retaining within its prisons;

King Jesus Christ who had prevailed,

The death along with all its might.

4

He's resurrected now we know,

The faithful ones will not have fear;

For if they die they will have life,

Only those who believe in Him.
