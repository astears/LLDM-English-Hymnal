100 JESUS IN HIS MISSION

Jesus in his mission here on this earth,

One day with tears so sad in His soul, saw the city.

CHORUS

Oh //Jerusalem//, city of God,

I cry for you with all of my heart;

Just as birds care for all their young ones, I bring to you,

Salvation now to all of your souls.

2

Oh Jerusalem the beauteous who killed the prophets,

Servants and all those, God sent His people.

3

As a hen who gathers all her children,

So is the Lord with all of His children: May He shield me.

4

A bright shining church that has risen,

With one side injured, from spears of evil, from enemies.

5

He who is protected by Christ Jesus,

Will be found ready, and go with Jesus, up to His home.
