114 WHEN I WAS FOUND

When I was found by my Lord, lost in wickedness and sin,

He gave to me liberty, by extending me His hand;

I am grateful to my Lord, for His liberty and grace,

Jesus has given me life, by His death upon the cross!

CHORUS

Liberty, I attained, He would not leave me astray,

Liberty, through His love, for He is greatest in power;

Oh my blessed Jesus Christ.

2

My soul was wandering and lost, in this world of wickedness,

But the Lord sheltered my soul, in my hour of disgrace;

For this I am so joyful, deep inside my grateful heart,

And I have promised to serve Him, in utmost humility.

3

My most divine Redeemer, Thou are great and powerful,

Forever guard me in your love, and I'll serve most fervently;

Your promise has been to love the most wicked man of sin,

Salvation he may obtain, for his soul eternally.

4

I am prostrate at your feet, forgiveness I come to seek,

For this world of sin I leave, and give you my heart O Lord;

I want to be subservient, this I promise you my Lord,

For me you have shed your blood, when they nailed you to the cross.
