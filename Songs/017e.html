017 TOWARDS THE LIGHT

//Towards the light// till we meet with Jesus Christ,

For Him my desire blazes;

Oh what joy when I'll be with Him,

Who for me endured the cross.

2

//Don't delay// 'cause the Prince of Peace is coming,

With all those, saved in Jesus;

Rescued by His holy bloodshed,

There I'll contemplate your face.

3

//Oh how sweet// are the songs of all the angels,

If I had their wings then maybe;

I would fly away to heaven,

To the hills of Mount Zion.

4

//What will be// when in Zion we will enter,

Where the streets of gold are shining;

Where the chorus will receive me,

Oh what joy awaits me there.

5

//Supreme greatness// celestial Jerusalem,

Holy fountains; trees of life;

Harps will be played giving welcome,

Take us God, to your Eden.
