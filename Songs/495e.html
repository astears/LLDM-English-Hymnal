495 IF YOU ARE CROSSING

If you are crossing through the darkest valley,

Without light in your path is so fatiguing;

If no one has responded to your clamor,

Do not fear for the Lord is at your side now.

CHORUS

Bring to the Lord your troubles and your worries,

And come to Him with all of your impureness;

In His immense kindness He will cleanse you now,

He will fill you with holy love and His grace.

2

Christ Jesus is a living fount of comfort,

He calms all of the burdens of this life;

He brought just like a dove coming from heaven,

The balsam of peace for the injured souls here.

3

Are you able to hear the loving calling,

Of Christ the innocent Lamb of our God;

He came so He could rescue you with His blood,

They mocked Him as He died giving us His life.
