326 ALL THOSE WHO HAVE FOUND LIFE

All those who have found life in Jesus,

Rejoice with their Savior and King;

They have joy and gladness giving praise to Him.

CHORUS

Communion, oh yes, communion, oh yes,

The Church of Christ rejoices with Him, oh yes.

2

Oh blessed seed of our Jesus, Come give praises to Him;

As you sing your praises with most holy zeal.

3

Rejoicing in life with our Jesus, All misery forget;

How sweet and how holy is our good King.
