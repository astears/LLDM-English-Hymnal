461 I WANT TO DIE

I want to die while I'm fighting in battle,

I want to suffer like Jesus my Lord;

I want to reign with Christ up in glory,

I want to go with Him.

CHORUS

//And when up in glory, you will see a crown on me,

And singing songs of praise to Jesus King//

2

I want to see Jesus Christ in heaven,

Then I will be victorious like Him;

I wish I could be at the side of Jesus,

I will not suffer more.

3

I want to meet Jesus Christ when I die,

Fighting hard always in the battlefield;

Although my life could be in risk of danger,

I will give it to Christ.
