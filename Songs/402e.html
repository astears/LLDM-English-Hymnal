402 OH BE PROCLAIMING

Oh be proclaiming with much gladness,

He's coming in glory;

Whether during the day or at night,

To this world He will soon return.

CHORUS

Announcing the story, Jesus the Savior in full glory;

With triumph and victory, To this world He will soon return.

2

Do not grow weary don't be fainting,

He's coming in glory;

The light is shining brighter each day,

And He will be appearing soon.

3

With faith and hope we should be praying,

He's coming in glory;

Oh Lord your people won't be resting,

We will continue watching on.

4

You'll see that He will keep His promise,

He's coming in glory;

Let it not be that He'll surprise you,

Let us continue watching on.

5

Oh let us give the King a welcome,

He's coming in glory;

Await for Him and keep your light on,

In glory He'll be reigning on.
