339 GLORIOUSLY MARCH OH YOU

Gloriously march oh you the youth,

Combat against the enemy;

Even though you might have to die for Christ your Lord,

Do not be frightened by your trials;
When you preach of our Savior, Only our Lord is

able to save the lost souls.

CHORUS

Fight for Lord Jesus Christ,

Without fear, without fright;

Look at the ones without Jesus,

They walk without God, salvation too.

2

Of every member you will make,

As warrior with a strong heart;

Without fear to antagonize the enemy,

Let him know that Lord Jesus Christ;

He was a conqueror too,

Always with Him at His side we'll go marching on.

3

When all the members of God's church,

Go out to work in God's field;

Full of God's spirit, full of grace, wisdom too,

So that with valor they will march;

Against all evilness and sin,

Until we reach, victory of all the truth.
