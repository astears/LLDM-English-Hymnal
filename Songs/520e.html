520 WE ARE STRONG SOLDIERS OF THE WAR

We are strong soldiers of the war, with triumph we'll viciously fight,

With the sword full of God's faith, against the world of evilness;

Nothing will harm us as we fight, Christ Jesus will be our Captain,

In His name there is much power, to fight Satan well and clear.

CHORUS

All the fighting here will end, and there will be peace,

We'll be sitting on the shore, and be resting there;

All those in the battle faithfully fighting Lord,

A prize in the end they'll have, of Jesus their Lord.

2

With the shield of faith in hand, the darts of evil cannot strike,

Because to all the sinners here, God will provide us with a shield;

If in obedience we walk, and always guard His laws of love,

He has promised us the victory, each and every day, praise Him.

3

If you feel weak when you go fight, and feel your strength is coming down,

Jesus is offering you the strength, the victory and valor too;

After with songs of praise to Him, against the spirits we will fight,

Rescuing souls from all their evil, they who walk in all their sins.
