203 DO LISTEN, OH MY GOOD LORD

Do listen, oh my good Lord,

The pains of this vile sinner;

I do suffer many harsh pains,

Trials the enemy has given me.

I don't want to follow pathways,

Of losses, anguish, or hate;

Wipe away Lord all my crying of my vile heart,

I'm imploring your forgiveness.

CHORUS

My implorations, hear oh my Lord,

I want to serve you with all my heart and soul;

Come to my soul! Soon come to reign!

What I ask you Father, you can give to my being.

2

Oh Lord I am feeling sadness,

Tired of my endless battles;

I have lost all peace in my heart,

I've not tried to find tranquility.

I feel so miserable my Lord,

As cause of all of my sin;

Consider me oh my Father, in all my soul,

To be joyful in your love.

3

Christ Jesus, so full of sweetness,

I adore you, oh my good Lord;

You are my celestial portion,

You have brought to me many blessings.

Do listen Christ to my crying,

I elevate it with love; Accept it,

Lord as a fragrance, up to heaven,

Just as a sweet-scented flower.
