104 JESUS IS OUR CAPTAIN

Jesus is our captain, with him we will fight,

In his holy name we'll, always go to war;

For the holy 'cause, we should go and fight,

Faithfully we'll follow him.

CHORUS

Going forward, its order of the Lord,

Going forward, let's go without fear;

Going forward, sweetly singing too,

The victory is ours with the King.

2

It is our victory, Jesus Christ in God,

In His holy name we'll battle without fear;

Nothing will surround us, fearless we shall go,

And we'll always conquer here.

3

One day we were baptized, In Christ Jesus' name,

And He saved our souls, by dying on the cross;

He has sealed us with His Holy Spirit too,

Faithfully we'll follow Him.

4

Our banner doth shineth wherever we go,

With might and much glory they'll always be seen;

Jesus is our captain, He conquered the world,

Faithfully we'll follow Him.

5

The struggle we're battling, will not feel so long,

In His holy name we'll, always sing a song,

When we congregate to sing a triumph hymn,

Faithfully we'll follow Him.
