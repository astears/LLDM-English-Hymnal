073 I WILL SING

I will sing from all of my soul to my King,

I will give glory, exalting Him;

He brought peace to earth, and goodwill to the mortal souls,

News of salvation wherever do tell the world.

CHORUS

I am so happy in Jesus,

'Cause He has saved my dear soul,

Angels are singing so joyful, for He has redeemed our souls;

Oh what a joy is to live life, calmly so full of the faith,

I am a pilgrim on this earth;

But I walk with Jesus deeply in my heart.

2

On the cross in Mount Calvary His flesh ached,

He drank the cup full of bitter herbs;

And as He expired all the light of sun was dimmed,

He was more than a conqueror when He rose up high.

3

Jesus Christ, ascended far beyond the skies,

For me He's pleading with tender love;

Soon the Lord will come to this earth again for the saints,

Glories ineffable will be to reign with Him.

4

What will be in the mansions above of light,

When face to face I, will look at Christ;

And with joy with Christ as we enjoy the greatness there,

And many pleasures the faithful will obtain there.
