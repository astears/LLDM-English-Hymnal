162 THE WORLD IS A COUNTRYSIDE

The world is countryside of love,

Where Jesus Christ has ordered to preach;

And throughout its parts, we should all be preaching,

The word of salvation.

CHORUS

Let's proclaim this Salvation, oh friend,

Let's proclaim it without any fears;

Christ will be returning, He has promised us,

//Let us proclaim// God's salvation.

2

We'll complete our grand mission on earth,

Always teaching the truth of the Lord;

From the Counselor, to the true believer,

Who hopes for liberty.

3

We should not fear anything on earth,

Finishing our duty below;

And through all the checkpoints, together we'll work hard,

And all will come out right.
