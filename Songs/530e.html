530 SOFT MURMURS

Soft murmurs under all the bright stars,

They say so clearly: Christ's on His way!

The noise is heard amongst all the heavens,

I see the lights shine, of pretty light.

CHORUS

For this I'm singing //yes I am singing// from all my heart,

I follow Jesus morn, noon, and nighttime;

Because He's saved me and died on the cross.

2

Oh what joy it will be up in heaven,

Where all the sanctified reign with Christ;

There won't be sadness, crying, or suffering,

Only felicity in that place.

3

Here all the saints are singing so happy,

It doesn't matter what pains they have;

Even if in the world they may suffer,

But in the heavens they will all live.

4

All of the Missioners and the Pastors,

Speak to the peoples the Word of God;

All of their cries the Lord takes upon Him,

And soon He's coming to take His church.
