481 I WILL PLANT

I will plant the most precious of seedlings,

Of the glorious evangel of love;

I will plant with a joyful soul,

God does love the one giving to Him.

CHORUS

I will give to the Lord,

Offerings overflowing with love;

I will give by my faith,

Because God always loves those who give.

2

I will plant with my love and so generous,

So the seed of the Lord surely grows;

I will plant in a way that's so constant,

With my offering I give with my love.

3

I will plant through the rough and steep mountains,

This small grain of sand that I give now;

And in mansions and in humble cabins,

To my God I will give what I am.
