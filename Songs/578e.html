578 I LIVED WITHOUT PEACE

I lived without peace on earth,

Without hope and without God;

Until one blessed evening, I came close to my Christ.

CHORUS

He has erased all my wickedness,

He will not take account of my sin;

As holy as He is, He has redeemed me,

2

With Christ I found holy peace.

He has changed all of my life,

He is the rock eternal;

My tender Savior Christ.

3

He calls the sinners to Him,

He wants no evil for them;

For them He suffered death,

To forgive them their sins.
