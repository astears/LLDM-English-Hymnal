099 THE BEST DOCTOR OF MY SOUL

The best doctor of my soul is Christ,

He knows all your aching and your pains;

Struggles that you've told Him when you pray,

Christ your Savior knows everything well.

CHORUS

Sins and our guilt He took away,

Through His shed blood on the wooden cross;

You souls who to sin have been tied down,

Come to Jesus, liberty you'll find.

2

Any wave rising too high He'll calm,

All the hungry Christ will satisfy;

All the sinners who come to Jesus,

Will find salvation for their lost souls.

3

Sinner, if salvation you do seek,

Christ the doctor will heal your sick soul;

With His right hand He'll will guide your steps,

And to heaven He will take your soul.
