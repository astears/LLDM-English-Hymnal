331 LET US FIGHT LIKE THE FAITHFUL

Let us fight like the faithful servants of God,

Only defending the will of our Lord;

Now that Lord Jesus is walking at our side,

Let us fight for Him people of Israel;

He is the one who gives us victory.

CHORUS

He who dies for God will surely find,

With Christ in heaven he will gain the prize;

He who dies fighting for our Lord,

He'll be resurrected by our Jesus Christ.

2

Whoever faithfully fights as soldiers of war,

For the defense of the battle of the faith;

Justice and power are garments that he should wear,

And triumph He'll have through Christ his savior and Lord;

Through Jesus' power he'll win the war.

3

Don't you fear, 'cause with us is Jesus Christ the Lord,

All of His strength is always by our side;

He is the captain now and He is guiding us,

Let's fight for Him, oh people of the Lord;

Only victorious we will be through Him.
