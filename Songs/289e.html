289 JESUS FOUNDED A CHURCH

Jesus founded a Church and it will be known to all,

By fruits and other signals, the masses will then see;

Its name is in the Bible, and two cannot be found,

Apostle Paul does teach us, that it's the Church of God.

CHORUS

The church of the Lord may it always reign,

Because it's arriving, all over the world;

And then it will rise, holding its banner,

Because the one who's guiding, is an Apostle of the Lord.

2

The Church is marching onwards, to the celestial place,

Guided by an Apostle, who's Servant of our God;

Give glories without ceasing, to our Savior and Lord,

His love is so immense that He's cleansed us from our sins.

3

He comes to take the one, and it's the Church of God,

To His holy bosom, to the great city;

And His beloved wife, that He will surely crown,

For all that she has suffered, for his holy name.
