504 IF YOU WANT TO BE RESURRECTED

If you want to be resurrected,

Look for all the things of heaven;

Where Lord Jesus Christ is seated,

At the right hand of our celestial God.

CHORUS

Look for the things of heaven,

And not perishable things;

Because you are dead,

And you are living a life with Christ.

2

If we want to be resurrected,

Let's leave all our sins forever;

All our hatred, envy, and malice,

All our worldliness and passions too.

3

If we want to be resurrected,

May in sanctity we be walking;

Learning from Jesus the Bishop,

Meekness, humility, sanctity.

4

If you want to be resurrected,

Look for all the things of heaven;

Only looking at the White One,

This is the prize for the people of the Lord.
