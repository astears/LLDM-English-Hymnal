514 IF YOUR CUP OVERFLOWETH

If your cup overfloweth, your doubts will then all flee,

'Cause the Lord takes good care of you;

God will order your pathways and angels will come too,

In journeys they will guard you.

CHORUS

//If your cup overfloweth with love, Hallelujah//

With a joyful heart you'll sing, you will always be in prayer;

If your cup overfloweth with love.

2

If your cup overfloweth, not one fear you will have,

That may be burdening your heart; His grace is abundant;

His love is so sublime, His peace like no other exists.

3

If your cup overfloweth, you will never deny,

Christ Jesus, your Savior Lord;

His tender affection, forever you will speak,

Always living for my Lord.

4

If your cup overfloweth, you also will have rest,

His blessings will come find you;

The wine in your cup will never be running out,

Accept when He invites you.
