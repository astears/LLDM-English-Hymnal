548 A STONG TOWER

A strong tower is the name of our Lord,

Where salvation my soul finds only through Him;

He will always be my refuge there's no doubt,

Under His wings He will shield me with His love.

CHORUS

There will be no evil that will harm me here,

I embrace my hope in Jesus Christ the Lord;

My hope is sustained by He who died for me,

I will reach His palace and mansion of light.

2

Those who place their willingness in Him today,

In the asylum of His love they will be raised;

From all anguish and all pain they will be saved,

So that they may only rejoice in His love.

3

For all those who will receive Him on this day,

They will have power and virtue as a gift;

Christ has said: I give you all my might today,

And all those who do believe then will rejoice.

4

As an ointment is the name of our Lord,

To the soul who can accept His divine love;

It will be shed like a scent of tenderness,

Over all souls who will witness His great love.
