041 OH BLESSED APOSTLE

Oh blessed Apostle, a leader by God,

A tireless warrior who doesn't fear the war;

In Jesus your Savior you have won the war,

Confiding you follow your King and your Lord.

CHORUS

Oh you blessed Servant, who announces God,

Messenger sent from God; of a vibrant voice;

You are the Announcer, of the light and truth,

You're the one who's chosen to give liberty.

2

To triumph and victory the Lord Christ calls you,

As a valiant servant your light shineth bright;

With joy in the world you are sowing with love,

Continually fighting with valor till end.

3

Constantly sowing, continue to plant,

If the King of hell is shrewd and astute;

Don't fear, God's protection will guard you in love,

And you have Christ Jesus profound in your heart.

4

No human dominion can conquer your being,

The weapons of mankind will easily break;

But Christ Jesus gives you His armor of steel,

And you as your father will conquer and win.
