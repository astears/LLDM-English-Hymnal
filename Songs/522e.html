522 I HAVE BEEN BAPTIZED

I have been baptized like my Savior has ordained,

What a great joy I do feel deep within my heart;

All of my sins have been erased by Jesus Christ,

I will arrive to His mansion pure and clean.

CHORUS

I will follow Jesus Christ,

I've given up what the world has offered me;

And with the aid of His great light,

I will continue in His footsteps all my life.

2

Goodbye to this world where I once lived in sin,

Where all the sins had been destroying my life;

I feel a joy after I had been baptized,

And to go back to sin again, I'd rather die.

3

I follow only the way that is full of faith,

That very few people have chosen to pursue;

I ask my God to keep me safe in all His love,

I will arrive to His mansion pure and clean.
