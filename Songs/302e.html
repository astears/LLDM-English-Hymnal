302 THE WIFE NOW PREPARES

The wife now prepares all her garments,

She's also dressed in happiness;

'Cause she is awaiting for her Savior Lord,

Because He draws nearer with growing radiance.

CHORUS

Be vigil, beloved wife,

As you wait for your dear Lord;

With your garments well prepared,

You'll fulfill your mission here.

2

In holiness always await, In pleading and in prayers too;

At the end of all things, you will have your crown,

In Zion the city with the Lord Jesus Christ.

3

We always should be on alert,

'Cause we are the Church of our God;

And we always should be patiently awaiting;

Always being ready for our redeeming Lord.

4

The servant who receives a talent,

And faithfully does all God's will;

He will fulfill in him His almighty promise,

Be faithful in little, and much you'll oversee.
