528 I AM A VERY BLESSED LAMB

I am a very blessed lamb rescued by Jesus was I,

Thus when I hear His voice calling, and then He offered a cross.

CHORUS

The cross that my Jesus offers, it is a cross of great suffering,

//If you want to take that cross//

//You will become a new being//

And also a beloved child.

2

Look at my feet how they're bleeding, because of this rocky mount,

This is where I lived my whole life, without a hope and my God.

3

All my life was quickly passing, all full of torment and pain,

Now I am really rejoicing, in the sweet love of my Jesus.

4

This Sacrosanct, He's a Pastor, looking for those who are lost,

Sometimes He finds them almost dead,

He extends His hand to them.

5

Oh happy and how joyous, when a new life comes to be,

And if we're faithful we will go, all to that heavenly shore.
