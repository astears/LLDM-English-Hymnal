488 OH LORD, YOU SEE WITHIN MY HEART

Oh Lord, you see within my heart, the ways in which I think,

And when I meditate you weigh, my thoughts on balance beam.

CHORUS

Explore oh Lord my heart today; sift through all of my thoughts,

And see if there are any ungodly things;

And guide me in the life that is eternal.

2

My thoughts and tongue you do know well, you know of all my Lord,

And wherever my soul will go, your favor I receive.

3

If I am on the wings of dawn, the world that's far beyond,

I know that you are there with me, your hand is by my side.

4

I can't hide myself from you God; you're always on my pursuit,

To you light and darkness alike, they differ not at all.

5

Oh Lord always be my fair Judge, with you I want to go,

I long to be always with you, to never depart you.
