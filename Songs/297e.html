297 JUDGE ME OH GOD

Vindicate me God, and plead my cause,

Rescue me from ungodly nations;

From deceitful men and the wicked,

For you are my God and my stronghold;

Why have you rejected me?

Why must I go about mourning Oppressed by the cruel enemy?

CHORUS

Send forth your light and your truth, let them guide me,

They will lead me to your mountain of holiness;

To the place where you dwell,

Then I will go to the altar;

To God, my joy and my delight,

I will praise you Lord with the harp, oh God, my God.

2

Why are you downcast, oh my soul?

Why so disturbed in me?

Put your hope in God, for I will yet praise my Lord God,

He is my Lord Savior before my soul and my Lord God.
