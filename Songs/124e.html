124 WHEN MY DEAR LORD JESUS COMES

When my dear Lord Jesus comes, I will be waiting,

My lamp will be shining on with light and peace;

He is ready at the doors, of this I am sure,

I know I should be preparing, because He's coming;

And I know He won't be late.

CHORUS

My Lord is coming very soon and filled with glory,

To earth He's coming, and I know He won't be late;

For us He's coming, His sanctified church, the faithful,

The Living God's church, Christ will raise it up that day.

2

When my dear Lord Jesus comes, over the clouds,

With His angels He will come, I will await;

And He always says to me, be very watchful,

And also to keep on praying, so He can take me;

To His palace far away.

3

When my dear Lord Jesus comes, what greatest joy,

Because He will come to take, His holy church;

I don't want to stay behind, alone in this world,

I would like to follow Jesus, up to His kingdom;

And with Him I will rejoice.

4

When my dear Lord Jesus comes, I will be waiting,

I would like to always be, doing His will;

And in this way He will take, me to His dwellings,

This for me will be a blessing to reign with Jesus;

Throughout all eternity.
