085 AS A PILOT

Just as a pilot on board ship,

Jesus goes deep within my heart;

He goes directing all my life,

Towards His magnificent home.

CHORUS

I go so glad, 'cause I'm with Him,

I'm walking over the waters;

With Him I go so glad;

Until I reach my native country, the place of Jerusalem.

2

Although the winds may be bothersome,

Though storms so strong beat upon me;

No breath from me will be taken,

From wickedness I'll depart.

3

With sovereign power of my God,

All the roughest storms will be calmed;

Now I invite you, who's passing,

So that your soul can be saved.

4

I won't be lost on my journey,

I go happily to my home;

Until I arrive at the doorway,

Of the great city of Zion.
