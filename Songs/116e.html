116 WHEN MY SOUL

When my soul is in trials and struggles,

And I feel that I can't see your light;

A cry surges from deep within my soul,

Place your hand, Lord, place it over me.

CHORUS

Place your hand, Lord, place it over me,

Place that same feeling within my soul;

Give me grace so that I may obey,

Give me strength so I may overcome.

2

My soul battles and so does my flesh,

All to feel your sweet calling in me;

It's not possible to keep on hiding,

It is not possible to resist.

3

When my soul is in trials and struggles,

And my faith's falling down in dismay;

With my weeping I claim to you, Father,

Consolation you send back to me.

4

Many souls perish without God's blessing,

Meanwhile I'm living in wellness here;

What will I say when Christ asks towards our souls,

When He asks: What have you done for me?
