101 CHRIST IS A STAR

Christ is a star shining brightly, He is our Pastor Lord,

He will take care of our beings, through the doorway of love;

For this we give Him all glory, to our Redeemer divine,

For this we're singing so joyful, We saints to whom He has freed.
