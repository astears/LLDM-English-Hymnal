089 WITH GREATEST PATIENCE

With greatest patience the bride is awaking,

Always a waiting her husband and Lord;

He will come soon let us not fall asleep now,

Let all the lamps of our love start to shine.

CHORUS

Come Jesus Christ, oh yes come once again,

//Come Jesus come!// Come Jesus Christ, oh yes come once again,

Come back soon Lord Jesus Christ.

2

If our time waiting becomes overbearing,

In just a moment we'll all pass away;

The precious star of the dawning will soon shine,

It will shine deep within all of our souls.

3

Oh Lord Savior Jesus Christ, our beloved,

Permit your bride to attentively hear;

And be parted from all her transgressions,

So that day she'll be encountered with joy.

4

Time is advancing oh Christians keep watching,

For soon the trumpet will begin to sound;

Time is advancing let us keep on marching,

See that our husband is ready for us.
