043 IN THE EARLY HOURS

In the early hours of sweet happy mornings,

The fragrance of flowers perfume your great throne;

The pleas and the prayers of the people, who love you,

And sweet hymns of glory proclaiming your love.

CHORUS

Christians also worship and praise God, To you my greatest King;

To you we give all the glory for your great love,

To you mightiest King of all,

To you my loving God.

2

In the early hours of sweet happy mornings,

The happiest souls give you praises oh Lord;

Of rich zestful fragrance and sweet smelling perfume,

Are all of the prayers and the sweetest hymns.

3

I laid down to rest, went to sleep, and awakened,

Because God's dear hands sustained me all night;

That's why this great day I do sing and give praises,

To the blessed name of my Savior and Lord.

4

Blessed be the name of my dear Jesus Christ,

Who gives us His hand and protects us from sin;

He who with His power sustains our souls,

This is why we give praise to my greatest Lord.
