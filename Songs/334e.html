334 WITH TEARS THEY CRY

With tears they cry, those who carry seeds so righteous,

With tears they go, through the wicked evil world;

But they will come, bringing palm leaves in their hands,

With hearts of joy a new song they'll be singing Lord.

CHORUS

How beautiful are the feet walking through mountains,

Of those who carry the news of heaven, to save the souls;

They're holy, they're beautiful, and very delicate,

In that day coming close to God, they will be.

2

How many valiant in the battle have been beaten,

When they were faced against Satan and the world;

But all their blood has lifted up high the glorious banner,

And the Evangel is being spread, without delay.

3

Although the fight may seem arduous and gloomy,

Their knees are weakened; they've fallen in dismay;

However soon we will all receive strength,

His Holy Spirit, our God will send us, without delay,
