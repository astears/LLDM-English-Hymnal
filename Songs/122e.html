122 WHEN ALONE AND IN SADNESS

When alone and in sadness I was,

And I lived without hope or any peace;

I did call on the Savior of souls,

And the healer of my sickness too.

CHORUS

Alone I did talk to my Jesus,

Telling him all of my necessities;

And He took in his arms all my burdens,

And salvation He gave to my soul.

2

Through this world I did go without Jesus,

And despising the love of my Lord;

Understanding my terrible fault,

I did seek the forgiveness of God.

3

Now I'm happy and very delighted,

Because Jesus my soul He has saved;

And if sorrows or doubts wear me out,

I acclaim to my Savoir and Lord.

4

If you want attain this good fortune,

And would like to be saved on this day;

You can follow my most humble counsel,

And do give all your heart to my Lord

2nd. CHORUS

Alone you can talk to my God,

And He'll take in His arms all your burdens,
