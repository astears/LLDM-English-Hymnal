241 HAVE YOU HEARD

Have you heard of the story of Jesus?

The sweet story of grace and of love;

When in weakness and poverty fallen,

He redeemed me in His precious love.

CHORUS

His immense love I am sure of,

Nothing will ever have compare;

Brilliantly I'm going through life,

And heaven's here deep within my soul.

2

Have you heard that He said to the people:

If you're tired you may come to me now?

I came to Him and instantly felt there,

That the object of His love I was.

3

Have you heard of those who once in blindness,

Into their eyes He gave them His light;

And when I was also in the shadows,

Jesus came to guide me with His light.

4

Have you heard of the deep stormy oceans,

That the Lord pacified with His voice;

You should know that in the roughest torments,

In my life the Lord came to give peace.
