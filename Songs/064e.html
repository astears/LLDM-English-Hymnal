064 THE LORD WILL GIVE BLESSING

The Lord will give blessing, to those obeying Him,

To those who don't follow the sinful pathway;

To those never standing in ways of wickedness,

Nor those who've been sitting with mockers and their ways.

CHORUS

In the congregation blessing they will have,

All those who have good will, good pleasures they will find;

Gladly meditating in the law of God,

In daytime and nighttime they will be without cease.

2

They will be like trees standing firm and vigorous,

Alongside the rivers we see them fertilized;

Their seasonal fruits and their pleasant color green,

They will be prevailing in everything they do.

3

However the wicked will be just like fluff,

And scattered throughout all the face of this earth;

And so the ungodly will never be among,

The righteous and holy when judgment day will come.

4

The path of the righteous will be guarded by Christ,

Because with perfection He knows their path so well;

The evil, not so, will be sad when Christ returns,

And sadly destruction assuredly they'll have.
