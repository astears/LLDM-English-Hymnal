408 OH COURAGEOUS SERVANT

Oh courageous Servant, Apostle of God,

Preserve your position, always lifting the flag;

With God in assistance, in perpetual battle,

While taking the message of pardon to the souls.

CHORUS

Yes, preaching to the souls, the message of the Lord,

Yes, announcing the message of truth;

Courageously fighting, and saving the people,

While taking the message of pardon to the souls.

2

While suffering in life, you always find song,

In battles so fiery, you win through all your prayers;

Because God has sent you, He is your protector,

While taking the message of pardon to the souls.

3

You've come from a distance, to wish us good health,

And asked God to bless us and prosper us in love,

Our cups overflowing, we are more willing,

To take the Lord's message of pardon to the souls.
