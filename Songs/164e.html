164 THE PILGRIM LIVING

The pilgrim living in this world,

Can't see all their wrongdoing;

Living in deep sleep and dreaming,

And they can never live free.

CHORUS

Don't ever become discouraged,

Approach the great General;

Walk always with the courageous,

Only they can guide you on.

2

When my soul wandered in this world,

Without God's help and without hope;

I heard a voice that was calling,

Come and you will be rewarded.

3

But the flesh of all mankind,

Is enemy of the soul;

Always rejects the Lord's favor,

That Jesus Christ offers to all.

4

But our God who is ready,

He's good and compassionate;

He can do good in your heart,

And simplicity He gives.

5

In these latter times we're living,

God has come down to this earth;

His Holy Spirit conveying,

To those who believe in Him.
