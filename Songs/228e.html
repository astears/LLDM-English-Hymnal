228 LET US REJOICE

Let us rejoice and let us be glad and give Him glory,

For the wedding of the Lamb Jesus has come;

And His bride has made herself ready.

CHORUS

And all the nations that by our Lord Savior,

In his light will walk through, forevermore;

And the kings of the earth will bring forth their honor and splendor.

2

The Lord Jesus Christ all the power be throughout all the ages,

He's the King of kings, and the Lord of lords, Almighty;

Jesus Christ is reigning in heaven.

3

The Lord Jesus Christ will come to raise up all his faithful servants,

Come and congregate for the supper of the Lamb;

For the Lord Jesus Christ resurrected.

4

That City of God is embellished with gold, pearls, and diamonds,

And the bride is dressed in bright white linen garments;

She's the church who's been purchased with His blood.
