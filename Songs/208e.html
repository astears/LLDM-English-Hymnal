208 PRAYER IS THE WAY

Prayer is the way which we can speak to God,

To His church who walks with the fear of God;

If you see His word in it you'll surely find,

Prayer is the way that brings you closer to Lord Christ.

CHORUS

Oh to speak with Jesus, what great happiness!

You can really //tell him// everything;

As you tell Him your necessities,

He will hear you from His heavenly throne up above.

2

And if you are saddened, put yourself in prayer,

Speak unto the heavens speak with all your heart;

It is a commandment that the Lord left us,

You will have an answer it's His promise that he made.

3

If you are still waiting for the Counselor,

You should wait in prayer, yes, with faith and zeal;

And when the Lord Jesus sees your fervent plea,

He will grant you consolation and fill you with joy.

4

If you see no answer, just pray more and more,

Do not be discouraged, Christ is not folklore;

He is always faithful with all His promises,

Whatever you need the Lord will gladly give to you.
