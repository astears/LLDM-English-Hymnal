285 JESUS COME HEARKEN

Jesus come hearken to my plea,

My life depends only on you;

Listen to me and quickly come,

To give me aid with all your might.

CHORUS

My meditation you will keep,

Consecrate me as my voice speaks;

And come to liberate my heart,

From going after evil Lord.

2

And before morning gives its light,

I will raise my voice up to thee;

And as an incense I will take,

Up to your altar all my pleas.

3

As astral lights dim in the sky,

I will be searching for your peace;

You will accept, my love oh Lord,

As a nocturnal sacrifice.

4

Oh my good Lord, if it's too hard,

When I receive your reprimand;

With humble heart your holiness,

You will infuse in all my heart.

5

And the dark night has now arrived,

So Lord I come to you with faith;

I know I will find peace in you,

For your salvation I now yearn.
