340 FARTHER THAN THE SUN

Farther than the sun where the King resides,

there's a golden palace, and of crystal stone;

All its streets are golden, and its gates are pearls,

Many stones so precious, made by Jesus Christ.

CHORUS

///Glory/// to the Lamb who's holy,

Sacrificed by God, forever Amen.

2

The twenty-four elders who replied amen,

Cherub by the thousands giving praise to him;

To the Holy Lamb who was sacrificed,

To save all the sinners who would come to him.

3

In the golden mansions where the King resides,

There are many cherubs, and thousands of angels;

Giving Hallelujahs, and glories forever,

To the holy Lamb, sacrificed for us.
