398 ALL YOUTH COME

All youth come to the Lord, under his loving veil,

Christ has been unfolding you before nations;

All lined in formation, He would like to see you,

And when He goes out to battle with Him you will go.

CHORUS

Let us go to Christ we're all ready without fear,

To battle we'll go, filled with courage and valor;

Youth let us be fighting together against sin,

Jesus Christ our General is leading on.

2

Oh youth come to the Lord, our Savior leads us on,

All around Him He would like to see you there;

Go out with Him in battle do not hesitate,

Let us hurry to the battle, my companions come.

3

Invincible arms of the Chief who guides us on,

With His holy gospel and wonderful love;

Let us be dressed in them, and His power will fill us,

My companions let us follow Him we will prevail.

4

The enemy engendered by Satan is so fierce,

But we will be sustained by our Captain;

Oh youth let us be standing so firm and without fear,

At the right hand of the leader is our Savior Lord.

5

Who ever comes to battle will listen to His voice,

Christ we be the one to grant them victory;

Let us go fight companions, let us fight hard for Him,

And with Jesus we will conquer for immortal crowns.
