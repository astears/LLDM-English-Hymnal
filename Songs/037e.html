037 OH LOVE, OH WHAT SUBLIME LOVE

Oh Love, oh what sublime love,

A gift from God above, without compare;

Just like the firm rock of Hermon,

So God did bless this world for ages more.

CHORUS

Divine sweet love, oh what great love,

Full of His grace, and of shining light;

The glory of His splendid love,

Was when they nailed Him up on the cross.

2

His footprints show kindness for all,

Eternal mercy as no one can give;

It overflows the heart and soul,

Rainfalls of blessings flooding as a spring.

3

Most glorious work of kindness,

That brought salvation to humanity;

Health full of benevolence,

Was that which only God could offer us.

4

For God so loved humanity,

This world clothed with sin and wickedness;

God's love in Calvary was shown,

By giving His son's life for all mankind.
