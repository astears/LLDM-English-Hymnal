188 UPON THE MIGHTY BILLOWS

Upon the boundless oceans where mighty billows roll,

I've fixed my hope in Jesus; blest anchor of my soul;

On my knees I called Jesus and the storm disappeared,

I rest upon His mercy and trust Him more.

CHORUS

Its Jesus the fortress, the anchor of my faith,

Though evil and dangers, and many cries of fear;

They'll end in life eternal, when I am with my King,

My Jesus always is my haven.

2

He keeps my soul from danger, from evil and from pain,

His voice had stilled the waters and bid their tumult cease;

My pilot and deliverer, to Him I all confide,

For always when I need Him, He's by my side.

3

He is my friend and Savior, in Him my anchors cast,

He drives away my sorrows, and shields me from the blast;

By faith I'm looking upward beyond lies troubled sea,

There I behold a haven prepared for me.
