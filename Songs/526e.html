526 I AM A PILGRIM

I am a pilgrim, I am a stranger,

//For a short time I'll live here//;

//For a short time// I'll live here,

Do not withhold 'cause I want to serve you.

Conquering always the tempest storm,

The peace I look for, the world does not have;

And I wish to arrive there, to my faithful countryside,

I desire, I desire, to arrive!

CHORUS

There's no more crying, there's no more pain,

There's no more sinning, no death exists there;

I am a pilgrim, I am a stranger,

//For a short time// I'll live here.

2

In that countryside, I'll happily look at,

//Sunlight will always shine there//;

//Sunlight always// will shine there,

In this sad world, so sad and lonely.

When many dangers are thrown at me,

I only ask you, my divine Pastor;

//May you guide me my dear Lord//

//May you guard me// only you.

2nd. CHORUS

So that I'm ready, for your grand coming,

Until I'm living with you in heaven;

My divine Pastor, I only ask you,

//That you guide me// only you.
