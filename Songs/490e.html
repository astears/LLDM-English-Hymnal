490 I WILL REJOICE IF CHARITY

I will rejoice if charity does reign in my soul,

Now that the Omnipotent God has saved my being;

I am now dead for this world full of many vile sins,

I want to arrive at that celestial dwelling afar.

CHORUS

//Naught am I, if I don't have charity, flowing with love,

Without love, I won't prevail in Jesus Christ my Savior Lord//

2

I will arrive, if I prevail in all your pathways,

And if I also take delight in words of wisdom;

I will not follow men of this world, because they're nothing,

I follow footsteps of Christ Jesus, Lamb so faithful.

3

I mark the footsteps with His blood flowing from His veins,

'Cause God the Father offered Him as a live offering;

So that the world was saved from all their woes and worries,

From all their chains, oppressively, from this false world.

4

I put my sight in Him who's white and who is perfect,

In which no change, nor worldliness, can be detected;

I want to follow while He's giving me a calmness,

Until I wait for Him to make His final comeback.
