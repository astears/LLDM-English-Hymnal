262 TO THE HOME OF ALL MY MEMORIES

To the home of all my memories,

I dream to find once more,

There's no place under the heavens, sweeter than my home above;

Laying in all of the castles, running towards this world eternal,

Not one do I prefer more, my home, oh my sweet home.

CHORUS

//My home// my sweet home,

There is no place under heaven, sweeter than my home above.

2

There the light of the high heavens, so tranquilly descending,

Many joys, delights, and pleasures, the blessing of this place;

All the hours passing quickly, and so brief but so joyful,

All the things will be remembered forever in my home.

3

More than earthy pleasures I dream, to be living in that dwelling,

To return to this calm homeland, to my cabin to return;

Thousand cherubs singing glories will make me so happy,

Thousand enchantments in the heavens, the light of peace will reign.
