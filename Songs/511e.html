511 ZION, CELESTIAL CITY

Zion celestial city, there I'm waiting to live,

Zion you are my desire;

Where ever I will see you my God and our Lord.

CHORUS

I am weary in the desert, discouraged I may become,

But then the Lamb of my God comes to my rescue to save;

Quickly He comes to my side, and He calms me from within,

And to that heavenly city I continue to walk on.

2

Zion city of truth I want to see you myself,

Zion city of my Jesus who was crucified;

Just so He could save my soul.

3

Zion further than the sun, I will look upon you,

Zion promised from the Father;

That Jesus Christ our Savior He went up to prepare.
