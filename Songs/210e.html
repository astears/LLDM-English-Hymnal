210 ALL THE PEOPLE

All the people once living in darkness,

By God's grace they have seen a great light;

In the land of the shadow of death,

My Lord's light has dawned inside their souls.

CHORUS

I was once blind, but now I see,

By the grace of my Lord Jesus;

I know battles will come to me,

But I'll conquer through my Lord.

Conquer all and win the war,

And as David, the good Shepherd;

Fought the giant named Goliath,

We must fight and conquer satan, having triumph over sin.

2

Christ will never forsaken His people,

He has constantly freed us from evil;

Having parted the sea for a pathway,

And through dry land His people passed through.

3

But the people defied God's commandment,

By creating a calf made of gold;

They then worshiped this man-crafted idol,

Thereby facing their backs to the Lord.

4

Let us trust in our Father in heaven,

He is with us in trials and temptations;

We will triumph, arriving with Jesus,

To Canaan, the land of our God.
