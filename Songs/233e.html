233 THERE IS JOY IN MY HEART

There is joy in my heart today,

For Jesus has saved me by faith;

He has broken my chains for me,

And my soul He has saved.

CHORUS

//Glory// to Jesus Christ,

Truly you are my everything;

By your grace you have saved my soul,

In the cross where you died for me.

2

Gratitude there is in my soul,

And with joy I will sing to you;

Melodies to my Savior Lord,

Glories given to Him.

3

From the tempter He will save me,

And will help me to overcome;

His protection is laid o'er me,

With His greatest power.

4

Of His love I would like to preach,

To the ones who walk in darkness;

I do want them to understand,

That He came to save them.

5

Sinners do come to Jesus Christ,

And salvation, accept from Him;

Jesus will never reject you,

He will save you today.

6

When in glory I find myself,

Where the love of my God will reign;

My crown will be given to me,

Oh what great happiness!
