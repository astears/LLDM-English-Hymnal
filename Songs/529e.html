529 I AM A SOLDIER

I am a soldier of the cross,

And servant of my Lord;

I shall not fear to take His cross,

Or suffer for His name.

CHORUS

And when the battle is over,

We shall wear a crown,

//God will give me a crown//

And when the battle is over,

We shall wear a crown in the New Jerusalem.

//In that land// in the New Jerusalem,

And when the battle is over;

We shall wear a crown in the New Jerusalem.

2

Others have fought for my Lord's faith,

No coward shall I be; For my good Lord,

I'll stand and fight, His faith shall be my guide.

3

Fight the fight of faith and of love,

Increase my courage Lord;

I'll always follow on your steps,

In peace you'll guide me through.
