143 GOD OF THE HEAVENS

God of the heavens come and bless your people,

All are together in one holy love;

All are content as they give praise to your name,

God of the heavens our beautiful Lord.

CHORUS

On this earth we're merely pilgrims, and we are absent from you,

But we expect you to grace us;

To be in your kingdom, very close to you.

2

This flock of people who you have elected,

They were once lost in this world without light;

Now they have life in the name which is holy,

King of the heaven our glorious Christ.

3

The vestments that you have gone to prepare us,

They're brightly shinning with splendorous light;

You will convey them above in your kingdom,

Also diadems oh most glorious Lord.

4

This congregation of united people,

The Holy Spirit of God made it so;

Before His presence they'll be reunited,

As they give glories to your name oh Lord.
