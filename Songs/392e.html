392 OH GOD COME VISIT US

Oh God come visit us, don't be late,

Send your great power Lord down to us;

Your altar we are now approaching,

Manifest Lord your power to us.

This is your promise Savior Lord,

Great consolation give to us;

It will give us much greater strength,

Come and show us oh Lord your great power.

2

Oh God of Elijah we're asking,

Come reveal your great power to us;

Let the world know who we are serving,

The living God of grace and of might.

Take all our wickedness away,

Convey your holiness to us;

And guard us in your liberty,

Yes come show us oh God your great power.

3

Revive us Lord, oh come and hear us,

Manifest Lord your power to us;

Lift up our courage and our faith Lord,

Execute your high power, oh Lord.

Inflame our hearts so we may feel you,

And grant us more compassion too;

Let us enjoy your sweet communion,

Yes come show us oh God your great power.
