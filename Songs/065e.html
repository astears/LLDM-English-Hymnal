065 BLESSED ARE ALL THEY

Blessed are all they who fear the Lord Jehovah, our grand King,

Who walk in all the Lord's ways;

You will eat the fruit of the labor from your hands,

Blessing and prosperity will be yours.

•••••

Your wife will be as a vine, Blessed, fruitful within all of your household;

Your sons will be like blessed olive shoots,

All around your table.

•••••

Thus is the man blessed by the Lord Jehovah,

Who fears the Lord God;

//May the Lord bless you from Mount Zion,//

And see prosperity of Jerusalem.
