596 MY WHOLE LIFE I CONSECRATE

My whole life I consecrate,

Oh my blessed Savior Lord;

And your hand full of all blessings,

Will carry me in your light.

CHORUS

Always working, always working,

I will live for you my Lord;

News of peace has been announced to,

The lost sinners of this world.

2

Fears and doubts I have in thousands,

Will always besiege me so;

But you Jesus will help always,

And they won't make me afraid.

3

I am ready with all pleasure,

With love to fight for my faith;

Jesus Christ gives me the courage,

And without doubts I will win.

4

I will gain the joyful victory,

With the soldiers of the Lord;

They are full of all your glory,

All around this whole world.
