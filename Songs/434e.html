434 I HAVE PROMISED

I have promised to accept your good word,

To defy every vile temptation;

To give myself entirely to your work,

For in your word I found salvation.

CHORUS

Our Father, thou who art in heaven,

On this earth and everywhere else too;

Give us our daily bread on this day,

And come soon to be reigning in us.

2

I am willing to serve in your vineyard,

To be useful in your hands, my Lord;

Make me faithful and valiant in battle,

Do not let me fall in error.

3

A vile sinner I was and I know this,

All my life was always far from thee;

But you forgave my horrendous sins,

And will always if I come to you.

4

Any sinner who's seeking salvation,

Who wants to feel a change in their life;

Come surrender your soul and existence,

To your maker and King Jesus Christ.
