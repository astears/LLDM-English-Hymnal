047 TO YOU MY ONLY KING

We give you praises my King,

Just to you belongs the praise;

Because you have all the power,

We praise your glorious name.

CHORUS

To you King of all glory,

To you we'll give our praises;

Glories to your wondrous name,

In the earth and in the heavens.

2

Lord and Father we give glory,

All to you our sovereign King;

You will always hear our voices,

Because you are our haven.

3

Jesus Christ we give you praises,

Give you honor and all glory;

And proclaim your blessed name,

Give You praises and all glory.
