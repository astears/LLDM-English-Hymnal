126 WHEN I RETURNED

When I returned to my home,

Christ did not reject my soul,

//All of my vices and my transgressions;

Christ has taken them from me//

CHORUS

//Marvelous yes it is!, oh beautiful He is,

Christ gave salvation to me//

2

This I would never have thought, that He wanted to save me,

//He came to wash me, cleanse me from my sins;

Health he has given my soul//

3

His works are so marvelous, and He has loved me His way,

//He has erased all my sins and evil;

Daily he sanctifies me//

4

He gives me wellness and health;

He's taking well care of me,

//Eternal life I have up in heaven;

Glory, Hallelujah to Him//
