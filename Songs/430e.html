430 BECAUSE OF THE AMAZING

Because of the amazing daylight,

That comes to give my heart such joy;

As we awaken every morning,

When we admire all the warmth;

Of a bright dawning with its beauty,

Let us give thanks unto our God!

2

For the aroma the flowers,

Where in their colored finery;

We see the mighty hand of our God,

That with His stencil gave them hue;

For many, many wondrous marvels,

Let us give thanks unto our God!

3

Because our daily bread He gives us,

This He has granted us with love;

So tireless so good and holy,

To all creation He conveys;

Because this daily bread gives us life,

Let us give thanks unto our God!

4

And for the roof giving protection,

And for His holy blessing too;

And for the life of our parents,

Who gave us to the Lord with love;

For all those precious things He gives us,

Let us give thanks unto our God!

5

From the Lord's amazing knowledge,

Intelligence He gave to us;

So that we may think of His glory,

As beautiful wings spread with love;

Because of that wonderful privilege,

Let us give thanks unto our God!

6

Because of that distinguished honor,

Which in His kindness He dispensed;

Sending His sacrosanct Son to us,

To consummate our redemption;

For all the humans who are sinners,

Let us give thanks unto our God!
