519 WE ARE SAVED

We are saved through Jesus Christ, we are saved through faith in God,

Your love gives us, with one voice we sing a hymn;

And with all our hearts and souls, for me He died.

CHORUS

//Oh Lord// my good Shepherd, here you have a flock of love,

Congregated in your love, your faithful flock;

Send us your love, send us your light, may your Spirit so divinely,

The Counselor so great, come now, my Lord.

2

We adore Lord Jesus Christ, with gratitude profound,

My Redeemer in this day do bless our beings;

And protect our souls from harm, oh my good Lord.

3

Give us with your immense love, your spiritual comfort, Lord,

May you send us, we have opened up our ears;

All your flock to listen well, your voice, oh Lord.
