258 JERUSELEM, BEAUTIFUL AND GRAND

Jerusalem, beautiful and grand,

Is the great city of our King; In it I'll always reign,

In all my ways I will proclaim, praise to my Lord;

All my heart will be in Him, rejoicing.

CHORUS

All of your palaces I'll see,

And all your glories I will sing;

//Oh great Jerusalem//

2

Beautiful province is your place,

It is the joy of all the earth;

In Zion, mount of God,

With all my heart I trust in Him, with all His pow'r;

I will await with faith, justice from God.

3

A new heaven I'll surely see,

There a witness I will be;

That Jesus Christ is light,

Though sun and moon do shine so bright,

God is our light;

Because the Lord up there, will make it shine.

4

Beautiful, great celestial place,

Of gold, and finest crystals too;

A dwelling forever,

Belonging to the righteous ones, a joyful place;

And God will be a temple in that place.
