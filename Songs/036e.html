036 I LOVE THE NAME OF GOD

I love the name of God, I love Him greatly,

I love the Lord who came, to save my soul;

I love His powerful and holy hand,

Drying up my weeping, it comes to rescue me.

CHORUS

The arms in which I lay, all of my worries,

They serve my troubles as great consolation;

His arms will always be all of my glory,

That's why I sing like this: that Jesus Christ loves me!

2

As I roamed through this earth, in hopeless mess,

With all the crimes of sin, I stained my soul;

Yet his compassion, and his noble heart,

All my dark transgressions, He rid from my soul.

3

I love the name of God, through all my worries,

By faith I will prevail, over distress;

So for this reason, I do give Him glory,

For my only weapon, is prayer to my Lord.

4

He loves me as I am, I understand this,

For life He gave to me through His own death;

I will entrust Him with my grief and burdens,

And His holy hand, indeed will bless my soul.
