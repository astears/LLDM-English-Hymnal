382 SEE THE NEW HORIZONS

See the new horizons shining,

All the storms have now disappeared;

Anguish has disappeared,

Peace appeared unto us.

CHORUS

What divine peace it was once far away,

And without you, I lived just wandering;

But the tender voice of our Lord,

Gave consolation to my soul;

And a happy life I live with you,

My good Jesus.

2

Now I can enjoy a new life,

This through Jesus my Savior Lord;

He has lent me His hand,

And His love has saved me.

3

We will go rejoice with Jesus,

Up in the new Jerusalem;

I invite you my brother,

Let's be faithful to Him.

4

Palms and crowns he's holding with Him,

He will give you richest fruits;

Decorating your temple,

With Him forever more.
