045 IN SILENCE AND ALONE

In silence, and alone meditating,

As I see your divine splendor Lord;

//And I feel in the deepest parts of my soul,

And amidst all of the calmness you will speak to me Lord//

2

I want to give you all of my life,

And make my flesh as an altar;

//May I feel that you're living in my soul,

And therefore your divine grace will be dwelling with me//

3

And sometimes when I'm going through trials,

I proclaim, give me Lord of your aid;

//The voice of the Lord keeps me breathing,

And as I hear it near me He comes to comfort me//
