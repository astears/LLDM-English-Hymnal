322 THE FIELDS FULL OF FLOWERS

The fields full of flowers are also adorned,

And you as those flowers adorn well your flock;

All your flock is happy to see you again,

Man who's been elected by Jesus my King.

CHORUS

Welcome now my Brother, oh Ambassador,

Announcing to the world, the message of love;

You're like Brother John, who also announced,

You have brought a doctrine, to save many souls.

2

You march in front of the great battalion,

You conquer the whole earth, for our Lord above;

You as John the Baptist, announcing God's news,

To the soul who is lost and in error lives.

3

More than sharpened arrow and as sweet honey,

These words for your people, which is God's flock;

All of your advice that you impart as well,

You instill great knowledge, comfort and courage.

4

In this church of God there's a Man sent from God,

You as an Apostle are Servant of the Lord;

God's always provided a man who He's chose,

Who may carry your flock, with Christ our Lord.
