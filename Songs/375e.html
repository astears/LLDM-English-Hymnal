375 IN THE VALLEY

In the valley of death there'll be no shadows,

When our battle in this life comes to an end;

We will be hearing the calling of the good Lord,

As He takes us with Him to eternal rest.

CHORUS

Shadows, we'll see no shadows,

As we leave this world so filled with pain;

Shadows, we'll see no shadows,

When I reach the heavens with success.

2

When we're left by those we love there'll be no shadows,

If we know that in our Jesus they had faith;

Because they will go to live for all the ages,

With the one who has redeemed us on the cross.

3

When He comes to take His own there'll be no shadows,

For His glory and His majesty will shine;

And the hosts that were redeemed with their good leader,

They will enter the high mansions filled with light.
