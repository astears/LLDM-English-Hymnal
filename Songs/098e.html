098 CHRISTIANITY IS MY JOYFUL SONG

Christianity is my joyful song,

The Redeemer has saved us;

All the grandiose hymns have been sung to God,

Honoring Christ, Jesus Lord.

Quickly make ready all your heart and soul,

Offer them to Christ the King;

Christ came from above to die happily,

So the law could be fulfilled.

CHORUS

//He's reigning with glory and pow'r//

With His pow'r, and His love, our Savior came to this earth;

//Jesus Christ was born// our Lord and our King,

Honor we give to the Lord, our Redeemer, King;

Honor we give, honor and love,

Glory forever, to our Savior Lord.

2

Jesus came to die on a wooden cross,

So salvation we could gain;

From a land above, where no sunrays shine,

Came Christ Jesus our Lord.

Giving bread and water to all who thirst,

Because this He promised us;

He that comes to Christ, will never face death,

Now He has fulfilled the law.
