298 THE BIBLE IS THE FLAG

The Bible is the flag of, that great eternal city,

Its stripes and stars do tell us, the story of its love;

A refuge for the poor ones, a place full of redemption,

Bought with the blood of Jesus, atonement with great price.

CHORUS

I am a citizen of that eternal city,

By faith I was received there, and citizen I am;

Christ is the one who governs in that heavenly land,

And when the flag is waving redemption's been announced.

2

Sixty six stars in number, that are marked on this flag Lord,

These represent the books, in her you'll surely find;

The stripes twelve count in number, these represent the twelve tribes,

The flagpole which it's held by, is the immortal Lamb.

3

Of many race and nations, of every tribe and language,

One country's been created, in Zion up above;

Up there when all together, under a flag that's flowing,

We'll sing up in God's glory, in peace, and redemption.
