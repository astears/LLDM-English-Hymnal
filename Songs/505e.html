505 IF I HAVE FOUND GRACE

CHORUS

If I have found grace, before the Lord my God,

//If I have found grace, was through the Servant of God//

1

My dearest Lord Jesus, shed His blood on the cross,

//My dearest Lord Jesus, shed blood for love of us all//

2

Our Lord Jesus descended bringing good new to us,

//And guiding our souls to heaven, to reign with Him above//

3

Oh Lord most omnipotent, oh heavenly Father,

That gave our souls salvation, through Jesus Christ our Lord;

Kneeled we pray towards heaven, and ask of His love.

4

Oh my soul always bless Him, your maker and your God,

// Oh my soul always bless Him, your Savior and your Lord//

5

Oh my soul always bless Him, praise your Redeemer and Lord,

//In earth and in the heavens, forever is your sweet love//
