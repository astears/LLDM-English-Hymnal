421 MY MASTER DEPARTED

My master departed, to a unique country far beyond earthly skies,

He left many mandates and to obey my dear Lord; My duty will be.

CHORUS

Loyal I want to be until I see His coming,

In His service I will be constant and true;

Loyal I want to follow where your hand will guide me,

Until I have the great joy of seeing your face.

2

The fields are now ready and have sprouted but the workers are very few,

Well then I will gladly accept the place I've been given; And my portion.

3

All others are working in the vineyard while I am being negligent,

As others use their gifts, and their talents wisely; Why should I hide my own.

4

The moment's arriving of His grand coming, but when it is I don't know,

I only yearn that, you will find me; active, fervent; And faithful too.
