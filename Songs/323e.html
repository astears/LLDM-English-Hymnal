323 ALL OF THE MESSENGERS

All of the messengers journeying through this world,

They are announcing the news of our Lord;

They're casting out all the ungodly spirits,

Only using the good word of our Lord.

CHORUS

The obedience of our God's manifested,

By the King, also the superior;

God will give testimony of their lives here,

They are servants of Jesus Christ in love.

2

When they go throughout the towns preaching His good word,

The good tidings the word the Lord has brought;

They will never be seen causing an outrage,

To the laws that were established by God.

3

The Lord Jesus Christ gave us an example,

Through His meekness, humility, and faith;

He adorned, He honored His holy temple,

When He shed His blood for all of the people.

4

He is our teacher and light to our messenger,

The good tidings the word the Lord has brought;

It instructs you to be humble like Jesus,

And to be serving Jesus in His love.

5

We will inherit the promise of our Father,

Until that day let us keep searching peace;

We'll confirm that Christ is our head and leader,

He who frees us from evil and from pain.
