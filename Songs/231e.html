231 THERE IS GREAT JOY

There is great joy and also contentment,

Engraved in my soul for my dear Lord;

//'Cause I remember that I have been saved,

Through the man chosen that God sent us//

CHORUS

In this day we commemorate blessings,

Of when our God had changed your name;

//Because you heard of this voice that told you:

Your new name will be Aaron//

2

A morning memorable and blessed,

Was when you heard the voice of the Lord;

//Today your children, we all remember,

So we are joyful in our Lord//

3

He suffered trials throughout His life time,

For having taught how to save our souls;

//But your God who has lift you up this day,

Will always give you comfort and rest//
