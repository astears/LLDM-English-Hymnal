165 THE PEOPLE OF THE LORD

The people of the Lord through battle will prepare,

And through their holiness, triumphant they will be;

They have a helper through, our Savior Jesus Christ,

Always He is victorious, against the devil too.

CHORUS

In the distance we see light, it's the promised land we see,

Where all those may rest in peace, who have purity inside;

Brother I'm inviting you, to the church of our Lord,

So you can participate, of this great blessing too.

And also in my heart, it praises you my God,

On this day of rejoicing in the assembly of God.

3

Things that the eye can't see, nor that the ear can hear,

These have been shown to us, from our Jesus Christ;

For His death on the cross, on which He suffered pain,

He showed us His bright light, His never ending love.
