524 I AM A PILGRIM HERE

I am a pilgrim here, just wandering in this cold world,

I have no place, where I can reign, so I should go fight;

So that I may rejoice with Jesus my King,

My soul will grieve, so in the end I'll win.

CHORUS

A crown of pearls await, up in the heavens,

//And if I'm true, I will win it oh Lord//

2

When sorrow sickens me, comfort I'll find in Jesus,
