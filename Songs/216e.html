216 THIS I HAVE AGAINST YOU

This I have against you, says the Lord Almighty God,

//You've abandoned me your first love, and rejected all my love//

CHORUS

Your Lord God is calling now, don't reject His kindness,

And if you have lost His love, you can find it once again.

2

Give back to your God of love, come return to righteousness,

//Do the work as when you entered, so over the devil win//

3

Humbleness, obedience, is what I demand from you,

//So you are able to enter, to the promised land above//

4

God gives two pathways to walk, look so wisely which you walk,

//And two payments are waiting, which one does your faith wait for//
