236 SPEAK TO ME OH LORD

Speak to me oh Lord, if I am sleeping,

That I may serve you always faithfully;

That I may be in your altar always serving,

And in your temple living as Samuel.

CHORUS

Awake me from my dream so I may hear you,

And give me a willing heart just like David's;

That I just like Samuel may respond,

Oh Lord your servant listens, here I am.

2

Whisper in my ear so I may hear you,

And I just like Samuel will answer you;

If useful I may be until I die,

Just order what you will and I'll obey.

3

Send me with your message to your people,

Anoint me with the oil of thy love;

That I may take the message to the lost ones,

And I shall have your blessing as Samuel.
