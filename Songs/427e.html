427 IT IS A TRUE PLEASURE

It is a true pleasure to serve Jesus Christ,

No payment is greater, or work more nobler;

I would like to serve Him, with faith and with love,

I promise to serve Him hence forth.

CHORUS

To serve Jesus Christ, to serve Him with faith,

What rich and sweet payment I'll have;

Suffering will not matter, He suffered for me,

I am happy, serving Jesus Christ.

2

I will speak the truth and always be faithful,

No matter if I lose everything for Him;

Such eternal riches in Christ I will have,

Hence forth I will only serve Him.

3

The hatred of this world for Him I will bear,

The burden I know without doubt will be hard;

I know that I will not ever lack His grace,

I will serve my Christ till the end.
