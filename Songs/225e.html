225 GLORY WE'RE SINGING

Glory we're singing to Jesus Christ,

Who is coming to this earth, with light and peace;

He's coming for His pure bride, who is prepared in white,

//The lovely church desires now to go with her groom//

CHORUS

And now my Brothers sealed in Christ Jesus,

We struggle against the prince of darkness;

//We see the victory in God's church always,

And with His glory Christ will crown us//

2

You who believe in Lord Jesus Christ,

May your heart not fall back, but be so strong;

Looking at the target: our Savior Jesus Christ,

//Triumphant we will be, and Jesus will crown us//

3

When our battle ends in this world,

A palm leaf and a robe we'll take with us there;

The church of God is calling, for you to be prepared,

//So you will be joyful in celestial riches//

4

When in the new city: Jerusalem,

We will sing of the victories with all the Saints;

Close to God's throne above, all eyes will surely see,

//There we'll proclaim Hosannas to the King of Israel//
