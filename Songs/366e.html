366 MY LIFE CANNOT REPAY YOU

My life cannot repay you, for blessed salvation,

You found me lost in this world so far away and deep in shame;

It is Jesus whom I love!

CHORUS

I'm marching in great love with the Lord's kindness,

I follow Jesus, till I see where He is.

2

You know I feel ashamed, because of all my sins,

This was the cause of your death, just to save my soul from death;

It is Jesus whom I love!

3

A cry I feel within me, of joy and gratitude,

Although I deserve nothing, my soul has tasted His sweet love;

It is Jesus whom I love!

4

An angel encamps around the soul who fears the Lord,

Around the Christian pilgrim, to save them from being condemned;

And to help them live with God!
