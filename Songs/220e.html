220 'TIS FROM HEAVEN

'Tis from heaven you were sanctified by God,

Not by man, but from the Lord your election came;

You're a fount that is flowing in the pathway,

Where the thirst of all sinners has been quenched.

CHORUS

'Tis the glory that to Jacob was revealed,

Was passed on through the centuries amen;

We are reaping the joy from God's salvation,

All one body through our Lord Jesus Christ.

2

Oh the promise dearest Jacob God gave you,

'Twas a promise bestowed unto you;

Your descendents will be like dust of the earth,

And forever be blessed by our Lord.

3

If your language speaks of Jesus and His love,

If a gift He has bestowed on those He loved;

You have whispered mysterious in Jacob's dreams,

In God's fear He deciphered the Lord's words.
