448 HOW PRECIOUS IS LIFE

How precious is life, in serving my Jesus,

//And praising my Lord// forever, amen.

CHORUS

What pleasure to serve Thee no matter where I am,

//To live by His grace// forever, amen.

2

In suffering with Jesus, I am most secure,

//I'm praising His name// forever, amen.

3

//So take now your cross// forever, amen.
