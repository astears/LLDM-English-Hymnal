469 ALL ARE REJOICING

All are rejoicing, those who are marked in Lord Christ,

Let's march behind close to the Lord;

Serving the Lord with faithfulness,

//Until to Zion we fly//

CHORUS

//Zion// as soldiers marching go,

To this city full of sunshine, where Jesus Christ does reign.

2

With peace in your heart, march without fear God sees,

Leaving behind our grief and pains;

Our sight is only placed on Christ,

//Until to Zion we fly//

3

I won't be fearful, in front of us goes the Lord,

From treacherous dangers He will save;

'Cause Jesus is our Captain, Lord,

//Until to Zion we fly//

4

Glory and honor to the celestial King,

With all His people He will reign;

No more pain or grief we will feel,

//In this celestial place//
