531 THE TRUMPETS ARE SOUNDING

The trumpets are sounding in the highest heaven,

And the multitudes are singing songs of joy;

It's 'cause they are passing all those who are valiant,

Who on earth have loved, Jesus Christ their Lord.

CHORUS

No, //never no// don't ever separate me,

No, //never no// never should I leave Him;

Your great love you had shown me, when He came to save my soul,

No, //never no// never should I, be far away from Him.

2

Prudent virgins are those, members of the Lord's church,

Of Christ Jesus Savior, the most good Pastor;

He that has the oil, and a lamp ascended,

Because he is waiting for his Savior king.

3

Others who are weary walk with tears of sadness,

Because they are feeling a great bitter pain;

Because the divine judge, with a flaming finger,

Has denied them entrance to the His place above.

4

Which are you my brother? Of those who are joyful,

Or of those who suffer separation here;

If you like rejoicing, stand up tall, be fighting,

Diligently working to save your dear soul.
