187 IN HIGHEST HEAVENS

In highest heavens all of the angels will,

Praise God the Father in one sweet voice;

Also the righteous, on all the earth will be,

Praising the Lord with eagerness.

CHORUS

Glory, Hosanna to our Lord, Savior and the King,

My redeemer too; Glory, Hosanna,

He is coming soon,

Coming to lift up His church.

2

The day is coming when Jesus Christ returns,

He'll come to lift up, all holy beings;

To they who sleep now, in Jesus Christ the Lord,

He will with His voice, waken them up.

3

The everlasting God in the heavens,

Who made the earth, also the seas;

Let's give the honor, to Him who made us,

The Kings of kings, our Savior Lord.
