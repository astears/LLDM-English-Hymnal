221 ON A SIXTH OF APRIL

On a sixth of April, a day of great blessing,

A man was elected, to save all humanity;

His name was changed by God, to the name of Brother Aaron,

//Aaron Joaquin will be your name, these were words spoken by God//

CHORUS

Awaken oh great Church of God, and behold your ambassador,

He has left us God's doctrine, teachings without error;

As the good Samaritan, He healed and cured our wounds,

//His remembrance we'll honor, engraving it in our hearts//

2

With a staff from our God, He governed well all His people,

The grace that God gave Him, for salvation for our souls;

Come and drink all you thirsty, from this fountain full of love,

For the table is ready, there is abundance of bread;

For all those sufferings we cost Him, He now lives with Jesus Christ.

3

Your children remember, all of your hard works and love,

Working to save our souls, He never gave himself rest;

But the good Lord gave him rest, the One whom had sent him,

The seeds you planted on this earth, no one can uproot them;

Your dear friend who had sustained you, He will take care of your works.
