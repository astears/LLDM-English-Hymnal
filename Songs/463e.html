463 BLESSINGS I WANT TO FEEL

Blessings I want to feel, oh holy God,

In the reunion of your faithful people;

I want to sing, and to rejoice,

With spiritual devotion Lord.

CHORUS

Oh Lord of peace, cleanse me each moment more,

So that I may partake in the banquet you have prepared.

2

My heart awaits to feel God within,

And feel communion with His Holy Spirit;

I want to sing and to rejoice,

Filled with your spiritual strength.

3

There is still hope left within my heart oh Lord,

To hold the crown of eternal salvation;

I want to sing and to rejoice,

In God's celestial dwellings.
