325 THE CHILDREN BELONG TO JESUS

The children belong to Jesus; He is their Savior Lord,

They are such precious jewels; He bought them with His love.

CHORUS

///Precious/// They belong to Jesus Christ,

Living among us in this world they serve as light and love.

2

The children are like rich treasures; they are from heaven too,

They give refulgent light in time of affliction too.

3

The children are like the stars, giving us clarity,

Jesus wants them announcing, His truth around the world.

4

The children belong to Jesus, and He will come for them,

And with Him for all centuries, blessedly they will live.
