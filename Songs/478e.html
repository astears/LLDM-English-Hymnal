478 OH HOLY MAN

Oh Holy Man, your heritage is holy,

It's come from above, from heaven it was given;

Before your birth, you were already chosen,

We'll always be with you, and we always confirm;

Our loyalty to you.

CHORUS

Oh Church, Light of the World, walk on triumphant,

Singing a hymn of joy, firmly walk forward;

The Lord our God gave us a Valiant Prince,

We'll always be with him, heeding and obeying;

The voice of our God.

2

Glory to God, communion and alliance,

Peace and good will are within our hearts;

You will be strong courageous in your battle,

And you'll have His grace, fulfilling your mission;

With all who hear your word.

3

Hymns sung with love, resounding in the temple,

With great esteem, God's children all are singing;

Our anguish has passed, also our pain and sadness,

For we have been united, in constant prayer and pleading;

With Jesus our Lord.
