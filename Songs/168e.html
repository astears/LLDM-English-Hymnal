168 THOSE WHO PREVAIL

Those who prevail here, will be attired,

With vestments that are whitened;

And his name won't be deleted,

From the great book of life.

CHORUS

With courage yes, and with our love,

Yes, we'll be marching on till we arrive;

With courage yes, and with our love,

Let us be marching towards Him of Zion.

2

And through the desert we're marching onward,

Let us follow our Jesus;

He will be there to shield us,

During the tribulation.

3

After the victory here on this earth,

A precious crown awaits us;

With palm leaves in our hands,

Triumphantly we'll enter.
