257 BEAUTIFUL

///Beautiful/// are all the words of my dear God,

///Beautiful/// is the true pathway of the Lord;

For this I'm continually praying, giving the glories to the Lord,

He is my best friend, my courage;

He guards me always through His love.

2

///Beautiful/// spiritual communion with God,

///Beautiful/// giving eternal life someday;

God sends it to us by His grace, it is the peace of Jesus Christ,

We feel the Lord's Holy Spirit, In the Apostle of the Lord.

3

///Beautiful/// is the holy doctrine of God,

A present from heaven God sends us:

the great Apostle of the Lord;

And now I do follow the footsteps, of a faithful man on earth,

For this I am happily saying:

Be welcomed Brother in the Lord.
