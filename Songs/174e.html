174 IN THE GARDEN

In the garden of the Lord there's many plants,

They give off the most delightful aroma;

Which reaches the celestial throne of Jesus,

Where the Almighty dwells now and forever.

CHORUS

Let us all sing Hallelujahs to the Lamb,

Let us say that Jesus Christ is our King;

He is guiding us through rough and thorny pathways,

So up to heaven we will go to praise the Lord.

2

Now the people that Christ has been redeeming,

Hallelujahs they are giving to the Lamb;

And they must be victorious over evil,

For they've confided in the Holy Father.

3

When our trials seem so hard and so fatiguing,

I raise up all my pleas to my Redeemer;

And the answer comes to me in an instant,

From Him of Counsel, my Master up above.
