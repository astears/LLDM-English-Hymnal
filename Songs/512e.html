512 IF WE'RE SOLDIERS

If we're soldiers in a war,

Of the troops of Jesus Christ;

Let's not disregard our job,

We must fight until the end.

CHORUS

March on, you soldiers of Christ Jesus,

March on, march with the cross of Jesus;

March on, follow your leader Christ,

May our enemies of battle, Flee from the name of Christ.

2

If the victory's on our side,

If we follow Jesus Christ;

With His mighty arm with us,

He will help us overcome.

3

Though the battle may be strong,

Go on forward says the Lord;

Valor faith put in your hearts,

And to no one we should fear.

4

If a soldier's falling down,

Ask for courage to your Lord;

He is able to sustain,

Any soldier with His power.

5

All our crowns await for us,

In the palace of Jesus;

And a faithful soldier will,

Obtain a crown full of pearls.
