002 I WANT TO SERCE THE LORD JESUS

I want to serve the Lord Jesus,

While I'm living in the world;

For you will be my helper Lord,

You are my God, my Savior.

CHORUS

With you I will live, and suffer never more;

You are a haven to the poor, for this I will praise you my Lord.

2

You're safe haven for the meager,

Firm foundation with your love;

By your kindness and grace, I am now saved,

I praise your name forevermore.

3

I was once lost and you found me,

Your love gave me liberty;

Your sorrow on the cross, gave me new life,

For which I'll ever praise your Name.

4

And when the struggle has ended,

I will dwell eternally;

By your side I will be and then rejoice,

Living with you, my Savior.
