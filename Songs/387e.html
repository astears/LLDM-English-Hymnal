387 OH CHRIST COME LISTEN

Oh Christ come listen to my prayer, attend to my clamor,

I'll only have refuge in you;

If my heart faints I know that I will only come to you,

I'll only have refuge in you.

CHORUS

Oh take me to the rock that is higher than me,

Take me now my Lord, I will follow you;

Oh take me to the rock that is higher than me,

I'll only have refuge in you.

2

No matter if I journey to far places on this earth,

I'll only have refuge in you;

I will always be thinking that in your kindness I'm safe,

I'll only have refuge in you.

3

In you I find my strength and courage to walk forward more,

I'll only have refuge in you;

The evil doer keeps trying to make me fall down but can't,

I'll only have refuge in you.

4

In your presence I will abide without disputes or fear,

I'll only have refuge in you;

I'll always feel surrounding me, your wings full of love,

I'll only have refuge in you.

5

I will be always praising you, forever without cease,

I'll only have refuge in you; On your altar,

I will pay all my vows to my Lord,

I'll only have refuge in you.
