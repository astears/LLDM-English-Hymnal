055 BEAUTIFUL ARE YOUR WORDS

Beautiful are your words, Aaron, Father of our faith,

They bring health, happiness, and a reason for living;

Your only plan was to save and to heal the injured soul,

I am yours, Come and save us my Lord!

2

A Tuesday, 6th of April, your name was changed by God,

A name of blessings, fortune, salvation for my soul;

You always were so faithful, you bore thousands of trials,

Your living in our memories, your love and goodness too.

3

Proclaim Hosannas! Let's all together proclaim it,

Two misfortunate men, were seen in Jericho;

Christ has freed them from misery, and His pardon He gives,

Darkness passed, the light is shining on.

4

With palms placed in their hands, all God's people will exclaim,

Mounted up on a horse, all His virtues He gave;

All the children and old age also all the youth too,

Will all say, "Blessed are you my King!"
