390 OH SEE HOW PRECIOUS

Oh see how precious when we're with Jesus,

To love Him always with all our hearts;

Leaving this world with all of its glory,

All we expect is the protection of our Christ.

Yes, Jesus will come, upon the high clouds,

With glory and majesty;

He will take His church with Him,

For time is ending very soon.

2

All of the souls who await in Jesus,

They're always watchful living in prayer;

They are obeying the good Lord's words,

They are all waiting for the blessing of the Lord.

3

The cup is waiting up in the heavens,

The one Jesus said He would not drink from;

Until his people enter triumphal,

Giving their loud praises with joy and happiness.

4

This is the time that has been announced,

When Jesus Christ would make His return;

Also Jesus Christ announced and preached it,

Before the people begin to deny their faith.
