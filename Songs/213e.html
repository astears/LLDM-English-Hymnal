213 THIS IS THE CHURCH

This is the church of God, of Jesus our Lord,

He who let his bloodshed run down the cross;

This is the loving wife, the one who is spotless,

And pure and clean in their hearts and souls.

CHORUS

This is the church of God, of the living God,

The column and the support of all the truth;

It's the wife of Christ, living in holiness,

The Pillar and the ground of all the truth.

2

This is the lovely wife, of the Lord Jesus Christ,

The one which He bought, over there on the cross;

This is the church of God, of our Savior Jesus,

And Christ has rescued her with His love.

3

This is the wife of Christ, beloved by all,

She is now prepared, for heavenly mansions;

This is the faithful wife, of the Lamb of God,

Who will praise the Lord forever amen.
