455 WHO IS THIS

Who is this who appears just as the dawning,

Beautiful as the moon and, shining clearly as the sun;

So impressive just like soldiers all in order,

This is the church of the Lord.

CHORUS

//Hallelujah// to Jehovah King,

Always walking ahead of us;

Glory to God up in the heavens, and on earth be peace,

To all wise men with their spirits of good will.

2

Oh leave all you virgins and go, from Zion to see Solomon,

With a golden crown that was placed on His head;

The joyful day of delight in his heart,

This is my King, Jesus Christ my Lord.

3

Who is this that arises from the desert,

Swiftly as the smoke arises, it's the church of Jesus Christ;

It was purchased with the blood of Jesus Christ,

We're hoping always for Christ.

4

This is the church of the Lord, once in the desert,

By the words of our Father, up in heaven watching down;

Now Christ Jesus made it shine just as the sun shines,

Come you who sins to Jesus Christ.
