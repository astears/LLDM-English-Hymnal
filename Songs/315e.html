315 I'VE GIVEN JESUS MY WHOLE LIFE

I've given Jesus my whole life,

A hope that changed the way I lived past times ago;

He saved my soul through His grace that is so blessed,

He chose me so that I fulfill all that He wants.

CHORUS

Nothing is nobler, nothing more worthy,

Than consecrating our whole lives to Jesus Christ;

Needles and thorns that are in His pathway,

His blessed presence is the one that sanctifies it.

2

I understand what the price is,

To follow Jesus in the steps of His labor;

It does not matter I will keep my forehead upright,

And by your grace I'll be more than a conqueror.

3

I could not pay you with my life,

What your sweet love in Calvary did for my soul;

My life and faith at your full service are surrendered,

Just as a consecrated offering to you.

4

I consecrate to you my life,

It's for your service oh my precious Savior Lord;

A crown of glory I will wear up in the heavens,

For every soul that I can rescue through your love.
