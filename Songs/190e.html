190 IN THE DENSE DARKNESS

In the dense darkness I saw my soul stranded,

And I was living without hope in this world;

But soon I heard the voice of Jesus calling,

It was my future that through His word I saw.

CHORUS

Beautiful light that shone in me,

I found it deep within my heart;

When Jesus Christ came to save my soul,

This was the moment His light came shining through.

2

Now I have joy with all of them rejoicing,

Now I can sing so delighted and with joy;

Now I can join the fighters in this battle,

Because Christ Jesus is reigning in my soul.

3

What happy moment I remember feeling,

When Jesus Christ came to liberate my soul;

I recognized the Lord's tender voice calling,

And in that moment my soul was filled joy.
