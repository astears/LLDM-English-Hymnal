584 THEY ARE SOUNDING

They are sounding, blissful songs of joy,

Forever be sanctity; Salvation is heard from on high,

Forever be sanctity.

CHORUS

//Forever be sanctity// Let us all praise,

Jesus, the King of glory;

Forever be holiness.

2

From innate sin God has cleansed our souls,

Forever be sanctity;

With His perfect love He has filled us,

Forever be sanctity.

3

We will raise our banners faithfully,

Forever be sanctity;

We'll be fighting without turning back,

Forever be sanctity.

4

Praises, glories we give to the Lord,

Forever be sanctity;

We are singing all unto the Lord,

Forever be sanctity.
