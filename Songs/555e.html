555 YOU'RE A VALIANT SERVANT

You're a valiant Servant of God, Oh we know it,

Man of God; Your grand faith is never absent,

That which Jesus gave to you.

CHORUS

Fight with valor, oh my Brother,

Fighting bravely, don't cease;

Your enemies are placed in your hands,

In the Name of our dear Lord.

2

//You are valiant// Not one thing,

Man, you do fear;

May the Lord be at your right side,

And the oppressor you'll beat.

3

In this battle there's much bloodshed,

It is terrible with rage; In the heat of the long battle,

Invoke the name of your God.

4

You be strong when in the battle,

The Lord has for you a prize;

Jesus Christ's ears are wide open,

To listen to all your prayers.
