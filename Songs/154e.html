154 THEY ARE SWEET MOMENTS OF PRAYER

Sweet moments of prayer and praise to God,

The ones that pass at God's altar;

When I contritely go implore,

I feel God's blessings over me.

CHORUS

Oh Brother draw to the altar now,

Bring to the Lord your heart and soul;

Come with your brothers to enjoy,

All of God's blessings, He's prepared.

2

If my cries flood my prayer to God,

If they're disturbing what I plea;

Although I'm crying without words,

My God does know my heart and soul.

3

God doesn't hate the sinful heart,

He wants to pardon all our sins;

When humbly we kneel at His feet,

God gives His children blessing there.
