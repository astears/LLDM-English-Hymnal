376 I CANNOT FIND JUST HOW TO PLEASE

I cannot find just how to please my dear Lord Jesus,

I'd like to give Him something from all my possessions;

But even the air that I breathe here is not mine,

My soul, my body, my heart I give to you Lord.

CHORUS

Grateful we are to you Jesus,

For your great blessed salvation;

Here take, I give you all my heart,

So you may take away my vain illusions too.

2

I know you're watching and your always walking by me,

And as a measly little earthworm I am moving;

I searched for glory on this earth and could not find it,

But now I have my glory only placed in you.

3

There is only two paths your word has come to show us,

One's very narrow and the door's very constricted;

The other's wider but it leads me to the abyss,

The narrow path is what I chose to follow Lord.

4

Do not be late in doing your work in my soul,

So when you come back I will be prepared and ready;

Help me to constantly be watching and be praying,

So that I may be free from tribulation Lord.
