433 YOU'VE TAKEN

You've taken all of my heart, my God beloved,

You've hastened all of my heart with your love;

I was vile, yes! But, however, you gave me your love,

Just to rejoice with Him.

CHORUS

Only my God, has had compassion for me,

Only His love, came to give me life;

Death had me, already harshly imprisoned,

And I did not have any hope;

But Jesus Christ gave life unto my being.

2

My Savior you were my beloved Jesus,

You've given your blessing to me, of great health;

Unfaithful I had lived but you gave me your peace,

This to rejoice, oh King.

3

I want to in the end arrive my Jesus.

And be in your grand mansion of your light;

I will see the joyful face of my God in Zion;

And I will shine with Him.
