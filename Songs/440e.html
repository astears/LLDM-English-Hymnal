440 WHAT BEAUTY TO BE

What beauty to be, in the arms of Jesus Christ,

What beauty to go, from the darkness to His light;

To love, always love, Him who gave us light and health,

My heart is overflowing, with immense gratitude.

CHORUS

Fill, Jesus Christ all of my heart,

Fill it up more and more my Lord;

Now that you've pardoned all my sins,

Fill it up with your holy love.

2

Sunk below the tides of sea, I once was living so,

And your matchless love, took me from my filthy sins;

And to know that everything came from you my Lord,

It just makes me want to cry, from immense gratitude.

3

//If you want to taste// the love of the Lord,

Move close to the Lord, all your sins He will forgive with His love;

//If you want to gain// salvation from God,

Be close to the Lord, you will never find love like our God's.
