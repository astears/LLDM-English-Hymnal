371 NECESSITY WE'RE FEELING

Necessity we're feeling, of Jesus Christ the King,

Because in Him we have peace, a joy without compare;

We bless you Christ forever oh Jesus our Lord.

CHORUS

Jesus, all of the souls are feeling, necessity of you,

Because you do sustain them, and they are saved through you.

2

Because you nurture them with your wonderful love.

Creator of the heavens, to you celestial King,

We ask your consolation, a spiritual manna;
