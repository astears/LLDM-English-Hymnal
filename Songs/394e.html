394 OH GREAT APOSTLE

Oh great Apostle searching for the faithful soldiers,

They will be lined against the devil and will fight;

//If this is what you are searching we are here now,

And we are ready to follow your commands//

2

The enemy will not bring us down or scare us,

Because our Jesus is marching on with us;

//If you are calling the warriors out to battle,

We are prepared with swords now in our hands//

3

Oh see them shining we are holding them in our hands,

We are only waiting to hear the high command;

//So that we may go after the adversary,

To take the souls who are grounded by His power//

4

In the militia of the Lord they need more soldiers,

They need to be preparing to fight in the war;

//And if you want it we all are here and willing,

We want to always be helping in your work//

5

Your father always wanted more who could help Him,

At that time there weren't enough to fulfill His wish;

//But we are here now to do only as you please,

For you the Prince and Apostle of the Lord//
