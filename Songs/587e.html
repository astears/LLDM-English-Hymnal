587 THIS GREAT DAY IS APPROACHING

This great day is approaching, of the things Christ promised us,

If we faithfully serve Him, we will find the things above.

CHORUS

//Hallelujah, Christ the King is coming//

All of the angels walking in the light; Sing of the wonders of His love.

2

All the hard work you're doing, Jesus Christ will pay your soul,

When on clouds He's descending, in His kingdom you will find.

3

Constantly continue fighting, rewards in heaven you'll have,

In Christ Jesus grand coming, you will enter through the gates.

4

Christ looks for all the valiant, those not looking to the past,

Although struggling in battle, they never denounce their God.
