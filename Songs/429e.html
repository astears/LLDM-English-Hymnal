429 UNITL DEATH FIGHTING

Until death fighting for the Lord Jesus,

I will live always constant to His flag;

And although my fortune is deceiving,

I'll never fear again, trusting in God.

CHORUS

My inspiration comes from Christ,

His truth alleviates the heart;

A light that can destroy darkness,

The most beauteous reality.

United to Christ and by faith,

I will with joy always tell Him;

If you are calling, here I am,

Oh blessed Jesus, I am thine.

2

That my life be fully consecrated,

May it always be lived for my Jesus;

As I follow Christ throughout my journey,

I'll never fear again, I trust in God.

3

Come to Christ for He's the one who calls you,

And abandon all of your transgressions;

And after being baptized in His Name,

His Holy Spirit the Lord will give you.
