535 WHEN YOU LET SIN GUIDE

When you let sin guide your path you will sink your ship at sea,

You will be lost more and more in the vast sea;

The sea waves will carry your ship farther out from the shore,

You will be lost more and more in the vast sea.

CHORUS

You'll be deviating more, more and more, in the vast and stormy sea,

You will deviate more, from our Father's love above;

And towards such a fatal death, You are deviating slowly more and more.

2

When your light shines on the waters, you will note all of the dangers,

You are slowly deviating more and more;

There's a storm ahead of you, you are drifting from the dock,

You are deviating slowly more and more.

3

All these voices in your soul who are calling you will cease,

You are slowly deviating more and more;

Oh my Brother, hear the voice, for only this voice saves you,

Before you go deviating more and more.

4

Don't you hear Christ calling you? He sees that you deviate,

You're in danger, a great danger more and more;

If in wandering you insist, He cannot come to your aid,

And your death is coming closer more and more.
