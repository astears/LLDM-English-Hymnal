300 THE HOPE

The hope of every Christian is Jesus,

And to rejoice arriving with Christ;

In the kingdom of light and of glory,

This is where all His children will be.

CHORUS

There are dwellings where He is awaiting,

Filled with life, with joy, and with peace;

With His blood He has come to retrieve us,

To the faithful He promises aid.

2

How often all the faithful have suffered,

As they all kept their lives close to Him;

And their names have been already written,

In the book of the living of God.

3

If you would like to fight oh you Christians,

You should always ask the Lord for help;

With His strength He will help you prevail,

If you suffer you will have your prize.

4

Oh how happy are all of the brothers,

Keeping themselves with Christ till the end;

They will someday contemplate his dwellings,

If they suffer with him till the end.
