426 MERCY OH HOLY GOD

Mercy oh holy God, mercy,

Mercy my heart implores of you;

Oh wash me from my wickedness!

Give me forgiveness, peace, and joy.

2

All my transgressions are so grave,

They are all only against you;

However come renew my heart,

And a new spirit put in me.

3

You want no other sacrifice,

But a contrite and broken heart;

My offering you won't despise,

For you are fully compassion.

4

Save me oh God with your great power,

Because you are my only hope;

Trembling I try to keep your will,

Show your compassion unto me.
