303 THE GLORY OF JESUS

The glory of Jesus the Lord I will sing,

It fills up my heart with much joy and much peace;

To not speak of favors which I have received,

My lips are not able to close.

CHORUS

He always shows mercy for me,

With Him I can never have craves;

For all of the highest desires I have,

It takes only Him to fulfill.

2

In hours of anguish Jesus is with me,

And the sweetest voice which is His I can hear;

That tells me and gives me ineffable peace,

The infinite peace of the Lord.

3

If in harsh battles He can see that I fight,

And lets me to fight them all on my own;

But if in an instant He sees me retreat,

Salvation He comes offer me.

4

Also when in gladness I see Him arrive,

Then all of my blessing the Lord will increase;

He'll fill up my cup, so it runneth well o'er,

With all of His beloved gifts.
