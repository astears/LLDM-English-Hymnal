356 MY PEOPLE HARKEN

My people harken to my voice, oh listen to my call,

I have saved thee am your God, I want to forgive thee.

CHORUS

///Oh come and open up my heart///

I will be satisfied.

2

Oh if my people were so true, salvation I would give,

I would even break open the chains, from the cruel enemy.

3

My people will not hearken me, they won't obey my voice,

I will leave them in agony, I know they will be lost.

4

If they repent and come to me, I will be merciful,

Sufficient my power will be, to heal and liberate.

5

I will give them my blessing with, rich honey and bread,

Their souls will be well satisfied, they will joyously sing.
