049 EVEN THOUGH YOU ARE SO ANGUISHED

Even though you are so anguished, it will be your task,

To never give up, and always trust in Jesus Christ;

//Who will give you liberty//, and protect you from all faults,

So that invincible you will be!

CHORUS

//I will fight// In Jesus Christ our King,

Through Jesus Christ our King;

I'll overcome the devil too,

//And I will shine inside// that courage in me,

Because I'm always ready, to follow through.

2

When iniquity calls you, your duty will be,

To always reject, all of the dangers around you,

//Which can disturb your soul//, that might keep you from the Lord,

So invincible you must be.

3

Through His great love we will challenge, the power of sin,

And all errors too, and through the doctrine of Jesus;

That will give us all His love, Christ is the way and a light,

He is a spiritual guide, so you may win a heavenly home!
