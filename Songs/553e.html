553 YOU HAVE SUCH A GLORIOUS EXAMPLE

You have such a glorious example here for me,

Born in the city of Bethlehem;

In palace or mansion, you were not born,

So a manger is where you were born.

CHORUS

//Oh come in to my heart, oh Jesus come,

I have made room in it just for you//

2

The angels are now raising their celestial hymns,

Peace and goodwill is what they proclaim;

But so humble you came to the earth, oh my Lord,

To give life to the doers of sin.

3

The raccoons on this earth can now have their own caves,

And the birds make their nests just as well;

But the Son of Man never found a home for Him,

Where He could be reclining His head.

4

You came down my good Lord bringing blessing from high,

To give us liberty and our health;

But with hate and despises they drove you to death,

Though they saw your great virtue and love.
