081 JUST LIKE THE DAWNING

Just like the dawning that brings the splendor,

And makes the darkness all disappear;

Just like the rain in the barren pasture,

You are like that with your authority.

And the true doctrine that God has left you,

Will give all its fruits of holiness;

And a new song we'll joyfully sing,

For God's election that in His Servant comes to outshine.

CHORUS

Who will they be? Who will sing the glorious song,

Who will they be? Wearing vestments that are whitened;

Who will they be? Those who will reign with the Redeemer,

Who will they be? Those who accept your authority.

2

The firmament has been decorated,

With all the stars, the sun, and moon;

Also the Lord has adorned you Brother,

With all the virtues and the election.

Sing nightingales and all of the gold finch,

The rivers sounding, and mighty sea;

Angels are singing, let us be singing,

Yes God do bless your great majesty.

3

A staff of glory the Lord has left you,

A crown of beauty He's placed on you;

Of finest pearls it's brightly shinning,

Beautiful jewels diamonds they are.

The faithful souls who have taken victory,

Purest adornments of greatest price;

Beautiful glory the Lord has left you,

Just like He gave to Brother Aaron.
