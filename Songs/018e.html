018 AT THE BREAK OF DAWN

At the break of dawn, of a beautiful morning,

When the birds sweetly sing songs to the Lord;

I can then hear a sweet voice that tells me,

You should take your time to pray.

CHORUS

In Christ I put all of my burdens,

He is more than life in me;

He changes all of my worries,

Every day He accompanies me;

But today He invites us all,

To the garden of prayer;

You should take your time to pray.

2

If your pathway's light has been covered by darkness,

And you want to depart from the Lord;

He has taught, me to pray, without ceasing,

You should take your time to pray.

3

If you come seek me; I will wipe away your tears,

When many people turn away, denying help;

I will ease all your sadness and your pain,

If you take some time to pray.
