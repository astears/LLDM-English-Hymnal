014 ON TO THE BATTLE

On to the battle, on to great vict'ry,

Forward with God our King;

His arm in power strong and so mighty,

Always defending His church.

And without fear let's go forward,

Enthusiastic with faith;

While we are joyfully singing,

Glory to God our King.

CHORUS

Victory in Jesus, comes not to the swift,

Nor the battle for the strong;

But for the faithful in Jesus,

He's their eternal relief.

2

On to the battle, on to great vict'ry,

Who might this pleasant King be?

Which is the army, who follow this King?

In this great battle of faith.

It's my Lord Jesus the bravest,

He is the master of Lords;

With Him we are marching fearless,

All those who cherish His love.

3

On to the battle, on to great vict'ry,

Under such great leadership;

When evil forces come forth in battle,

His people will swiftly win.

And all the armies of Satan,

We will see them pass on by;

But those who love the Lord Jesus,

Eternal life they will gain.
