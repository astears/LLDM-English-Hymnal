330 BATTLING FOREVER BATTLING

Battling forever battling,

Triumphant we have arrived;

Oh Lord we have walked in your courts,

A mansion, oh church of love.

The cherubim's also lived there,

And there's a consuming fire.

CHORUS

Oh blessed land you are the most beloved on this earth,

//You have received a name that is worthy;

A colony of sons of God//

2

Beautiful province you have been,

Joy to all of the whole earth;

Many missioners left from you,

Wherever they walk they're preaching.

Through their words we've 'come believers,

And faith we have to reach heaven.

3

Church of God the Lord has sealed you,

And sent consolation to you;

In your breast you're an asylum,

To the poor, also the sinner.

Receive us with arms wide-open,

Church of God, reigning in love.
