536 REVIVE US AGAIN

We praise thee, Oh God, for the Son of Thy love,

For Jesus who died and has now gone above.

CHORUS

Hallelujah! Thine the glory,

Hallelujah! Amen! Hallelujah!

Thine the glory, revive us again.

2

We praise Thee, oh God, for Thy Spirit of light,

Who has shown us our Savior and scattered our night.

3

All glory and praise to the Lamb who was slain,

Who has borne all our sins, and has cleansed every stain.

4

Revive us again, fill each heart with Thy love,

May each soul be rekindled with fire from above.
