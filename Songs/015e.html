015 TO JACOB'S FOUNTAIN

To Jacob's fountain, the Lord arrived,

Jesus Christ the Lord, water He asked for;

To a woman he saw closely by,

Jesus told the woman give me water now to drink.

CHORUS

Samaria received salvation from Lord Christ,

Because of a woman speaking of His word;

And telling the news, news that Christ had given her,

Then she told the people "Come, come, and see;

Who spoke to me, if perhaps He is Christ the Lord."

2

This woman then became quite amazed,

That the man she saw revealed all to her;

With great joy she entered town to tell,

And announcing the surprise she felt within her heart.

3

The Samaritan then understood,

The gift of the Lord; that was close to her;

For this reason also Christ gave her,

Living water that gives all eternal life.

4

He who wants should come and always drink,

The water of life, Jesus Christ prepared;

It is free for those who will accept,

Don't despise Him; He invites you with most sincere love.
