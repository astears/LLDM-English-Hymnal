446 WHAT JOY I HAVE

What joy I have now, because I'm saved,

I felt it in my heart today; I was a stranger,

but Jesus brought me,

To be a part of His great mission.

CHORUS

He died for me, and gave me life,

And with His blood my sins were erased;

How great the love, that Jesus gave me,

And with His spirit He baptized me.

2

I was astray in ways of darkness,

In all my evils and without fear;

I lived without this joy I feel now,

I was awaiting the judgment day.

3

Precious the tidings that one day I heard,

From God's own messenger sent to earth;

He spoke of Jesus, and of His doctrine,

He wants salvation for my soul.

4

Now I have freedom, because of Jesus,

Since He gave pardon to all my sins;

I live secure and fear no more,

This blessed promise He gave to me.
