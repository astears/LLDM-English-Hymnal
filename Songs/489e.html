489 OH LORD KNEELED AT YOUR FEET

Oh Lord, kneeled at your feet, to give you praises,

'Cause I was walking lost without my God;

You've brought me with your love to join your people,

You adopted me as your son with love.

CHORUS

I give you thanks, oh my God,

Because your Son, His life gave for me;

I only ask to serve you faithfully,

Because my life is yours Lord, and you are my God.

2

Remembering that one day, I said with my lips,

I swore I'll never leave you oh my Lord;

I want to keep my promise that I swore you,

'Cause the sinner you love and want to change.

3

Oh Lord, you've guarded me in all your pathways,

In my affliction you have held my hand;

And faithfully I'll follow in your pathway,

'Cause your love gives me strength to march along.
