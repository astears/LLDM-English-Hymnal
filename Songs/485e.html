485 OH LORD JESUS WHAT BEAUTY

Oh Lord Jesus what beauty, the church in which you reign,

My soul suspires for this church, it does without a cease.

CHORUS

Come, oh friend come, to God's temple,

Oh, come to the home of the Lord;

Gives me joy to hear those who do say come now,

To the house of our Lord let's proceed.

2

My souls filled with great longing, also your church would like to see,

And I want to give Jesus, my soul and all my heart.

3

One day in your court's better, with you in sanctity,

Than thousands filled with happiness, in pathways of evil.

4

Blessed are those who enter, the temple of the Lord,

For in your grace obtaining, your vigor and your joy.

5

I do prefer the gateway, the celestial temple Lord,

Than the uncertain pathways, or worldly joys and bliss.
