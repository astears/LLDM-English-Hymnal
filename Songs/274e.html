274 JEHOVAH IS MY GOD

Jehovah is my God, He who reigns in all my heart and soul,

Temptation you have freed from my soul, and from all the dangers too;

And now I only want to live in holiness, humility,

Because you are my God, jealous in your love.

2

Hosannas we sing to the one who reigns in all of our hearts,

Your praises we give you 'cause He's freed our souls from hell;

And now happily going, to God singing a song, with much love,

To where He is awaiting with all of His heart.

3

Joyously my soul is, glorified in the Lord God,

Through your loving kindness to our souls He gives;

I'm praying constantly to the Lord with my heart,

He answers me 'cause He isn't weak and He loves me so.
