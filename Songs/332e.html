332 THE TIMES ARE NOW AMONG US

The times are now among us, the days have been fulfilled,

Of all the prophecies that were given by the Lord;

As they left from the temple to the mount of the olives,

Along with words of wisdom, prophecies He told too.

CHORUS

// As they left from the temple to the mount of the olives,

Along with words of wisdom, prophecies He told too//

2

Today we see these new times are briefly passing by us,

And all the signs that tell us our Lord is coming back;

He speaks to us through the wars He speaks to us through the winds,

The time is coming closer when Jesus will return.

3

All things have been fulfilled now, there's nothing else to wait for,

If anyone is sleeping it's time to be awake;

In Him well find the cure and faithful consolation,

The King of heaven will come to take his Church with Him.

4

Why wouldn't Jesus carry your sins and your transgressions?

For He has come to save us with His death on the cross;

He is your advocate and for you He's interceding,

He wishes to redeem you as proof of His great love.
