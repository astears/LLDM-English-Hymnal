178 OH ON THIS BEAUTIFUL MORNING

Oh on this beautiful morning, morning of blessing too,

In which the dawn of this day, awakens with a smile;

Goodness and cheer is now with us, in every breath we take,

We come with gladness to greet you, you anointed of the Lord.

CHORUS

Beautiful morning of perfect peace,

Of cheer and gladness and happiness;

There will be many blessings today,

May the hand of God be with you;

Where ever you may go.

2

The sweet perfumes of the flowers, birds also come and sing,

The golden rays of the bright sun, come greet you on this day;

With happiness overflowing, we come to sing and to pray,

Hoping your life is extended, for all of eternity.

3

Never in need will we find you, you have God's grace and love,

Because the Lord's always ready, to answer when you call;

He said He'll never forsake you, that is His holy word,

And if you ever feel weary, like a shield He'll cover you.
