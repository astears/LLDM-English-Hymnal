560 ONE DAY CHRIST WILL COME

One day Christ will come back to earth,

He promised when He rose above;

We will all see Him the same way,

And His people will see the King Jesus Christ.

CHORUS

Yes very soon, Jesus will come,

And his people will gladly see Him;

We should always be watching and in prayer,

So that we may see Him that day.

2

The Messengers sent by the Lord,

Confirm that Jesus will return;

So does the Spirit of our God,

In the Bible He has promised this to us.

3

Oh what glory without compare,

It will be when we see our King;

His beautiful face will outshine,

It will be an eclipse for the sun.

4

Oh welcome back King Jesus Christ,

Your church has been waiting for you;

Our faith will never be in vain,

Our pleasure is Lord for you to return.
