351 WHILE I'M LIVING

While I'm living in this cruel world,

Enemies in all places I will have;

To the Lord I'm always asking,

In the struggle to please increase my faith.

CHORUS

Jesus increase more of my faith, yes, increase more of my faith,

And each one of my foes I will defeat;

Jesus increase more of my faith, yes, increase more of my faith,

Oh my Jesus, you're able to do this.

2

In this world I am detested,

In no way I will let this sadden me;

The lord Jesus is victorious,

Grace He gives me to triumph in the world.

3

The devil is our enemy,

Once he came close to tempt Lord Jesus Christ,

Now he draws near but I follow,

The straight pathway the Lord has left behind.

4

Many powerful enemies,

Are in pursuit of ways to ruin us;

But the Lord says: fear no more now,

By your side I will always remain now.
