539 I HAVE A FAITHFUL FRIEND

I have a faithful friend, close to my being,

Guiding me with His light, when I walk along;

It's Jesus Christ my Lord, to whom my voice,

Forever I will sing a hymn of love.

CHORUS

He's my King, who always reigns in me,

He's my King, to whom I give all praise;

My beloved Jesus, whom does live forever,

There's no one like Him. He's my King,

I'll tell it to the world,

He's my King, proclaim it high and well;

When I am in heaven,

I will tell my story, of how Christ saved me.

2

The Savior of this world is close to me,

Hearing from His voice, phrases of love;

I want to see the Lord, just as He is,

So power, peace, and love will shine in me.

3

I have God's Spirit deep inside of me,

It gives me the strength, valor, and control;

He fills my heart with strength and His fervor,

So with my faith I will sing to my Lord.
