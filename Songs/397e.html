397 OH CHRIST YOU GAVE

Oh Christ you gve forgiveness to me,

You perfumed all my soul with your love;

From the Valley of Sharon's perfume,

It is overflowing from my heart.

CHORUS

You are my eternal God,

My strong Redeemer, lofty King;

What divine love, my faithful friend,

My inspiration, my Savior too.

2

For much time I resisted your voice,

I turned off my soul's strongest desire;

But I saw that I was close to death,

With such pain I confessed "I have sinned".

3

I give worship to you oh my Lord,

I will follow you armed with my love;

An advantage I have at your side,

I would someday like to live with you.

4

I am now in a sweet scented place,

Where the lily's expelling its scent;

There's also a rose in the valley,

It's sweetens my life with the Lord's love.
