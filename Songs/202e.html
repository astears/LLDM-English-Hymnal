202 AN APOSTLE GOD HAS SENT

An Apostle God has sent from the heavens,

Let us not murmur, against the friend of our Lord;

For the Church of the Lord has descended again,

With Her light brighter more than the moon's;

The past maidens are dazzled in awe,

She's the wife, the elected of God.

CHORUS

Rejoice, oh blessed Church, beloved of the Lord,

All the false-hearted men quiver with fear;

For they destroyed the just path of the Lord,

The voice of the good Sheppard,

His church has come to know;

His sheep ignore the calling,

Of any stranger, who may lure them with their voice.

2

I am God who commanded destruction,

Against these men: Korah, Dathan, and Abiram;

For they thought their perception much wiser than Mine,

And rejected the man, I chose then;

They were once chosen people of God,

But erased were their names from God's book.

3

Be not severed from the path of the Lord,

By being spiteful, and not releasing your bitterness;

Your within Israel, a great people of peace,

Israel whom I've loved since its birth;

Never question My words and decrees,

They form pathways that lead up to Me.
