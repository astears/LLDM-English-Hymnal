091 WITH THE MOST PRECIOUS BLOOD

With the most precious blood that has been shed on the cross,

Jesus Christ marked for us, the most glorious pathway;

I am now walking through it, I know that I won't fall,

For my Christ so divinely now sustains me with His might;

With His mighty great strength.

CHORUS

Yes with anguish and pain, I am now walking,

I'm waiting for my Christ, He guards me in His love;

He has marked an example; and has ciphered my longing,

I am longing for heaven;

Which is where Jesus is.

2

Jesus Christ I will follow, Jesus Christ is my light,

He is all of my gladness; He is all of my health;

At His side I would like to, live in peace forever,

For all that I'm expecting is for Him to take my soul:

When I cease to exist.

3

Like the sweet scents of fragrance, that the flowers expel,

As the divine aroma scattered on His altar;

So are the words of wisdom to His children of light,

Those who have found a refuge, joyfully they have found it;

A peace from Jesus Christ.

4

I am walking through this life, only walking through faith,

Holding on to my hope of one day reaching my Lord;

And with a loving voice from His lips someday I'll hear,

With such beautiful words, like the perfume of the roses;

"All loyal come to me."
