059 OH BLESSED PEACE

Oh blessed peace, that was heard from above,

Voice of the Lord descending with great love;

A hidden mystery that men had never known of,

For this I was lost, walking in my ignorance.

CHORUS

To you, oh blessed man, was the holy election,

To you, the divine knowledge, the Lord has revealed;

Now with joy freely we praise God united,

We give glory to He who freed us all.

Now your children proclaim Your glory,

Confirming your an Apostle of the Lord.

2

By God's grace, you have been anointed,

And in your mind engendered was a light;

Light that now shines on the path of all the lost ones,

This through the doctrine that God revealed to you.

3

The shadow fled at the breaking of dawn,

That happy day when God called you to us;

Great man, Apostle, much greater than your brethren,

God's promise to those He has chosen for this work.
