592 I CAN SEE UP IN THE HEAVENS

I can see up in the heavens,

All the dawning in its brilliance;

Like a light giving reflections,

Christ, the star alive and shinning; Jesus has illuminated,

The path of the sinful man.

CHORUS

You gave salvation unto my soul,

You were my protection and my refuge;

All of my sadness has dissipated,

Now I live in Christ;

Anguish has passed on, and pain has ceased.

2

Darkness is now disappearing,

And the light has come to shine on me;

Christ is guiding me by His side,

He is showing me His path;

I'm a pilgrim on this earth,

Towards His mansion I move on.

3

There is no doubt in my soul now,

Christ gave me His confirmation;

I am a witness of His grace,

To the people I will say;

There is only life in Jesus,

And forgiveness is through Him.
