543 LET US RAISE UP

Let us raise up tender songs to the Lord,

Hymns that build faith in our souls and our hearts;

May they speak highly of His fervent Love,

This only lives in the man who believes.

Let them come fervently bringing their song,

Children and elderly to the altar;

Bring unto God, come bring your hearts,

The only gift that He will accept.

CHORUS

//Heavens and earth sing unto the Lord of all the nations//

And all men together with their hearts so full of gladness;

Let them serve the Lord, for only He gives life and peace.

2

He is the fountain of kindness and love,

He's life, He's light, and He' is our warmth;

He only frees us from anxiety,

He only takes pain away from my soul.

Therefore He's worthy for man to give Him,

Glory and honor that's heard everywhere;

Let's go to him, filled with our faith,

With His great power He will save us.
