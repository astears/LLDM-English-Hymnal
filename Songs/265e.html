265 HOSANNA

///Hosanna!///

The earth and heaven are the Lord's;

All glory and all power,

And we're surrounded by His love.

His holy divinity.

Raise up therefore your hymns of praise,

This pleases the high God; All honor we render to God.

Now and forever; Amen,

To God let us render ///all honor///

To God let us render all honor,

Now and forever; Amen.
